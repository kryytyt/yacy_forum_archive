NASA to Discuss Mars Curiosity Radiation Findings
=================================================

Date: 2013-05-28 21:05:00

[![This artist\'s concept features NASA\'s Mars Science Laboratory
Curiosity
rover](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20120607/pia15791-th.jpg){width="100"

findings from the Mars Science Laboratory Radiation Assessment
Detector(RAD) aboard the rover Curiosity.
