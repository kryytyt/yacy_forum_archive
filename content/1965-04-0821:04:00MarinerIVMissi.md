Mariner IV Mission Milestones
=============================

Date: 1965-04-08 21:04:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6070)\
\
The Mariner IV spacecraft, during the past week, reached a number of
milestones along its 228-day scientific mission to the planet Mars, the
National Aeronautics and Space Administration reported today.
