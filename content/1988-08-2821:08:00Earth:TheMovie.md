Earth: The Movie
================

Date: 1988-08-28 21:08:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5825)\
\
Image-processing scientists at the Jet Propulsion Laboratory, Pasadena,
Calif., have used digital animation techniques to produce
three-dimensional, whirlwind tour of Earth, flying the viewer over,
under and through the cloudtops in the atmosphere of our planet.
