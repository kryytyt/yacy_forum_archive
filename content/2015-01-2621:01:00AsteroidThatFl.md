Asteroid That Flew Past Earth Today Has Moon
============================================

Date: 2015-01-26 21:01:00

[![This GIF shows asteroid 2004 BL86, which safely flew past Earth on
Jan. 26,
2015.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/asteroid/20150126/2004BL86-226.gif){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-033&rn=news.xml&rst=4459)\
\
Radar Images from Goldstone indicate that asteroid 2004 BL86, which
safely flew past Earth today, has a moon.
