NASA Sets New Launch Window for Supersonic Vehicle Test
=======================================================

Date: 2015-05-26 21:05:00

[![This artist\'s concept shows the test vehicle for NASA\'s Low-Density
Supersonic
Decelerator](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/technology/ldsd/ldsd20140602-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-179&rn=news.xml&rst=4600)\
\
The second flight test of NASA\'s Low-Density Supersonic Decelerator
(LDSD) now will launch no earlier than 10:30 a.m. PDT (7:30 a.m. HST)
Tuesday, June 2, from the U.S. Navy\'s Pacific Missile Range Facility
(PMRF) on Kauai, Hawaii.
