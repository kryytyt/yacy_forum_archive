NASA Pinpoints Cause of Earth\'s Recent Record Carbon Dioxide Spike
===================================================================

Date: 2017-10-12 21:10:00

[![Diagram of last El
Nino](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/oco/20171012a/earth20171012-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-267&rn=news.xml&rst=6973)\
\
A new NASA study provides space-based evidence Earth\'s tropical regions
were the cause of the largest annual increases in atmospheric carbon
dioxide in at least 2,000 years.
