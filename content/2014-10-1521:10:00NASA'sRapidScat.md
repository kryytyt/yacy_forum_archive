NASA\'s RapidScat Keeps a Watchful Eye on Ocean Storms
======================================================

Date: 2014-10-15 21:10:00

[![NASA astronaut Reid Wiseman captured this view of Typhoon Vongfong
from space while he orbited the
Earth](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/hurricane/20141015/earth20141015-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-354&rn=news.xml&rst=4337)\
\
NASA\'s RapidScat gives forecasters a new eye on winds far out to sea
and will help researchers understand why a breeze over the ocean
sometimes builds into a hurricane.
