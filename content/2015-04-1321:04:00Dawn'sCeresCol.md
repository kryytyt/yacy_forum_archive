Dawn\'s Ceres Color Map Reveals Surface Diversity
=================================================

Date: 2015-04-13 21:04:00

[![Dawn\'s First Color Map of
Ceres](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20150413/PIA19063-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-125&rn=news.xml&rst=4548)\
\
A new color map of dwarf planet Ceres, which NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft has
been orbiting since March, reveals the diversity of the surface of this
planetary body.
