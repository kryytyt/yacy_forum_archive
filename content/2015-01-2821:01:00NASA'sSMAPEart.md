NASA\'s SMAP Earth Mission Awaits Launch
========================================

Date: 2015-01-28 21:01:00

[![NASA\'s Soil Moisture Active Passive (SMAP) Ready for Jan. 29
Launch](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/smap/20150128/smap20150128-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-039&rn=news.xml&rst=4465)\
\
NASA\'s Soil Moisture Active Passive (SMAP) mission is set to launch
Thursday, Jan. 29, at 6:20 a.m. PST (9:20 a.m. EST) from Vandenberg Air
Force Base in California.
