Opportunity Working in No-Flash Mode for Now
============================================

Date: 2014-12-11 21:12:00

[![NASA\'s Mars Exploration Rover
Opportunity](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mer/2014-12-11/pia19082-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-425&rn=news.xml&rst=4406)\
\
Persistent computer resets and \"amnesia\" events that have occurred
after reformatting the flash memory on the Opportunity rover have
prompted a shift to a working mode that avoids the flash data-storage
system.
