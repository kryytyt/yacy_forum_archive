NASA Reformats Memory of Longest-Running Mars Rover
===================================================

Date: 2015-03-23 20:03:00

[![Mars \'Marathon Valley\'
Overlook](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mer/2015-03-23/pia19151-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-094&rn=news.xml&rst=4517)\
\
Operators of NASA\'s Opportunity Mars rover have reformatted the
rover\'s onboard flash memory so the team can resume storing data
overnight on the rover.
