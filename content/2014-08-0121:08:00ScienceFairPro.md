Science Fair Project Spins Up NASA Hurricane Study
==================================================

Date: 2014-08-01 21:08:00

[![Alice Zhai and Jonathan Jiang. Image credit:
NASA](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/hurricane/20140801/earth20140801-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-259&rn=news.xml&rst=4242)\
\
With the help of a JPL researcher, high school intern Alice Zhai has
turned her science fair project into the first look at the economic
impacts of hurricane size.
