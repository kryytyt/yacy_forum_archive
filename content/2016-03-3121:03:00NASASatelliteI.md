NASA Satellite Images Uncover Underground Forest Fungi
======================================================

Date: 2016-03-31 21:03:00

[![Nearly all forest trees live in symbiosis with underground
fungi.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/forest/20160331/earth20160331-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-091&rn=news.xml&rst=6241)\
\
A new way to detect underground forest fungi from space could help
researchers predict how climate change will alter forest habitats.
