NuSTAR Principal Investigator Receives Astrophysics Prize
=========================================================

Date: 2015-01-16 21:01:00

[![Fiona Harrison, the principal investigator of
NuSTAR](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/nustar/20150116/fiona20150116-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-021&rn=news.xml&rst=4447)\
\
The 2015 Rossi Prize has been awarded to Fiona Harrison, the principal
investigator of NASA\'s Nuclear Spectroscopic Telescope Array, or
NuSTAR.
