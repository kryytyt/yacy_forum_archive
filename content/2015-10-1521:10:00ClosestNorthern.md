Closest Northern Views of Saturn\'s Moon Enceladus
==================================================

Date: 2015-10-15 21:10:00

[![A Fractured
Pole](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20151015/PIA19660-226.jpg){width="100"

NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft has begun returning its best-ever views of
the northern extremes of Saturn\'s icy, ocean-bearing moon Enceladus.
