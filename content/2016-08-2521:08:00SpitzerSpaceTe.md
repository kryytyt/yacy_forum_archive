Spitzer Space Telescope Begins \'Beyond\' Phase
===============================================

Date: 2016-08-25 21:08:00

[![This artist\'s concept shows NASA\'s Spitzer Space Telescope in its
\'Beyond\' mission
phase.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/spitzer/20160825/spitzer20160825-226.jpg){width="100"

science and technology, NASA\'s Spitzer Space Telescope team has dubbed
the next phase of its journey \"Beyond.\"
