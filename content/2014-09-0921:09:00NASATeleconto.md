NASA Telecon to Discuss Mars Curiosity Science Plans
====================================================

Date: 2014-09-09 21:09:00

[![Mars Rover Curiosity in Artist\'s Concept,
Close-up](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20131112/pia14175-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-305&rn=news.xml&rst=4289)\
\
NASA will host a telecon at 10 a.m. PDT (1 p.m. EDT) Thursday, Sept. 11,
to discuss mission status and the future science campaign for the Mars
rover Curiosity mission.
