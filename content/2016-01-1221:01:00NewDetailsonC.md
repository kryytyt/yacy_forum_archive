New Details on Ceres Seen in Dawn Images
========================================

Date: 2016-01-12 21:01:00

[![Kupalo Crater from
LAMO](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20160111/PIA20192-226.jpg){width="100"

Intriguing features on dwarf planet Ceres stand out in exquisite detail
in new images from NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft, which recently reached its
lowest-ever altitude at Ceres.
