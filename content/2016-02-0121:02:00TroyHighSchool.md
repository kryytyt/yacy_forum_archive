Troy High School Takes First Place at Regional Science Bowl
===========================================================

Date: 2016-02-01 21:02:00

[![A team from Troy High School from Fullerton,
California.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/events/20160201/sciencebowl20160201-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-031&rn=news.xml&rst=4855)\
\
Troy High School triumphed over 23 other teams at the National Science
Bowl regional competition held at JPL on Jan. 30.
