NASA\'s Wind-Watching ISS-RapidScat Ready for Launch
====================================================

Date: 2014-09-12 21:09:00

[![Artist\'s rendering of NASA\'s ISS-RapidScat instrument
(inset)](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/winds/rapidScat-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-309&rn=news.xml&rst=4293)\
\
The fourth SpaceX cargo mission to the International Space Station,
carrying the JPL-built ISS-RapidScat instrument, is scheduled to launch
Sept. 20.
