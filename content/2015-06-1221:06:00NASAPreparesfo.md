NASA Prepares for First Interplanetary CubeSats
===============================================

Date: 2015-06-12 21:06:00

[![This illustration depicts a moment during the lander\'s
descent](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/marco/20150612/pia19388-226.jpg){width="100"

When NASA launches its next mission on the journey to Mars - a
stationary lander in 2016 - the flight will include the first two
CubeSats sent to deep space.
