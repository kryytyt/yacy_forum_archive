Larry N. Dumas Named Assistant Laboratory Director of Telecommunications and Data Acquisition Office
====================================================================================================

Date: 1987-07-20 21:07:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"

\
Larry N. Dumas has been named assistant laboratory director for the Jet
Propulsion Laboratory\'s Office of Telecommunications and Data
Acquisition, JPL Director Dr. Lew Allen announced.
