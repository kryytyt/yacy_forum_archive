Mariner IV Charts Vast Expanse Between Earth and Mars
=====================================================

Date: 1965-06-20 20:06:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6072)\
\
For 204 days the Mariner IV spacecraft has been charting the vast
expanse between the planets Earth and Mars on the longest deep space
mission in history.
