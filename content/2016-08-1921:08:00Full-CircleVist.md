Full-Circle Vista from NASA Mars Rover Curiosity Shows \'Murray Buttes\'
========================================================================

Date: 2016-08-19 21:08:00

[![360-degree vista was acquired on Aug. 5, 2016, by the Mastcam on

the horizon in the latest 360-degree color panorama from NASA\'s
