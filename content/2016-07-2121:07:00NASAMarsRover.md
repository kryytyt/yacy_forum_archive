NASA Mars Rover Can Choose Laser Targets on Its Own
===================================================

Date: 2016-07-21 21:07:00

[![Autonomous Selection of a Rover\'s Laser Target on
Mars](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20160721/pia20762-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-193&rn=news.xml&rst=6575)\
\
NASA\'s Mars rover Curiosity is using onboard software to select rocks
to zap with its laser spectrometer and to autonomously point the
instrument at the targets.
