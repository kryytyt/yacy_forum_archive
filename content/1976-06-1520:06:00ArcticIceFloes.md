Arctic Ice Floes Mapping Feasible
=================================

Date: 1976-06-15 20:06:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6046)\
\
Accurate continuous mapping of Arctic ice floes by airborne imaging
radar has been proven feasible by team of Jet Propulsion Laboratory
scientists, working jointing on National Aeronautics and Space
Administration program with colleagues from the United States and
Canada.
