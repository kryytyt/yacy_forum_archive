NASA-funded Website Lets the Public Search for New Nearby Worlds
================================================================

Date: 2017-02-15 21:02:00

[![Artist\'s concept illustrates a close-up view of a cool brown

NASA is inviting the public to help search for possible undiscovered
worlds in the outer reaches of our solar system and in neighboring
interstellar space.
