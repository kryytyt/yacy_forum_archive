SHERLOC to Micro-Map Mars Minerals and Carbon Rings
===================================================

Date: 2014-07-31 21:07:00

[![Artist\'s concept of the payload for NASA\'s Mars 2020
rover](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/2020/20140731/SHERLOC-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-254&rn=news.xml&rst=4238)\
\
One of the seven instruments chosen for the payload of NASA\'s next Mars
rover would detect key minerals and carbon chemicals and show where they
are, at microscopic scale.
