Das oberste Gericht Australiens hat Patent auf Gene \...
========================================================

Date: 2015-10-08 19:41:03

[Das oberste Gericht Australiens hat Patent auf Gene für ungültig
erklärt](http://arstechnica.co.uk/tech-policy/2015/10/gene-patents-probably-dead-worldwide-following-australian-court-decision/).

> Since the information stored in the DNA as a sequence of nucleotides
> was a product of nature, it did not require human action to bring it
> into existence, and therefore could not be patented.
