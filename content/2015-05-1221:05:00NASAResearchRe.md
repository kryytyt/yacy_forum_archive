NASA Research Reveals Europa\'s Mystery Dark Material Could Be Sea Salt
=======================================================================

Date: 2015-05-12 21:05:00

[![Remastered View of
Europa](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/jupiter/europa/20141121/pia19048-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-166&rn=news.xml&rst=4586)\
\
NASA laboratory experiments suggest the dark material coating some
geological features of Jupiter\'s moon Europa is likely sea salt from a
subsurface ocean, discolored by exposure to radiation.
