NASA Maps Beach Tar from California Oil Pipeline Spill
======================================================

Date: 2015-07-01 21:07:00

[![AVIRIS-NG red green blue imagery of the Refugio Incident oil
spill](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/california/20150701/aviris20150630-226.jpg){width="100"

When an oil spill sullied beaches near Santa Barbara, California, in
May, a JPL airborne instrument tested new techniques that may help
responders after future oil spills.
