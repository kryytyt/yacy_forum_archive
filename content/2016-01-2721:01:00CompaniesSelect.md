Companies Selected to Provide Early Design Work for Asteroid Redirect Robotic Mission Spacecraft
================================================================================================

Date: 2016-01-27 21:01:00

[![Asteroid Redirect Robotic Mission
(ARRM).](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/asteroid/20160127/asteroid20160127-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-026&rn=news.xml&rst=4833)\
\
NASA\'s Jet Propulsion Laboratory in Pasadena, Calif., has selected four
companies to conduct design studies for a
solar-electric-propulsion-based spacecraft for the agency\'s Asteroid
Redirect Robotic Mission (ARRM).
