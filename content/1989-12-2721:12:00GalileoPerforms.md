Galileo Performs Trajectory Maneuver
====================================

Date: 1989-12-27 21:12:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5888)\
\
NASA\'s Galileo spacecraft, on its way to Jupiter via gravity-assist
encounters with Venus and the Earth, performed its second trajectory
correction maneuver Friday, Dec. 22.
