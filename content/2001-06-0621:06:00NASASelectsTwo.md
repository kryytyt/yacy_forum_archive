NASA Selects Two Investigators for Pluto-Kuiper Belt Mission Feasibility Studies
================================================================================

Date: 2001-06-06 21:06:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion
Laboratory](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"

In the first step of a potential two-step process, JPL is included in
two proposals selected by NASA for detailed mission feasibility studies
as candidates for a Pluto-Kuiper Belt (PKB) mission
