1987 NASA Honor Awards
======================

Date: 1988-03-22 21:03:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5814)\
\
Achievements ranging from headline-making astronomical discovery to work
on computers that mimic the human brain have earned 1987 NASA Honor
Awards for 24 men and women at the Jet Propulsion Laboratory.
