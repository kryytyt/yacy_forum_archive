NASA Links Port-City Sea Levels to Regional Ice Melt
====================================================

Date: 2017-11-20 21:11:00

[![The contribution of melting ice in
Greenland](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20171120/greenland-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-301&rn=news.xml&rst=7007)\
\
A new NASA tool that links changes in sea level in 293 global port
cities to specific regions of melting land ice may help coastal planners
prepare for future rising seas.
