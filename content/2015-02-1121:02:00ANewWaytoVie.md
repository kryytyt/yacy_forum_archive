A New Way to View Titan: \'Despeckle\' It
=========================================

Date: 2015-02-11 21:02:00

[![Despeckling Ligea
Mare](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20150211/pia19052-226.jpg){width="100"

technique that greatly improves radar images taken by NASA\'s Cassini
spacecraft.
