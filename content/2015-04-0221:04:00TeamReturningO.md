Team Returning Orbiter to Duty After Computer Swap
==================================================

Date: 2015-04-02 21:04:00

[![Artist concept of Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter. Image credit:
NASA/JPL](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20081126/mro-concept-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-114&rn=news.xml&rst=4537)\
\
NASA\'s Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter, at Mars since 2006, made an
unplanned switch on Wednesday from one main computer to a redundant one
onboard, triggering a hiatus in planned activities.
