Deepest-Ever Dive Through Enceladus Plume Completed
===================================================

Date: 2015-10-28 20:10:00

[![Cassini spacecraft completed its deepest-ever dive through the icy
plume of
Enceladus](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20151028/enceladus-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-333&rn=news.xml&rst=4755)\
\
NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft successfully completed its close flyby of
Saturn\'s moon Enceladus today, passing 30 miles (49 kilometers) above
the moon\'s south polar region.
