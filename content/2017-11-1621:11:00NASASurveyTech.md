NASA Survey Technique Estimates Congo Forest\'s Carbon
======================================================

Date: 2017-11-16 21:11:00

[![Sabinyo volcano and thick forest in Virunga National
Park](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20171116/virunga-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-297&rn=news.xml&rst=7002)\
\
The equivalent of up to three-quarters of the carbon stored in
contiguous U.S. forests is locked in the living vegetation of one
African country, according to new research.
