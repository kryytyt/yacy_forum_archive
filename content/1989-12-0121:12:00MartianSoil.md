Martian Soil
============

Date: 1989-12-01 21:12:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5881)\
\
While Martian soil contains no organic matter, conditions beneath the
surface may once have been more favorable to the existence of life on
the Red Planet, said three NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory scientists.
