NASA\'s Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter Studies Comet Flyby
=======================================================

Date: 2014-10-19 21:10:00

[![Illustration of the Mars Reconnaissance
Orbiter](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/20141019/mro-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-361&rn=news.xml&rst=4344)\
\
NASA\'s Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter, which has sent home more data about
Mars than all other missions combined, is also now providing data about
a comet that buzzed The Red Planet on October 19.
