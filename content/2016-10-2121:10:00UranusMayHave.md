Uranus May Have Two Undiscovered Moons
======================================

Date: 2016-10-21 21:10:00

[![Uranus is seen in this false-color view from NASA\'s Hubble Space
Telescope from August
2003.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/voyager/20161021/UranusHST20161021-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-276&rn=news.xml&rst=6657)\
\
A new study suggests Uranus has two tiny, previously undiscovered
moonlets orbiting near two of the planet\'s rings.
