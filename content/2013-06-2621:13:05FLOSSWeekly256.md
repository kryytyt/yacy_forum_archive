Hosts: [Randal Schwartz](http://www.stonehenge.com/merlyn/) and [Aaron
Newcomb](http://thesourceshow.org).

Serenity for Android is a Plex Media Server client for tablets and
Google TV devices. It is optimized for the 10ft viewing experience, and
remote navigation. While it was designed for the television, it works
just as well on a tablet. Serenity provides a very visual interface to
accessing video and tv show categories on the server.
