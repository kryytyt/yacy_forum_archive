Hubble: Possible Water Plumes on Jupiter\'s Moon Europa
=======================================================

Date: 2016-09-26 21:09:00

[![This composite image shows suspected plumes of water vapor erupting
at the 7 o\'clock position off the limb of Jupiter\'s moon
Europa.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20160926/europa02-photoa-plumes1042x1042-160919-226.jpg){width="100"

\
Astronomers using NASA\'s Hubble Space Telescope have imaged what may be
water vapor plumes erupting off the surface of Jupiter\'s moon Europa.
