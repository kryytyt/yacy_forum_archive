Galileo Scientific Result Release
=================================

Date: 1989-12-01 21:12:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5886)\
\
The first scientific results from the Jupiter-bound Galileo spacecraft
were released today by scientists attending the 1989 Fall Meeting of the
American Geophysical Union in San Francisco.
