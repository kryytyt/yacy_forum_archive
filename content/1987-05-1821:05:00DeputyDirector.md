Deputy Director Robert J. Parks Retires
=======================================

Date: 1987-05-18 21:05:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5769)\
\
Robert J. Parks, deputy director of the Jet Propulsion Laboratory, will
retire June 30, 1987, JPL Director Lew Allen has announced.
