Gerald Levy Awarded Charles Stark Draper Award
==============================================

Date: 1988-06-21 21:06:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5822)\
\
Gerald S. Levy of NASA\'s Jet Propulsion Laboratory has been named
recipient of the Charles Stark Draper Award by the International
Aeronautical Federation for his innovative work in 3-year-long
astronomical experiment.
