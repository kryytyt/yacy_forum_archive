NASA\'s NuSTAR Captures Possible \'Screams\' from Zombie Stars
==============================================================

Date: 2015-04-29 21:04:00

[![Extra X-rays at the Hub of Our Milky Way
Galaxy](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/nustar/20150429/pia19334-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-147&rn=news.xml&rst=4569)\
\
NASA\'s NuSTAR has spotted a mysterious glow of high-energy X-rays in
the heart of the Milky way that could be the \"howls\" of dead stars as
they feed on stellar companions.
