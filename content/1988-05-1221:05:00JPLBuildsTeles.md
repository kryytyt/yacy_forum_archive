JPL Builds Telescope Building
=============================

Date: 1988-05-12 21:05:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5818)\
\
NASA\'s Jet Propulsion Laboratory has begun construction of domed
building at its Table Mountain site to house 48-inch optical telescope
acquired from the U.S. Air Force.
