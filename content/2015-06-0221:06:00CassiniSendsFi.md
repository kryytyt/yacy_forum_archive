Cassini Sends Final Close Views of Odd Moon Hyperion
====================================================

Date: 2015-06-02 21:06:00

[![Spongy
Surface](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20150602/pia17194-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-188&rn=news.xml&rst=4608)\
\
NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft has returned images from its final close
approach to Saturn\'s oddball moon Hyperion, upholding the moon\'s
reputation as one of the most bizarre objects in the solar system.
