Cassini Sees Dramatic Seasonal Changes on Titan
===============================================

Date: 2016-10-20 21:10:00

[![Dusk in the
South](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20161020/PIA17177-16.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-274&rn=news.xml&rst=6655)\
\
As winter comes to the southern parts of Saturn\'s moon Titan, NASA\'s
Cassini spacecraft has been following dramatic seasonal changes in the
temperature and composition of the atmosphere there.
