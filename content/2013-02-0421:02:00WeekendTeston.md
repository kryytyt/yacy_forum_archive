Weekend Test on Mars Was Preparation to Drill a Rock
====================================================

Date: 2013-02-04 21:02:00

[![Preparatory Test for First Rock Drilling by Mars Rover
Curiosity](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20130204/pia16717-th.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.cfm?release=2013-044&rn=news.xml&rst=3678)\
\
NASA\'s Mars rover Curiosity used the hammering action of its drill on
Feb. 2 as the second-to-last major test before the first full drilling
to collect a sample of rock dust.
