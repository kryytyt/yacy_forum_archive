NASA Selects Two Missions to Explore the Early Solar System
===========================================================

Date: 2017-01-04 21:01:00

[![Artist\'s concept of the Psyche
spacecraft](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/asteroid/psyche/PsycheAsteroid20170104c-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-001&rn=news.xml&rst=6713)\
\
NASA has selected two missions that have the potential to open new
windows on one of the earliest eras in the history of our solar system -
a time less than 10 million years after the birth of our sun.
