NASA Instrument on Rosetta: First Science Results
=================================================

Date: 2014-09-04 21:09:00

[![Rosetta at
Comet](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/rosetta/20140124/pia17666-226.jpg){width="100"

A NASA instrument aboard the European Space Agency\'s (ESA\'s) Rosetta
orbiter has successfully made its first delivery of science data from
comet 67P/Churyumov-Gerasimenko.
