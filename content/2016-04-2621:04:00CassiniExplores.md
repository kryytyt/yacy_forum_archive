Cassini Explores a Methane Sea on Titan
=======================================

Date: 2016-04-26 21:04:00

[![Sunlight glints off of Titan\'s northern seas this
near-infrared](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20160426/PIA18432-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-114&rn=news.xml&rst=6440)\
\
A new study finds that a large sea on Saturn\'s moon Titan is composed
mostly of pure liquid methane, independently confirming an earlier
result.
