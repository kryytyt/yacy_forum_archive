Die Saudis sehen sich mal wieder genötigt, den Amis \...
========================================================

Date: 2016-04-17 22:11:35

[Die Saudis sehen sich mal wieder genötigt, den Amis zu zeigen, wer hier
in der Beziehung die Hosen
anhat](http://www.nytimes.com/2016/04/16/world/middleeast/saudi-arabia-warns-ofeconomic-fallout-if-congress-passes-9-11-bill.html):

> Saudi Arabia has told the Obama administration and members of Congress
> that it will sell off hundreds of billions of dollars' worth of
> American assets held by the kingdom if Congress passes a bill that
> would allow the Saudi government to be held responsible in American
> courts for any role in the Sept. 11, 2001, attacks.
