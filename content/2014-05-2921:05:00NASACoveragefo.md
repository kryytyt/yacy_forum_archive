NASA Coverage for Saucer-Shaped Test Vehicle Flight
===================================================

Date: 2014-05-29 21:05:00

[![A saucer-shaped test vehicle holding equipment for landing large

NASA\'s Low-Density Supersonic Decelerator (LDSD) project will test a
rocket-powered, saucer-shaped vehicle from the U.S. Navy\'s Pacific
Missile Range Facility in Hawaii.
