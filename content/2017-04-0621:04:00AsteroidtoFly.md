Asteroid to Fly Safely Past Earth on April 19
=============================================

Date: 2017-04-06 21:04:00

[![Asteroid 2014
JO25](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/asteroid/20170406/asteroid20170406-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-100&rn=news.xml&rst=6807)\
\
A fairly large asteroid discovered nearly three years ago will safely
pass about 1.1 million miles from Earth on April 19. That\'s about 4.6
times the Earth-moon distance.
