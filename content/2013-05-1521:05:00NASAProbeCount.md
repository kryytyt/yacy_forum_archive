NASA Probe Counts Space Rock Impacts on Mars
============================================

Date: 2013-05-15 21:05:00

[![Fresh Cluster of Impact Craters on
Mars](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20130515/pia16928-th.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.cfm?release=2013-162&rn=news.xml&rst=3792)\
\
Scientists using images from NASA\'s Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter have
estimated that the planet is bombarded by more than 200 small asteroids
or bits of comets per year.
