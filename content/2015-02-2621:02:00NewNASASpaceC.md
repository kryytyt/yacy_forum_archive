New NASA Space Cowboy Successfully Deploys Its \'Lasso\'
========================================================

Date: 2015-02-26 21:02:00

planet](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/smap/20150224/smap20150224-16.jpg){width="100"

Soil Moisture Active Passive (SMAP), has triumphantly raised its \"arm\"
and unfurled a huge golden \"lasso\" (antenna).
