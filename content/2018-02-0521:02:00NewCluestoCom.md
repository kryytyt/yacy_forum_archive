New Clues to Compositions of TRAPPIST-1 Planets
===============================================

Date: 2018-02-05 21:02:00

[![This artist\'s concept shows what the TRAPPIST-1 planetary system may
look
like](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/exoplanet/20180205/PIA22093-226.jpg){width="100"

\
The seven Earth-size planets of TRAPPIST-1 are all mostly made of rock,
with some having the potential to hold more water than Earth, according
to a new study.
