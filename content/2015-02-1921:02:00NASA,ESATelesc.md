NASA, ESA Telescopes Give Shape to Furious Black Hole Winds
===========================================================

Date: 2015-02-19 21:02:00

[![How Black Hole Winds
Blow](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/nustar/20150219/pia18919-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-062&rn=news.xml&rst=4486)\
\
NASA\'s Nuclear Spectroscopic Telescope Array (NuSTAR) and ESA\'s
(European Space Agency) XMM-Newton telescope are showing that fierce
winds from a supermassive black hole blow outward in all directions \--
a phenomenon that had been suspected, but difficult to prove until now.
