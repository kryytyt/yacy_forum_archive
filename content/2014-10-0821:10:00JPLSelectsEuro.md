JPL Selects Europa CubeSat Proposals for Study
==============================================

Date: 2014-10-08 21:10:00

[![Artist\'s concept of CubeSats near Jupiter\'s moon
Europa](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cubesat/20141008/cubesat20141008-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-347&rn=news.xml&rst=4330)\
\
NASA\'s Jet Propulsion Laboratory has chosen proposals from 10
universities for CubeSats that could potentially enhance a Europa
mission concept currently under study by NASA.
