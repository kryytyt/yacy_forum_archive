Elachi Named New JPL Director
=============================

Date: 2001-03-17 21:03:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion
Laboratory](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6159)\
\
Dr. Charles Elachi has been named the new director of the Jet Propulsion
Laboratory, Dr. David Baltimore, president of the California Institute
of Technology, announced at a press conference today. Caltech manages
JPL for NASA.
