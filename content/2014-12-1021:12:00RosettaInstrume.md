Rosetta Instrument Reignites Debate on Earth\'s Oceans
======================================================

Date: 2014-12-10 21:12:00

[![Rosetta
Comet](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/rosetta/20141210/pia18899-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-423&rn=news.xml&rst=4404)\
\
A Rosetta spacecraft instrument has found that the composition of comet
67P/Churyumov-Gerasimenko\'s water vapor is significantly different from
that found on Earth.
