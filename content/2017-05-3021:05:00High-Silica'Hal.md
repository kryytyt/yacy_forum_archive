High-Silica \'Halos\' Shed Light on Wet Ancient Mars
====================================================

Date: 2017-05-30 21:05:00

[![Discolored Fracture Zones in Martian
Sandstone](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20170530/pia21649-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-154&rn=news.xml&rst=6859)\
\
Pale \"halos\" around fractures in bedrock analyzed by NASA\'s Curiosity
Mars rover contain copious silica, indicating that ancient Mars had
liquid water for a long time.
