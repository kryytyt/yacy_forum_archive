JPL Presents New Information on Crustal Deformation
===================================================

Date: 1989-12-01 21:12:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5884)\
\
New information on the rate of crustal deformation along earthquake
faults in central and southern California was presented Thursday by Jet
Propulsion Laboratory scientist before science conference in San
Francisco.
