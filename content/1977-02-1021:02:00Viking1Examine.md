Viking 1 Examines Martian Soil
==============================

Date: 1977-02-10 21:02:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6049)\
\
Operations have begun at the Viking landing site to dig foot-deep trench
in the Martian soil and distribute samples to three instruments.
