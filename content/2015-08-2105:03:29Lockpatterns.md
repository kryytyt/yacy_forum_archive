Lock patterns
=============

Date: 2015-08-21 05:03:29

[![](http://www.jwz.org/images/weak-android-lock-patterns-640x380.png)Surprise,
your Android lock pattern is just as bad as the combo on your
luggage:](http://arstechnica.com/security/2015/08/new-data-uncovers-the-surprising-predictability-of-android-lock-patterns/)

> More than 10 percent of the ones she collected were fashioned after an
> alphabetic letter, which often corresponded to the first initial of
> the subject or of a spouse, child, or other person close to the
> subject. The discovery is significant, because it means attackers may
> have a one-in-ten chance of guessing an ALP with no more than about
> 100 guesses. The number of guesses could be reduced further if the
> attacker knows the names of the target or of people close to the
> target.
