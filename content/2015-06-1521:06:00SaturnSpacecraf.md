Saturn Spacecraft to Buzz Icy Moon Dione June 16
================================================

Date: 2015-06-15 21:06:00

[![Cassini\'s penultimate encounter with Saturn\'s moon Dione is slated
for June
16.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20150615/cassini20150615-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-202&rn=news.xml&rst=4623)\
\
NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft will make a close flyby of Saturn\'s moon
Dione on June 16, coming within 321 miles (516 kilometers) of the
moon\'s surface.
