Cassini Finds Flooded Canyons on Titan
======================================

Date: 2016-08-10 21:08:00

[![NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft pinged the surface of Titan with
microwaves.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20160810/PIA20021-226.gif){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-207&rn=news.xml&rst=6589)\
\
NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft has found deep, steep-sided canyons on
Saturn\'s moon Titan that are flooded with liquid hydrocarbons.
