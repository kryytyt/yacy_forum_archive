NASA\'s Curiosity Mars Rover Finds Mineral Match
================================================

Date: 2014-11-04 21:11:00

[![\'Confidence Hills\' \-- The First Mount Sharp Drilling
Site](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20141104/pia19036-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-380&rn=news.xml&rst=4361)\
\
Reddish rock powder from the first hole drilled into a Martian mountain
by NASA\'s Curiosity rover has yielded the mission\'s first confirmation
of a mineral mapped from orbit.
