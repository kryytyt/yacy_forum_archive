NASA Finds a Large Amount of Water in an Exoplanet\'s Atmosphere
================================================================

Date: 2018-03-01 21:03:00

[![Comprehensive Spectrum of
WASP-39b](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/exoplanet/20180301/exo20180301b-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2018-041&rn=news.xml&rst=7072)\
\
Scientists used NASA\'s Hubble and Spitzer space telescopes to find
water \"fingerprints\" in the atmosphere of a hot, bloated, Saturn-mass
exoplanet some 700 light-years away.
