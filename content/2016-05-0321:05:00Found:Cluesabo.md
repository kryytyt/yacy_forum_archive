Found: Clues about Volcanoes Under Ice on Ancient Mars
======================================================

Date: 2016-05-03 21:05:00

[![Evidence Builds for Old Under-Ice Volcanoes on
Mars](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20160503/PIA20335-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-120&rn=news.xml&rst=6472)\
\
Volcanoes erupted beneath an ice sheet on Mars billions of years ago,
far from any ice sheet on the Red Planet today, new evidence from
NASA\'s Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter suggests.
