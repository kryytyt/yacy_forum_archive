New NASA Hurricane Tracking Mission on Track
============================================

Date: 2016-12-16 21:12:00

[![Artist\'s concept of one of the eight NASA Cyclone Global Navigation
Satellite System
satellites.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/hurricane/20161216/cygnss-artconcept20161216-226.jpg){width="100"

\
NASA confirmed all eight spacecraft of its new Earth science mission,
which will provide advanced technology to see inside tropical storms and
hurricanes, are in good shape.
