Icy Aquifers on Titan Transform Methane Rainfall
================================================

Date: 2014-09-03 21:09:00

[![Titan\'s Subsurface
Reservoirs](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20140903/pia18417-226.jpg){width="100"

A new study suggests that the chemistry of methane rainfall on Saturn\'s
moon Titan might change inside hydrocarbon \"aquifers.\"
