Study: Climate Change Rapidly Warming World\'s Lakes
====================================================

Date: 2015-12-16 21:12:00

[![Lake Tahoe on the California/Nevada
border](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/agu/20151216b/earth20151216-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-375&rn=news.xml&rst=4795)\
\
Climate change is rapidly warming lakes around the world, threatening
freshwater supplies and ecosystems, according to a new NASA and National
Science Foundation-funded study.
