NASA SMAP Observatory Ready for Launch
======================================

Date: 2015-01-16 21:01:00

[![Artist\'s rendering of the SMAP
instrument](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/smap/20141230/SMAP_20141230-226.jpg){width="100"

The launch of NASA\'s Soil Moisture Active Passive (SMAP) mission at
Vandenberg Air Force Base (VAFB) in California is scheduled for
Thursday, Jan. 29.
