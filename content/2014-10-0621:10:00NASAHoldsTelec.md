NASA Holds Teleconference on NuSTAR Discovery
=============================================

Date: 2014-10-06 21:10:00

[![Artist\'s concept of NuSTAR on
orbit.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/nustar/20120217/pia15265-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-339&rn=news.xml&rst=4322)\
\
NASA will host a news teleconference at 10 a.m. PDT (1 p.m. EDT)
Wednesday, Oct. 8, to announce new findings from its Nuclear
Spectroscopic Telescope Array (NuSTAR) mission.
