Mars Spacecraft Shipped to California for March Launch
======================================================

Date: 2015-12-17 21:12:00

[![Shipping InSight Mars Spacecraft to California for
Launch](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/insight/20151217/pia20278-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-378&rn=news.xml&rst=4797)\
\
NASA\'s next Mars spacecraft has arrived at Vandenberg Air Force Base,
California, for final preparations before a launch scheduled in March
2016 and a landing on Mars six months later.
