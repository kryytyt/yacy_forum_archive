How Far Away is That Galaxy? Vast Catalog Has Answers
=====================================================

Date: 2017-01-05 21:01:00

[![This graphic shows all the cosmic light sources in the sky that are
included in the NASA/IPAC Extragalactic Database

A team of researchers has compiled a special catalog to help astronomers
figure out the true distances to tens of thousands of galaxies beyond
our own Milky Way.
