JPL Open House
==============

Date: 1987-05-21 21:05:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5771)\
\
The nation\'s premier center for space exploration, the Jet Propulsion
Laboratory (JPL) in Pasadena, invites the public to visit the Lab during
two open house weekends, June 6-7 and June 13-14, 1987, from 9 a.m. to 5
p.m. each day.
