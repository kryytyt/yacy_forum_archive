Cloudy Days on Exoplanets May Hide Atmospheric Water
====================================================

Date: 2016-06-08 21:06:00

[![Hot Jupiters, exoplanets around the same size as Jupiter that orbit
very closely to their
stars.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/hubble/20160608/PIA20687-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-144&rn=news.xml&rst=6527)\
\
Scientists found that a group of hot Jupiters observed with Hubble may
have about half their atmospheres blocked by clouds or haze.
