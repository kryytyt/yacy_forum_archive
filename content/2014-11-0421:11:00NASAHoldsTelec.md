NASA Holds Telecon on Rocket Experiment Results
===============================================

Date: 2014-11-04 21:11:00

[![Spitzer Spies Spectacular
Sombrero](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/spitzer/20141104/pia07899-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-379&rn=news.xml&rst=4360)\
\
NASA will host a news teleconference at 11 a.m. PST (2 p.m. EST)
Thursday, Nov. 6, to announce discoveries from a sub-orbital rocket
experiment that are redefining what we think of as galaxies.
