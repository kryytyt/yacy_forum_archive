Black Hole Makes Material Wobble Around It
==========================================

Date: 2016-07-12 21:07:00

[![Black Hole with \'Wobbling\'
Disk](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/nustar/20160712/PIA20697-226.jpg){width="100"

The European Space Agency\'s orbiting X-ray observatory, XMM-Newton, has
proved the existence of a \"gravitational vortex\" around a black hole.
