New Tool May Assist US Regional Sea Level Planning
==================================================

Date: 2017-05-04 21:05:00

[![Correlations in U.S. coastal sea level rise between the new sea level
indicator
tool.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/ocean/20170504/sealevel20170504-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-129&rn=news.xml&rst=6834)\
\
A new tool may help planners and decision makers identify key regions of
the U.S. coastline that are vulnerable to regional sea level changes on
10- to 20-year timescales.
