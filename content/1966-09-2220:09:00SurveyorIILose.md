Surveyor II Loses Communication
===============================

Date: 1966-09-22 20:09:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6086)\
\
The Surveyor II spacecraft, which was to soft-land on the moon tonight,
lost communications with Earth tracking stations at 2:35 a.m. PDT
today\--45 hours and three minutes after it was launched from Cape
Kennedy.
