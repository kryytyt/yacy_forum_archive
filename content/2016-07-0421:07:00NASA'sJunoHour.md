NASA\'s Juno Hours From Gas Giant Jupiter
=========================================

Date: 2016-07-04 21:07:00

[![This illustration depicts NASA\'s Juno spacecraft at Jupiter, with
its solar arrays and main antenna pointed toward the distant sun and
Earth.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/juno/20160704/pia20706-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-175&rn=news.xml&rst=6556)\
\
After almost five years and 1.7 billion million miles (2.7 billion
kilometers), NASA\'s Juno mission is about to enter into orbit around
Jupiter.
