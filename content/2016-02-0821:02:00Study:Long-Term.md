Study: Long-Term Global Warming Needs External Drivers
======================================================

Date: 2016-02-08 21:02:00

[![Earth\'s atmosphere viewed from the International Space
Station.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/climate/20160208/earth20160208-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-036&rn=news.xml&rst=4936)\
\
A NASA/Duke University study provides new evidence why global
temperatures remain stable in the long run unless pushed by outside
forces, such as increased greenhouse gases.
