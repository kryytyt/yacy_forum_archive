NASA Satellite Identifies Global Ammonia \'Hotspots\'
=====================================================

Date: 2017-03-16 20:03:00

[![Global atmospheric ammonia trends measured from space from 2002 to
2016.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/airs/20170316/airs20170316-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-072&rn=news.xml&rst=6779)\
\
Study ties fertilizers, animal waste, changes to atmospheric chemistry
and climate change to increased ammonia over the United States, Europe,
China and India.
