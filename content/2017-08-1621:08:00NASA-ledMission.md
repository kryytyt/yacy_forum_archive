NASA-led Mission Studies Storm Intensification
==============================================

Date: 2017-08-16 21:08:00

[![NASA\'s Global
Hawk](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/hurricane/20170816/globalhawk-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-219&rn=news.xml&rst=6924)\
\
A group of NASA and NOAA scientists are teaming up this month on an
airborne field campaign to study severe storms in the Northern
Hemisphere and what makes them intensify.
