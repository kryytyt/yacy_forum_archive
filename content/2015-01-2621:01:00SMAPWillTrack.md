SMAP Will Track a Tiny Cog That Keeps Cycles Spinning
=====================================================

Date: 2015-01-26 21:01:00

[![Water evaporating from forest soil in the morning
sun.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/smap/20150126/smap20150126-16.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-032&rn=news.xml&rst=4458)\
\
Soil moisture, which keeps Earth\'s interlocking cycles of water, carbon
and energy turning in harmony, is the focus of NASA\'s SMAP mission,
launching Jan. 29.
