Pacific Remains Locked in Three-Year-Old Pattern
================================================

Date: 2001-06-21 21:06:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion
Laboratory](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6407)\
\
While change may be on the way, the Pacific is still dominated by the
strong, larger-than- El Nino/La Nina pattern called the Pacific Decadal
Oscillation (PDO), according to the latest data from the U.S.-French
TOPEX/Poseidon satellite mission, managed by NASA\'s Jet Propulsion
Laboratory, Pasadena, Calif.
