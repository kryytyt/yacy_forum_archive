NASA Holds Briefing to Discuss Comet Flyby of Mars Observations
===============================================================

Date: 2014-10-06 21:10:00

[![Artist\'s concept of comet Siding Spring (C/2013 A1) heading toward
Mars.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/20141006/mars20141006-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-340&rn=news.xml&rst=4323)\
\
NASA will host a briefing on Thursday, Oct. 9, to outline the space and
Earth-based assets that will image and study a comet from relatively
close range to Mars on Oct. 19.
