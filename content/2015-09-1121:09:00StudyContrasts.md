Study Contrasts Effects of Two Types of SoCal Fires
===================================================

Date: 2015-09-11 21:09:00

[![An October 2007 image, left, of Southern California wildfires shows
how offshore Santa Ana winds control these
events.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/fire/20150911/socalwilldfires2015091-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-297&rn=news.xml&rst=4717)\
\
Wildfires driven by autumn Santa Ana winds are 10 times more expensive
than summer fires, a new study shows. But both kinds are costly, and
both are on the increase.
