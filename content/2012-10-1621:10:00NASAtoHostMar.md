NASA to Host Mars Curiosity Rover Teleconference Oct. 18
========================================================

Date: 2012-10-16 21:10:00

[![This artist\'s concept features NASA\'s Mars Science Laboratory
Curiosity
rover](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20120607/pia15791-th.jpg){width="100"

NASA will host a media teleconference at noon PDT (3 p.m. EDT) on
Thursday, Oct. 18, about the latest status of the Curiosity rover\'s
mission to Mars.
