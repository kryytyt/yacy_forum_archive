NASA Teleconference to Preview Historic Flyby of Saturn Moon
============================================================

Date: 2015-10-22 21:10:00

[![Dramatic jets of
ice](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20151022/PIA11688-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-325&rn=news.xml&rst=4747)\
\
NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft will sample an extraterrestrial ocean next
week when it flies directly through a plume of icy spray from Enceladus.
A news telecon on Monday, Oct. 26, will preview the event.
