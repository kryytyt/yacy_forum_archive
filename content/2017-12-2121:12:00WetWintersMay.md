Wet Winters May Not Dampen Small Wildfires
==========================================

Date: 2017-12-21 21:12:00

[![Dried
grass](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20171221/wildfire20171221-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-324&rn=news.xml&rst=7029)\
\
A wet winter corresponds to more small wildfires in the following fire
season, not fewer, as is commonly assumed.
