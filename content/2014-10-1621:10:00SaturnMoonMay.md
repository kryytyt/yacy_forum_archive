Saturn Moon May Hide a \'Fossil\' Core or an Ocean
==================================================

Date: 2014-10-16 21:10:00

[![This mosaic of Saturn\'s moon Mimas was created from images taken by
Cassini](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20141016b/pia12569-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-359&rn=news.xml&rst=4342)\
\
A new study suggests the cratered surface of Saturn\'s icy moon Mimas
indicates the moon\'s frozen core is shaped something like a football,
or the moon has a liquid water ocean.
