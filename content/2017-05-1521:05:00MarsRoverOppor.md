Mars Rover Opportunity Begins Study of Valley\'s Origin
=======================================================

Date: 2017-05-15 21:05:00

[![Perseverance
Valley](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20170515/pia21495-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-139&rn=news.xml&rst=6844)\
\
NASA\'s Mars rover Opportunity has reached the main destination of its
current two-year extended mission \-- an ancient fluid-carved valley on
the inner slope of a crater rim.
