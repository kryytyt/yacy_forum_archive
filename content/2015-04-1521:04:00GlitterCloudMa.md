Glitter Cloud May Serve as Space Mirror
=======================================

Date: 2015-04-15 21:04:00

[![This image shows laser light reflected off of a glitter mirror onto a
camera
sensor.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/technology/20150415/tech20150415-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-131&rn=news.xml&rst=4553)\
\
Space telescopes may one day make use of glitter-like materials to help
take images of new worlds, according to JPL researchers.
