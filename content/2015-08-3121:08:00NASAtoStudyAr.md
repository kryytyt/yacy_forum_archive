NASA to Study Arctic Climate Change Ecosystem Impacts
=====================================================

Date: 2015-08-31 21:08:00

[![ABoVE campaign will combine field work, airborne surveys data and
computer
modeling](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/arctic/20150831/earth20150831-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-285&rn=news.xml&rst=4705)\
\
NASA has begun a multi-year field campaign to investigate ecological
impacts of the rapidly changing climate in Alaska and northwestern
Canada.
