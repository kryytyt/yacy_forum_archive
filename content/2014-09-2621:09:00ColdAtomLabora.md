Cold Atom Laboratory Chills Atoms to New Lows
=============================================

Date: 2014-09-26 21:09:00

[![Artist\'s concept of an atom chip for use by NASA\'s Cold Atom
Laboratory

NASA\'s Cold Atom Laboratory mission has succeeded in producing a state
of matter known as a Bose-Einstein condensate, a key breakthrough for
the instrument.
