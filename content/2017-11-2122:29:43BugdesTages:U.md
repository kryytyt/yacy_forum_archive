ausgibt](https://unix.stackexchange.com/questions/405783/why-does-man-print-gimme-gimme-gimme-at-0030).

Und die Erklärung, wieso man das tut, ist absolut großartig:

> The maintainer of man is a good friend of mine, and one day six years
> ago I jokingly said to him that if you invoke man after midnight it
> should print \"gimme gimme gimme\", because of the Abba song called
> \"Gimme gimme gimme a man after midnight\":
>
> <https://twitter.com/marnanel/status/132280557190119424>
>
> Well, he did actually put it in, a few people were amused to discover
> it, and we mostly forgot about it until today.

Wie geil!
