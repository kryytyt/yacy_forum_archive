Recent Hydrothermal Activity May Explain Ceres\' Brightest Area
===============================================================

Date: 2016-06-29 21:06:00

[![The center of Ceres\' mysterious Occator Crater is the brightest area
on the dwarf
planet.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20160629/PIA20694-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-164&rn=news.xml&rst=6547)\
\
The brightest area on Ceres, located in the mysterious Occator Crater,
has the highest concentration of carbonate minerals ever seen outside
Earth, according to a new study from scientists on NASA\'s Dawn mission.
