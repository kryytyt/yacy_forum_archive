NASA Targets May 2018 Launch of Mars InSight Mission
====================================================

Date: 2016-03-09 21:03:00

[![Artist\'s Concept of InSight Lander on

NASA\'s InSight mission to study the deep interior of Mars is targeting
a new launch window that begins May 5, 2018.
