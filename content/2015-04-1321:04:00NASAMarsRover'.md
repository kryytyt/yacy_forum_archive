NASA Mars Rover\'s Weather Data Bolster Case for Brine
======================================================

Date: 2015-04-13 21:04:00

[![Mars Weather-Station Tools on Rover\'s
Mast](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20150413/pia19164-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-127&rn=news.xml&rst=4549)\
\
Martian weather and soil conditions that NASA\'s Curiosity rover has
measured, together with a type of salt found in Martian soil, could put
liquid brine in the soil at night.
