Rosetta to Deploy Lander on November 12
=======================================

Date: 2014-09-26 21:09:00

[![Image depicts the primary landing site on comet
67P/Churyumov-Gerasimenko](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/rosetta/20140915/rosetta20140915-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-326&rn=news.xml&rst=4311)\
\
The European Space Agency\'s Rosetta mission will deploy its lander,
Philae, to the surface of comet 67P/Churyumov-Gerasimenko on Nov. 12.
