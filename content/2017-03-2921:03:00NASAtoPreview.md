NASA to Preview \'Grand Finale\' of Cassini Saturn Mission
==========================================================

Date: 2017-03-29 21:03:00

[![Artist\'s concept of Cassini\'s
orbits](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20170329/cassini20170329-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-093&rn=news.xml&rst=6799)\
\
NASA will hold a news conference on Tuesday, April 4, at JPL, to preview
the beginning of Cassini\'s final mission segment (called the Grand
Finale), which begins in late April.
