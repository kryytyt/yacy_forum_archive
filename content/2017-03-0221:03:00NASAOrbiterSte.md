NASA Orbiter Steers Clear of Mars Moon Phobos
=============================================

Date: 2017-03-02 21:03:00

[![This artist\'s sketch shows MAVEN above
Mars.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/maven/20170302/maven20170302-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-057&rn=news.xml&rst=6764)\
\
NASA\'s MAVEN spacecraft performed a previously unscheduled maneuver
this week to avoid a collision in the near future with Mars\' moon
Phobos.
