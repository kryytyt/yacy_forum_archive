Scientists Look to Skies to Improve Tsunami Detection
=====================================================

Date: 2017-05-17 21:05:00

[![Real-time detection of perturbations of the
ionosphere](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20170517/tsunami-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-143&rn=news.xml&rst=6848)\
\
A team of scientists has developed a new approach to assist in
development of timely tsunami detection systems, using measurements of
how tsunamis disturb Earth\'s atmosphere.
