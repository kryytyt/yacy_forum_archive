NASA \'Eyes\' Study Louisiana\'s Changing Wetlands
==================================================

Date: 2015-06-16 21:06:00

[![The Mississippi River Delta south of New
Orleans](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20150616/earth20150611b-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-205&rn=news.xml&rst=4621)\
\
NASA has completed an intensive study of Louisiana Gulf Coast levees and
wetlands, using three advanced instruments on two research aircraft.
