Versatile Instrument to Scout for Kuiper Belt Objects
=====================================================

Date: 2016-03-03 21:03:00

[![The Crab Pulsar was taken with CHIMERA, an instrument at the Palomar
Observatory](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/telescopes/20160303/PIA20347-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-059&rn=news.xml&rst=5702)\
\
The CHIMERA instrument at the Palomar Observatory near San Diego is
using hi-tech cameras to locate near-Earth asteroids and objects in the
Kuiper Belt.
