Small Asteroid or Comet \'Visits\' from Beyond the Solar System
===============================================================

Date: 2017-10-26 21:10:00

[![A/2017 U1 is most likely of interstellar
origin.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/comet/20171026/comet20171025-226.jpg){width="100"

to have originated from outside the solar system, coming from somewhere
else in our galaxy.
