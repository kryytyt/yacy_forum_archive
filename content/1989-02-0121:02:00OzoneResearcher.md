Ozone Researcher Receives AAAS Newcomb-Cleveland Prize
======================================================

Date: 1989-02-01 21:02:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5838)\
\
A paper written by JPL ozone researcher Dr. Mario Molina, which has come
to form the basis for current scientific understanding of Antarctic
ozone depletion, has been recognized by Science magazine as the best
paper it published during the June 1987-May 1988 period.
