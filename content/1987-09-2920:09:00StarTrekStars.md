Star Trek Stars Appears for JPL United Way Kickoff
==================================================

Date: 1987-09-29 20:09:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5785)\
\
Actor William Shatner and others from the \"Star Trek\" television and
motion-picture casts will appear at kickoff program for the Jet
Propulsion Laboratory\'s United Way campaign at noon Wednesday, Oct. 7,
in JPL\'s mall.
