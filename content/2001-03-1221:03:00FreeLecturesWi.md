Free Lectures Will Describe Nasa\'s Double-Teaming of Jupiter
=============================================================

Date: 2001-03-12 21:03:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion
Laboratory](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6411)\
\
NASA is taking advantage of having two spacecraft near Jupiter to
examine that planet and its surroundings in ways neither spacecraft
could do alone, and one of the scientists who organized the campaign
will describe it during free public lectures in Pasadena this month.
