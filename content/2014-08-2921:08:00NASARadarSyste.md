NASA Radar System Surveys Napa Valley Quake Area
================================================

Date: 2014-08-29 21:08:00

[![NASA\'s C-20A Earth science research aircraft with the
UAVSAR](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/earthquake/20140829/earth20140829-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-293&rn=news.xml&rst=4276)\
\
A JPL-developed airborne radar system is conducting an airborne survey
of earthquake fault displacements caused by the Aug. 24 magnitude 6.0
quake in Napa Valley, California.
