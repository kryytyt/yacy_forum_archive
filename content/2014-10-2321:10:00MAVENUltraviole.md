MAVEN Ultraviolet Image of Comet Siding Spring\'s Hydrogen Coma
===============================================================

Date: 2014-10-23 21:10:00

[![MAVEN Ultraviolet Image of Comet Siding Spring\'s Hydrogen
Coma](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/maven/20141023/pia18619-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-371&rn=news.xml&rst=4352)\
\
NASA\'s MAVEN spacecraft obtained this ultraviolet image of hydrogen
surrounding comet Siding Spring on Oct. 17, 2014, two days before the
comet\'s closest approach to Mars.
