Mariner IV Reports Back to Earth
================================

Date: 1966-05-26 20:05:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6081)\
\
The Mariner IV spacecraft, which took the world\'s first closeup
pictures of Mars last year, is once again in contact with Earth
reporting on the space environment and its own operating performance
after 18 months of flight.
