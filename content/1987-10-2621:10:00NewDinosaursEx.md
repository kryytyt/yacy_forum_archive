New Dinosaurs Extinction Theory
===============================

Date: 1987-10-26 21:10:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5788)\
\
Extinction of the dinosaurs may have occurred 65 million years ago as
result of single asteroid or comet impact, but it is also possible they
died out as result of many comet impacts over one to three million
years, group of scientists has theorized.
