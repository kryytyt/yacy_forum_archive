Der Ex-Chef von Citigroup findet heute, dass man große \...
===========================================================

Date: 2015-11-02 13:31:54

[Der Ex-Chef von Citigroup findet
heute](http://www.ft.com/cms/s/0/2cfa6f18-1575-11e3-950a-00144feabdc0.html),
dass man große Banken zerschlagen muss und wieder eine Trennung zwischen
Banking und Investment Banking einführen muss. Das gab es früher, das
Gesetz hieß [Glass-Steagall
Act](https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Glass-Steagall_Act).

> Now retired, Mr Reed firmly believes that the business logic behind
> the transaction was flawed, and that the old Glass-Steagall division
> should be reinstated. Mr Reed first expressed this view soon after the
> crisis erupted. Last year Sandy Weill, the other main architect of the
> Citicorp-Travelers deal, changed his position to support breaking up
> the banks.
