NOAA\'s Jason-3 Spacecraft Ready for Launch
===========================================

Date: 2016-01-14 21:01:00

[![Artist\'s rendering of Jason-3. Credit:
NASA](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/jason/20160114/jason3-20160114-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-010&rn=news.xml&rst=4819)\
\
Jason-3, an international mission led by NOAA to continue U.S.-European
satellite measurements of ocean-surface topography, is scheduled for
launch on Jan. 17.
