NASA Soil Moisture Mission Produces First Global Maps
=====================================================

Date: 2015-04-21 21:04:00

[![SMAP radiometer image from March 31 - April 3,
2015](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/smap/20150421/smapradar20150421MAIN-226.jpg){width="100"

NASA\'s new satellite mission to map the water in the soil under our
feet has passed another key milestone by generating its first full
global maps.
