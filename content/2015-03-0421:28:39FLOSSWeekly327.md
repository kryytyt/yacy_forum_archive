Hosts: [Randal Schwartz](https://twitter.com/merlyn) and [Aaron
Newcomb](https://plus.google.com/+AaronNewcomb/posts)

Aaron Titus drops in to talk to us about Crisis Cleanup. Crisis Cleanup
is software that is designed to be a platform for inter-organization
cooperation, communication, coordination, and collaboration at disaster
sites. Crisis Cleanup\'s core objective is to embrace and support the
interests of disaster survivors by providing transparent, collaborative,
and privacy-enhancing open source technology to assist those who
directly interact with and help survivors.
