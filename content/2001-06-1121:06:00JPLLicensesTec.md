JPL Licenses Technology to Map Earth
====================================

Date: 2001-06-11 21:06:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion
Laboratory](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6262)\
\
A new radar mapping technology designed to generate high-resolution,
three-dimensional maps of Earth beneath foliage and other vegetation has
been licensed by NASA\'s Jet Propulsion Laboratory in Pasadena, Calif.,
to EarthData International, Inc., Fresno, Calif.
