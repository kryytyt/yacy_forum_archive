NASA Instrument on Rosetta Makes Comet Atmosphere Discovery
===========================================================

Date: 2015-06-02 21:06:00

[![Rosetta
Comet](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/rosetta/20150602/pia18899-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-189&rn=news.xml&rst=4609)\
\
NASA\'s Alice instrument on Rosetta finds that electrons near its target
comet cause rapid breakup of water and carbon dioxide molecules released
from the comet\'s nucleus.
