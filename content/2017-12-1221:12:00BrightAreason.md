Bright Areas on Ceres Suggest Geologic Activity
===============================================

Date: 2017-12-12 21:12:00

[![Bright areas of Occator
Crater](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20171212/PIA21913-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-317&rn=news.xml&rst=7022)\
\
Scientists have a better sense of how bright areas on Ceres formed and
changed over time \-- processes indicative of an active, evolving world.
