NASA\'s Juno Mission 26 Days from Jupiter
=========================================

Date: 2016-06-09 21:06:00

[![This artist\'s rendering shows NASA\'s Juno spacecraft making one of
its close passes over
Jupiter.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/juno/20160506/PIA19639-226.jpg){width="100"

NASA\'s Juno mission is now 26 days and 11.1 million miles (17.8 million
kilometers) away from the largest planetary inhabitant in our solar
system \-- Jupiter.
