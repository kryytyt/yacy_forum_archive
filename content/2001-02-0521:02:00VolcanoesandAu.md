Volcanoes and Auroras Glow in Eclipse Movie of Jupiter\'s Moon IO
=================================================================

Date: 2001-02-05 21:02:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion
Laboratory](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"

The first movie ever made of Jupiter\'s moon Io while it is in eclipse
shows bright spots of hot lava and changes in auroral glows.
