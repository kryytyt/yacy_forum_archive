Spacecraft Data Suggest Saturn Moon\'s Ocean May Harbor Hydrothermal Activity
=============================================================================

Date: 2015-03-11 20:03:00

[![Enceladus: Possible Hydrothermal
Activity](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20150311/pia19058-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-085&rn=news.xml&rst=4507)\
\
The hidden ocean of Saturn\'s moon Enceladus could be home to
present-day hydrothermal activity, according to two new studies by
scientists with NASA\'s Cassini mission.
