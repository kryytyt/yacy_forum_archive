NASA Mountaintop Sensor Finds High Methane over LA
==================================================

Date: 2015-01-13 21:01:00

[![The Los Angeles basin from Mt.
Wilson](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20150113/methane20150113-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-014&rn=news.xml&rst=4440)\
\
NASA instruments on a mountaintop show that Los Angeles\' annual methane
emissions are 18 to 61 percent higher than the best previous estimates.
