NASA Team Probes Peculiar Age-Defying Star
==========================================

Date: 2016-08-29 21:08:00

[![An age-defying star called IRAS 19312+1950 exhibits features
characteristic of a very young star and a very old
star.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/spitzer/20160829/pia20914-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-226&rn=news.xml&rst=6607)\
\
For years, astronomers have puzzled over a massive star lodged deep in
the Milky Way that shows conflicting signs of being extremely old and
extremely young.
