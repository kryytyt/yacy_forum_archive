Planck: First Stars Formed Later Than We Thought
================================================

Date: 2016-08-31 21:08:00

[![This artist\'s impression shows a portion of the universe\'s
history.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/planck/20160831/plank20160831-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-228&rn=news.xml&rst=6610)\
\
ESA\'s Planck satellite, has revealed that the first stars in the
universe started forming later than previous observations of the cosmic
microwave background indicated.
