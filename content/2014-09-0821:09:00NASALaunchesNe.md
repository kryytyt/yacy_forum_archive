NASA Launches New Era of Earth Science from Space Station
=========================================================

Date: 2014-09-08 21:09:00

[![ISS International Space
Station](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/rapidscat/20140908/iss20140908-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-301&rn=news.xml&rst=4286)\
\
The launch of a NASA ocean winds sensor to the International Space
Station (ISS) this month inaugurates a new era of Earth observation from
the space station\'s vantage point.
