Galileo Mission Exhibit Changes in Atmospheric Feature
======================================================

Date: 1989-10-10 20:10:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5873)\
\
The planet Jupiter, scientific objective of NASA\'s Galileo mission,
scheduled for launch this week, has recently exhibited dramatic changes
in major atmospheric feature.
