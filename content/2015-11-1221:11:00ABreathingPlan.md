A Breathing Planet, Off Balance
===============================

Date: 2015-11-12 21:11:00

[![A Breathing Planet, Off
Balance](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/climate/20151112c/carbon20151112-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-347&rn=news.xml&rst=4769)\
\
As people burn fossil fuels and clear forests, only half of the carbon
dioxide released stays in the atmosphere. Earth\'s vegetation ecosystems
and oceans remove the other half.
