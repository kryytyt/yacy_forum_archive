2007 OR10: Largest Unnamed World in the Solar System
====================================================

Date: 2016-05-11 21:05:00

[![New K2 results peg 2007 OR10 as the largest unnamed body in our solar
system.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/kepler/20160511/kepler20160511-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-127&rn=news.xml&rst=6509)\
\
New results from NASA\'s Kepler/K2 mission reveal the largest unnamed
body in our solar system and the third largest of the current roster of
dwarf planets.
