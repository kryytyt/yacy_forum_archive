Jason-3 Launches to Monitor Global Sea Level Rise
=================================================

Date: 2016-01-17 21:01:00

[![A SpaceX Falcon 9 rocket carrying the U.S.-European Jason-3 satellite
launches](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/jason/20160117/Jason3-20160117-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-014&rn=news.xml&rst=4823)\
\
Jason-3, a U.S.-European oceanography satellite mission with NASA
participation, lifted off from Vandenberg Air Force Base in California
Sunday at 10:42 a.m. PST (1:42 EST) aboard a SpaceX Falcon 9 rocket.
