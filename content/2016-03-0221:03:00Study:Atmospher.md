Study: Atmospheric River Storms Can Reduce Sierra Snow
======================================================

Date: 2016-03-02 21:03:00

[![Rain falling on
snow.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/california/20160302/earth20160302-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-058&rn=news.xml&rst=5648)\
\
A new study finds atmospheric river storms are 2.5 times more likely
than other types of winter storms to cause destructive rains that melt
snow in California\'s Sierra Nevada.
