NASA\'s Curiosity Mars Rover Heads Toward Active Dunes
======================================================

Date: 2015-11-16 21:11:00

[![Curiosity Rover Will Study Dunes on Route up
Mountain](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20151116/pia19928-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-351&rn=news.xml&rst=4772)\
\
The NASA Mars rover that is studying layers of a Martian mountain will
soon get its first taste of the \"Bagnold Dunes,\" a dark sea of sand
along the mountain\'s base.
