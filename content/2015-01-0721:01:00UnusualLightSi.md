Unusual Light Signal Hints at Distant Black Hole Merger
=======================================================

Date: 2015-01-07 21:01:00

[![An artist\'s conception of a black hole binary in a heart of a
quasar](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/universe/quasar/20150107/quasar20150107-226.jpg){width="100"

Scientists have found what appear to be two supermassive black holes in
the final stages of a merger, a rare event never seen before.
