Joker arrested for \"wearing a mask in public\"
===============================================

Date: 2017-03-27 02:24:10

[![](https://www.jwz.org/images/joker.jpg)Jeremy Putnam, 31, was
arrested in Winchester, Virginia, and charged with \"wearing a mask in
public,\" a felony in that
state.](http://boingboing.net/2017/03/25/man-costumed-as-the-joker-arre.html)

> He was armed with a \"sword\" in public, which apparently alarmed
> residents. But they haven\'t charged him with that; they\'ve charged
> him with this, a fascinatingly terrible law:
