JPL Seeks Robotic Spacecraft Development for Asteroid Redirect Mission
======================================================================

Date: 2016-09-20 21:09:00

[![Artist\'s concept of Asteroid Redirect
Vehicle](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/arm/arm20160920-16.jpg){width="100"

the robotic spacecraft to capture an asteroid boulder for the Asteroid
Redirect Mission.
