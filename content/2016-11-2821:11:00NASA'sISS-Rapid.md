NASA\'s ISS-RapidScat Earth Science Mission Ends
================================================

Date: 2016-11-28 21:11:00

[![Artist\'s rendering of NASA\'s ISS-RapidScat and
instrument](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/rapidscat/20160909/rapidscat-226.jpg){width="100"

\
NASA\'s ISS-RapidScat Earth science instrument has ended operations
following a successful two-year mission aboard the International Space
Station.
