Cassini Caught in Hyperion\'s Particle Beam
===========================================

Date: 2014-10-16 21:10:00

[![Cassini obtained this false-color view of Saturn\'s moon
Hyperion](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20141016/cassini20141016-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-357&rn=news.xml&rst=4340)\
\
New analysis indicates that during a 2005 flyby, NASA\'s Cassini
spacecraft was zapped by a beam of electrons coming from the surface of
Saturn\'s moon Hyperion.
