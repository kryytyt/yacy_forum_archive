Second Viking Oven May Have Failed
==================================

Date: 1976-01-27 21:01:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6043)\
\
One of three small ovens in an experiment aboard the Viking 1 spacecraft
may not be working, similar to condition on Viking 2 reported last week.
Both Vikings are enroute to Mars for the first U.S. attempt to land on
the planet.
