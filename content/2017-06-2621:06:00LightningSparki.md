Lightning Sparking More Boreal Forest Fires
===========================================

Date: 2017-06-26 21:06:00

[![A lightning-caused wildfire burns in Alberta,
Canada.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/terra/20170626/earth20170626-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-178&rn=news.xml&rst=6883)\
\
A new study finds lightning storms were the main driver of recent
massive fire years in Alaska and northern Canada, with the storms likely
to migrate north as climate warms.
