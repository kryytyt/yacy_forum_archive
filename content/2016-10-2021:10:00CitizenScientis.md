Citizen Scientists Seek South Pole \'Spiders\' on Mars
======================================================

Date: 2016-10-20 21:10:00

[![This image shows spidery channels eroded into Martian
ground.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20161020/PIA21126-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-272&rn=news.xml&rst=6654)\
\
Ten thousand volunteers viewing images of Mars have helped identify
targets for closer inspection, yielding new insights about erosional
features known as \"spiders.\"
