Cassini Completes Final \-- and Fateful \-- Titan Flyby
=======================================================

Date: 2017-04-24 21:04:00

[![](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20170424/cassini20170424-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-120&rn=news.xml&rst=6825)\
\
NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft has had its last close brush with Saturn\'s
hazy moon Titan and is now beginning its final set of 22 orbits around
the ringed planet.
