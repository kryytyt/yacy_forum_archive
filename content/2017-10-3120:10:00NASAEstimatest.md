NASA Estimates the Global Reach of Atmospheric Rivers
=====================================================

Date: 2017-10-31 20:10:00

[![This visualization uses satellite data to show the movement of water
vapor and precipitation as an atmospheric river slams into
California.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20171031/atmospheric_river-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-283&rn=news.xml&rst=6988)\
\
A recent study estimates the global impact of atmospheric rivers on
floods and droughts, as well as the number of people affected by these
atmospheric phenomena.
