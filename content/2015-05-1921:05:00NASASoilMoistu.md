NASA Soil Moisture Mission Begins Science Operations
====================================================

Date: 2015-05-19 21:05:00

[![High-resolution global soil moisture map from SMAP\'s combined radar
and radiometer
instruments](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/smap/20150519/pia19337-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-171&rn=news.xml&rst=4591)\
\
NASA\'s new Soil Moisture Active Passive (SMAP) mission to map global
soil moisture and detect whether soils are frozen or thawed has begun
science operations.
