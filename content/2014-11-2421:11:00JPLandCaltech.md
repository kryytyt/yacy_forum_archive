JPL and Caltech to Host 2018 COSPAR Conference
==============================================

Date: 2014-11-24 21:11:00

[![Pasadena, California, will host the 2018 COSPAR
meeting.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/technology/20141124/cospar20141124-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-409&rn=news.xml&rst=4390)\
\
The Committee on Space Research (COSPAR), an international scientific
organization, will have its 2018 meeting in Pasadena, California, hosted
by Caltech and supported by JPL.
