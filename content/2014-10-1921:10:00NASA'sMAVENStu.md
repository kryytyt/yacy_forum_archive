NASA\'s MAVEN Studies Passing Comet and Its Effects
===================================================

Date: 2014-10-19 21:10:00

[![Illustration of the MAVEN Mars
orbiter](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/20141019/maven-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-362&rn=news.xml&rst=4345)\
\
NASA\'s newest orbiter at Mars, MAVEN, took precautions to avoid harm
from a dust-spewing comet that flew near Mars today and is studying the
flyby\'s effects on the Red Planet\'s atmosphere.
