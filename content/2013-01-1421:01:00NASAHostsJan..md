NASA Hosts Jan. 15 Telecon About Mars Rover Progress
====================================================

Date: 2013-01-14 21:01:00

[![This artist\'s concept features NASA\'s Mars Science Laboratory
Curiosity
rover](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20120607/pia15791-th.jpg){width="100"

\
NASA will host a media teleconference at 10 a.m. PST (1 p.m. EST) on
Tuesday, Jan. 15, to provide an update about the Curiosity rover\'s
mission to Mars\' Gale Crater.
