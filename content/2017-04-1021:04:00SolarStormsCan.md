Solar Storms Can Drain Electrical Charge Above Earth
====================================================

Date: 2017-04-10 21:04:00

[![A solar eruption on Sept. 26,
2014](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20170410/solar041017-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-103&rn=news.xml&rst=6804)\
\
Solar storms can vacuum electrical charge from upper-atmosphere regions
above the Arctic and create regions of excess charge.
