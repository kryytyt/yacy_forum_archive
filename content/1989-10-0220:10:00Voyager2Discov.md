Voyager 2 Discovers Eruption on Triton
======================================

Date: 1989-10-02 20:10:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5872)\
\
five-mile-tall, geyser-like plume of dark material has been discovered
erupting from the surface of Neptune\'s moon Triton in one of the images
returned last month to the Jet Propulsion Laboratory, Pasadena, Calif.,
by NASA\'s Voyager 2 spacecraft.
