NASA Tech Aids Search Following Mexico Quake
============================================

Date: 2017-09-22 21:09:00

[![An emergency responder in Mexico City carries an orange case holding
a radar instrument called
FINDER.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/technology/20170922/finder20170922-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-248&rn=news.xml&rst=6953)\
\
Disaster relief workers in Mexico City who are responding to a
7.1-magnitude earthquake used a suitcase-sized radar instrument than can
detect human heartbeats under rubble.
