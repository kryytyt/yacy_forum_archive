Fly Over Ceres in New Video
===========================

Date: 2015-06-08 21:06:00

[![The images come from Dawn\'s first mapping orbit at
Ceres](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20150608/dawn20150608-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-192&rn=news.xml&rst=4614)\
\
A new animated video of dwarf planet Ceres, based on images taken by
NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft, provides a unique perspective of this heavily
cratered, mysterious world.
