UTexas-NASA Study Sees New Threat to East Antarctic Ice
=======================================================

Date: 2015-03-16 20:03:00

[![East Antarctic
Coastline](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/glacier/20150316/earth20150316-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-089&rn=news.xml&rst=4511)\
\
Researchers have discovered two seafloor troughs that could allow warm
ocean water to reach the base of Totten Glacier, East Antarctica\'s
largest and most rapidly thinning glacier.
