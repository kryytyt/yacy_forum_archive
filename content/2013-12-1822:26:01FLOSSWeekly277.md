**Hosts:** [Randal Schwartz](http://twit.tv/floss) and [Gareth
Greenaway](http://www.twitter.com/garethgreenaway).

Magnolia is an open Java CMS that delivers smartphone simplicity on an
enterprise-scale. Magnolia CMS allows organizations to orchestrate
online services, sales and marketing across all digital channels,
maximizing the impact of every touchpoint. Blossom integrates Magnolia
CMS and Spring Framework, the framework for building Java applications
and Blossom brings the flexibility and capabilities of Spring to
Magnolia.

**Guests:** [Tobias Mattsson](http://www.magnolia-cms.com/) and [Zak
