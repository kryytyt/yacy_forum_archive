Stardust Discovers Potential Interstellar Space Particles
=========================================================

Date: 2014-08-14 21:08:00

[![35 micron-long hole produced by a 3 picogram
mote](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/stardust/20140814/stardust20140814-226.jpg){width="100"

\
Seven microscopic dust particles collected during NASA\'s Stardust
mission may be the first samples of contemporary interstellar dust.
