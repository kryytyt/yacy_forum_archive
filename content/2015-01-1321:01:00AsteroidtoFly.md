Asteroid to Fly By Earth Safely on January 26
=============================================

Date: 2015-01-13 21:01:00

[![This graphic depicts the passage of asteroid 2004
BL86](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/asteroid/20150113/Asteroid2004BL86-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-015&rn=news.xml&rst=4441)\
\
The January 26 flyby of asteroid 2004 BL86 will be the closest by any
known space rock this large until an asteroid flies past Earth in 2027.
