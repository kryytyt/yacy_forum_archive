Junior-High Students Science Projects Awarded
=============================================

Date: 1988-04-22 21:04:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5817)\
\
Projects ranging from study of \"superlearning\" to experiments with the
effectiveness of cough drops have won awards for handful of junior-high
students who entered area science fairs coordinated with JPL.
