NASA\'s Kepler Mission Discovers Bigger, Older Cousin to Earth
==============================================================

Date: 2015-07-23 21:07:00

[![Artist\'s concept depicts one possible appearance of the planet
Kepler-452b](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/kepler/20150723/pia19824-226.jpg){width="100"

\
NASA\'s Kepler mission has confirmed the first near-Earth-size planet in
the \"habitable zone\" around a sun-like star.
