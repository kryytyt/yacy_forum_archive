NASA Determines Areas of Mars to Photograph
===========================================

Date: 1965-02-15 21:02:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6068)\
\
The areas on Mars that are expected to be photographed by the single
television camera aboard Mariner IV have been determined by the National
Aeronautics and Space Administration.
