Hubble Detects Giant \'Cannonballs\' Shooting from Star
=======================================================

Date: 2016-10-06 21:10:00

[![This four-panel graphic illustrates how the binary-star system V
Hydrae is launching balls of plasma into
space.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/hubble/20161006/PIA21071-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-257&rn=news.xml&rst=6639)\
\
NASA\'s Hubble Space Telescope has detected superhot blobs of gas, each
twice as massive as the planet Mars, being ejected near a dying star.
