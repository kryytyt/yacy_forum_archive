**Hosts:** [Aaron Newcomb](http://twitter.com/aaronnewcomb) and [Dan
Lynch](https://twitter.com/methoddan).

Mifos.org is a diverse community of microfinance institutions,
technology professionals, business people, volunteers, and contributors.

Ed Cable has been a part of the Mifos project since 2007 in its early
days at\
Grameen Foundation. He oversaw the open source community, connecting
its\
members worldwide with the tools, support, and engagement needed to
build\
and use Mifos.

Vishwas Babu is the lead engineer at the Mifos Initiative where he works
on\
building an open technology platform for financial inclusion to the
poor.

**Guests:** [Ed Cable](http://mifos.org/), [Vishwas
Babu](http://mifos.org/).
