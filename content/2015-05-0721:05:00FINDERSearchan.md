FINDER Search and Rescue Technology Helped Save Lives in Nepal
==============================================================

Date: 2015-05-07 21:05:00

[![The Finding Individuals for Disaster and Emergency
Response](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/earthquake/20150507/finder20150507-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-157&rn=news.xml&rst=4578)\
\
In the wreckage of a collapsed textile factory and another building in
the Nepalese village of Chautara, four men were rescued, thanks to a
NASA technology that was able to find their heartbeats.
