Dawn Mission Honored With Collier Trophy
========================================

Date: 2016-06-10 21:06:00

[![Annual Robert J. Collier Trophy Dinner on Thursday, June 9, 2016 in
Arlington,
Va.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/awards/20160610/collier20160610-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-147&rn=news.xml&rst=6530)\
\
NASA\'s Dawn mission, the first spacecraft to orbit two extraterrestrial
targets, has been honored with the National Aeronautic Association\'s
2016 Robert J. Collier Trophy.
