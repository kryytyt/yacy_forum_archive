NASA Schedules Mariner D Launch No Earlier Than November 27
===========================================================

Date: 1962-11-18 21:11:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6065)\
\
National Aeronautics and Space administration officials are preparing
the Mariner D spacecraft for launch on a Mars trajectory within the
current launch opportunity. The launch of Mariner D, now undergoing
final checkout at Cape Kennedy, has awaited analysis of the failure of
the Mariner 3 mission launched November 5.
