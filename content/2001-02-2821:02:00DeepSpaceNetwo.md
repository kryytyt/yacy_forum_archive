Deep Space Network To Hear Last From Faraway Near
=================================================

Date: 2001-02-28 21:02:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion
Laboratory](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6156)\
\
The last data from the overachieving Near Earth Asteroid Rendezvous
(NEAR Shoemaker) mission to asteroid Eros will be received today via
NASA\'s Deep Space Network telecommunications system.
