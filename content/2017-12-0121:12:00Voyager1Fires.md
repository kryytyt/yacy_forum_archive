Voyager 1 Fires Up Thrusters After 37 Years
===========================================

Date: 2017-12-01 21:12:00

[![An artist concept depicting one of the twin Voyager
spacecraft.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/voyager/20170731/PIA21839-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-310&rn=news.xml&rst=7014)\
\
The Voyager team is able to use a set of four backup thrusters, dormant
since 1980, for orienting the spacecraft.
