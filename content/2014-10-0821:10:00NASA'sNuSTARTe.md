NASA\'s NuSTAR Telescope Discovers Shockingly Bright Dead Star
==============================================================

Date: 2014-10-08 21:10:00

[![NuSTAR Finds a Pulse in Cigar
Galaxy](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/nustar/20141008/pia18841-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-345&rn=news.xml&rst=4328)\
\
Astronomers have found a pulsating, dead star beaming with the energy of
about 10 million suns. This is the brightest pulsar - a dense stellar
remnant left over from a supernova explosion - ever recorded.
