Tiny U.S. Region Is Methane \'Hot Spot,\' NASA Finds
====================================================

Date: 2014-10-09 21:10:00

[![This map shows anomalous U.S. methane
emissions](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20141009/methane20141008-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-348&rn=news.xml&rst=4331)\
\
One small \"hot spot\" in the U.S. Southwest is responsible for
producing the largest concentration of the greenhouse gas methane seen
over the United States.
