Send Your Name to Mars on NASA\'s Next Red Planet Mission
=========================================================

Date: 2015-08-18 21:08:00

[![Your Name Could Go to Mars Aboard NASA\'s InSight
Lander](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/insight/20150818/insight20150818-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-270&rn=news.xml&rst=4691)\
\
Mars enthusiasts can participate in NASA\'s journey to Mars by adding
their names to a silicon microchip headed to Mars aboard NASA\'s InSight
Mars lander, scheduled to launch next year.
