NASA Selects New Science Teams for Astrobiology Research
========================================================

Date: 2014-10-06 21:10:00

[![Artist concept of an early
Earth](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/astrobiology/20141006/astrobiology20141006-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-341&rn=news.xml&rst=4324)\
\
NASA has awarded five-year grants totaling almost \$50 million to seven
research teams nationwide, including one from the agency\'s Jet
Propulsion Laboratory in Pasadena, California, to study the origins,
evolution, distribution and future of life in the universe.
