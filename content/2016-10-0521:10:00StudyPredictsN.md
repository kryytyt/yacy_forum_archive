Study Predicts Next Global Dust Storm on Mars
=============================================

Date: 2016-10-05 21:10:00

[![Dust Haze Hiding the Martian Surface in
2001](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/20161005/pia03170-16.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-256&rn=news.xml&rst=6638)\
\
Global dust storms on Mars could soon become more predictable \-- which
would be a boon for future astronauts there \-- if the next one follows
a pattern suggested by the past.
