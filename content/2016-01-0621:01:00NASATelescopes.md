NASA Telescopes Find \'Twins\' of Superstar Eta Carinae
=======================================================

Date: 2016-01-06 21:01:00

[![Eta twins in Galaxy
M83](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/spitzer/20160106/spitzerhubble20160106-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-004&rn=news.xml&rst=4813)\
\
A new study has found five objects with similar properties to the most
luminous and massive stellar system within 10,000 light-years of Earth.
