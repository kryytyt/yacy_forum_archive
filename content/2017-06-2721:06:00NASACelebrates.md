NASA Celebrates International Asteroid Day with Special Broadcast
=================================================================

Date: 2017-06-27 21:06:00

[![Artist\'s concept of a near-Earth
object.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/asteroid/20150126/asteroid20161103-226.jpg){width="100"

special program on NASA TV and online featuring the agency\'s work to
find and study near-Earth objects.
