NASA to Highlight Results from New Earth Missions
=================================================

Date: 2015-02-23 21:02:00

[![Five new Earth science missions have joined NASA\'s orbiting
fleet](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20150223/earth20150223-16.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-063&rn=news.xml&rst=4487)\
\
Over the past 12 months NASA has added five missions to its orbiting
Earth-observing fleet. NASA scientists will share early observations
during a media telecon Feb. 26.
