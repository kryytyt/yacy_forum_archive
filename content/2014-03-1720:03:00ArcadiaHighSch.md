Arcadia High School Surfs Over Competition at JPL
=================================================

Date: 2014-03-17 20:03:00

[![](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/events/20140317/bowl20140317-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.cfm?release=2014-083&rn=news.xml&rst=4080)\
\
Arcadia High School of Arcadia, Calif., beat 23 other California high
schools in an all-day \"buzzer-beater\"-style Ocean Sciences Bowl held
at JPL.
