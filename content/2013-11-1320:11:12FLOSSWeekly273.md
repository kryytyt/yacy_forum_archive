**Hosts: [Aaron Newcomb](http://thesourceshow.org/) and [Gareth
Greenaway](https://twitter.com/garethgreenaway).**

RDO is a community of people using and deploying OpenStack on Red Hat
and Red Hat-based platforms. We have documentation to help get started,
forums where you can connect with other users, and community-supported
packages of the most up-to-date OpenStack releases available for
download.

**Guests:** [Dave Neary](http://openstack.redhat.com/Main_Page) and
[Rich Bowen](http://openstack.redhat.com/Main_Page)
