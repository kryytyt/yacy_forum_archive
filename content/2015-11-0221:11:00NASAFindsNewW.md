NASA Finds New Way to Track Ocean Currents from Space
=====================================================

Date: 2015-11-02 21:11:00

[![GRACE satellites measured Atlantic Ocean bottom pressure as an
indicator of deep ocean current
speed.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/grace/20151102/earth20151102-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-339&rn=news.xml&rst=4761)\
\
NASA and university scientists have developed a new way to use satellite
measurements to track changes in Atlantic Ocean currents, which are a
driving force in global climate.
