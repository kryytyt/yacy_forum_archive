NASA\'s Juno to Soar Closest to Jupiter This Saturday
=====================================================

Date: 2016-08-25 21:08:00

[![This dual view of Jupiter was taken on August
23.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/juno/20160825/juno20160825-226.jpg){width="100"

\
This Saturday, NASA\'s Juno spacecraft will get closer to the cloud tops
of Jupiter than at any other time during its prime mission.
