NASA: California Drought Causing Valley Land to Sink
====================================================

Date: 2015-08-19 21:08:00

[![Total subsidence in California\'s San Joaquin Valley for the period
May 3, 2014 to Jan. 22,
2015](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/earthquake/20150819/earth20150819-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-273&rn=news.xml&rst=4693)\
\
A new NASA report shows land in California\'s San Joaquin Valley is
sinking faster than ever as Californians continue pumping groundwater in
response to the historic drought.
