**Hosts:** [Aaron Newcomb](http://google.com/+AaronNewcomb) and
[Guillermo Ameral](http://twitter.com/gamaral).

Sqitch is a database change management application. Sqitch is not
integrated with any framework, ORM, or platform. Rather, it is a
standalone change management system with no opinions about your database
engine, application framework, or development environment.

**Guest:** [David E. Wheeler](http://sqitch.org/)

David E. Wheeler is the creator, developer, and maintainer of Sqitch. He
has been involved in many open-source projects in his career.
