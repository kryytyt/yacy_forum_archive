JPL Debuts \'A Ticket to Explore JPL\'
======================================

Date: 2016-04-11 21:04:00

[![Visitors can enjoy a model rover demonstration at \'A Ticket to
Explore JPL\' at NASA\'s JPL on June
4-5.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/events/exploreJPL/20160411/oh20160411-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-102&rn=news.xml&rst=6362)\
\
JPL has a new approach to its annual public weekend. To ensure a safe,
enjoyable experience, the event formerly called \"Open House\" will
require tickets in 2016.
