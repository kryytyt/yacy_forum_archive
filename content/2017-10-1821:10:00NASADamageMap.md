NASA Damage Map Aids California Wildfire Response
=================================================

Date: 2017-10-18 21:10:00

[![NASA Damage Map Aids Northern California Wildfire
Response](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/california/20171018/PIA22048-226.jpg){width="100"

\
A NASA team has mapped areas in northern California likely damaged by
the recent wildfires, for use in the wildfire response.
