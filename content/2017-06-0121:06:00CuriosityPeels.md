Curiosity Peels Back Layers on Ancient Martian Lake
===================================================

Date: 2017-06-01 21:06:00

[![Sedimentary Signs of a Martian Lakebed (Shallow
Part)](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20170601/PIA19074-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-158&rn=news.xml&rst=6863)\
\
A long-lasting lake on ancient Mars provided stable environmental
conditions that differed from one part of the lake to another, NASA\'s
Curiosity rover mission has found.
