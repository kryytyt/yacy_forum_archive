Rosetta\'s Singing Comet
========================

Date: 2014-11-11 21:11:00

[![Sound wave superimposed on an image of comet
67P/Churyumov-Gerasimenko](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/rosetta/20141111/rosetta_singing20141111-226.jpg){width="100"

sing,\" thanks to instruments on the European Space Agency\'s Rosetta
spacecraft.
