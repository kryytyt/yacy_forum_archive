Study Finds Widespread Land Losses from Gulf Oil Spill
======================================================

Date: 2016-11-17 21:11:00

[![2010 photo of a shoreline in Bay Jimmy, Plaquemines Parish,
Louisiana](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/uavsar/20161117/oilspill20161117-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-296&rn=news.xml&rst=6677)\
\
Dramatic shoreline loss is revealed in new NASA/USGS maps of Louisiana
wetlands where the coastline was heavily coated with oil during the 2010
Gulf of Mexico oil spill.
