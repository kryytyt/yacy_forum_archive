Satellites See Silicon Valley\'s Quick Drought Recovery
=======================================================

Date: 2017-10-03 21:10:00

[![Ground motion in California\'s Santa Clara Valley from 2011 to
2015.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/california/20171003/calif20171003-226.jpg){width="100"

A space-based study finds underground water reserves in California\'s
Silicon Valley rebounded quickly from the recent drought in response to
conservation measures.
