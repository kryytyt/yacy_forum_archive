Flares May Threaten Planet Habitability Near Red Dwarfs
=======================================================

Date: 2017-06-06 21:06:00

[![This illustration shows a red dwarf star orbited by a hypothetical
exoplanet.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/galex/20170606/PIA21473-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-161&rn=news.xml&rst=6866)\
\
Data from the GALEX spacecraft suggest that planets around cool dwarf
stars may be subjected to intense flares.
