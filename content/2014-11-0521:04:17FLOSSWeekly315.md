Guest: [Slava Akhmechet](http://www.defmacro.org/).

RethinkDB is an open-source distributed database built to function on
the web in real-time. Slava Akhmechet is the CEO and one of the founders
at RethinkDB. Slava is interested in web technologies, distributed
systems, and building delightful developer products. Prior to starting
RethinkDB, Slava designed and implemented realtime trading systems in
the financial industry.
