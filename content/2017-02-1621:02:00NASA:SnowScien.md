NASA: Snow Science Supporting the US Water Supply
=================================================

Date: 2017-02-16 21:02:00

[![SnowEx campaign will improve remote sensing
measurements](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20170216/snow20170216-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-039&rn=news.xml&rst=6748)\
\
Researchers have completed the first flights of a NASA-led field
campaign that is targeting one of the biggest gaps in scientists\'
understanding of Earth\'s water resources: snow.
