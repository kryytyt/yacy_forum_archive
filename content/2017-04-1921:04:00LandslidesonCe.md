Landslides on Ceres Reflect Ice Content
=======================================

Date: 2017-04-19 21:04:00

[![NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft has revealed many landslides on
Ceres](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20170419/dawn20170419-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-114&rn=news.xml&rst=6820)\
\
As NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft continues exploring Ceres, evidence mounts
that the enigmatic dwarf planet retains a significant amount of water
ice.
