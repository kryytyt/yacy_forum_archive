On the Road to Finding Other Earths
===================================

Date: 2016-04-28 21:04:00

[![This illustration shows the prototype
starshade](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/universe/20140320/starshade20140320-226.jpg){width="100"

\
NASA is developing new technologies to help astronomers capture the
shine of Earth-like worlds and ultimately look for evidence of life
elsewhere in our galaxy.
