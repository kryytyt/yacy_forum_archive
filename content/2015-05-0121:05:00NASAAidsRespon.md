NASA Aids Response to Nepal Quake
=================================

Date: 2015-05-01 21:05:00

[![The decrease in emitted light over Nepal following the April 25, 2015
quake](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/earthquake/20150430/nepal20150430-16.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-149&rn=news.xml&rst=4571)\
\
NASA and its partners are gathering the best available science and
information on the April 25 magnitude 7.8 earthquake in Nepal to assist
in relief and humanitarian operations.
