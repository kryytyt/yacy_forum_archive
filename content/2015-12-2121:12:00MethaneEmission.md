Methane Emissions in Arctic Cold Season Higher Than Expected
============================================================

Date: 2015-12-21 21:12:00

[![Methane emissions in
Alaska](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20151221/MAINArctic-methane-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-383&rn=news.xml&rst=4804)\
\
The amount of methane escaping from the ground during the Arctic\'s long
cold period each year and entering Earth\'s atmosphere is likely much
higher than current estimates.
