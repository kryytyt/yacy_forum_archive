New Light on the Future of a Key Antarctic Glacier
==================================================

Date: 2017-05-31 21:05:00

[![Thwaites Glacier. Credit: NASA/James
Yungel](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/antarctica/20170531/ThwaitesGlacier20170530-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-156&rn=news.xml&rst=6861)\
\
Thwaites Glacier is melting rapidly but not as fast as earlier research
predicted, according to a new NASA study using a more realistic computer
model.
