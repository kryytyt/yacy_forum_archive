SMAP Readiness Review Gives \'Go\' for Launch
=============================================

Date: 2015-01-27 21:01:00

[![Sunset at
Vandenberg](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/smap/20150127/pia19137-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-037&rn=news.xml&rst=4464)\
\
Managers from NASA and United Launch Alliance (ULA) met Tues., Jan. 27,
at Vandenberg Air Force Base to assess the status of NASA\'s Soil
Moisture Active Passive spacecraft and the ULA Delta II rocket that will
boost SMAP into space.
