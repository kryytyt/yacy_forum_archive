NASA Celebrates Earth Day with Public Events, Online Activities
===============================================================

Date: 2015-04-14 21:04:00

[![NASA Administrator Charles Bolden speaks to
students](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/earthday/20150414/earth20150414-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-130&rn=news.xml&rst=4552)\
\
NASA will celebrate the 45th annual Earth Day April 17-22 with a variety
of live and online activities to engage the public in the agency\'s
mission to better understand and protect our home planet.
