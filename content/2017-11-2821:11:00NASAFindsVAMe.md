NASA Finds VA Metro Area Is Sinking Unevenly
============================================

Date: 2017-11-28 21:11:00

[![Motorists crossing a flooded
street](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20171128/norfolk20171128-226.jpg){width="100"

\
The Hampton Roads metropolitan area is sinking at highly uneven rates,
with a few spots subsiding 7 to 10 times faster than the area average.
