Voyager 2 Enters Final Planetary Encounter
==========================================

Date: 1989-06-05 21:06:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5854)\
\
NASA\'s Voyager 2 spacecraft today entered the observatoryphase of its
flyby of Neptune, signaling the beginning of its final planetary
encounter after nearly 12 years of exploring the outer solar system.
