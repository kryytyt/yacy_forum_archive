It\'s the Final Act for Larsen B Ice Shelf, NASA Finds
======================================================

Date: 2015-05-14 21:05:00

[![Antarctica\'s Larsen B Ice Shelf is likely to shatter into hundreds
of
icebergs](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/antarctica/20150514/antarctica20150514-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-170&rn=news.xml&rst=4589)\
\
NASA has found that the last section of Antarctica\'s Larsen B Ice
Shelf, which partially collapsed in 2002, is likely to disintegrate
before the end of the decade.
