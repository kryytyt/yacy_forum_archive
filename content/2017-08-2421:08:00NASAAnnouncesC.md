NASA Announces Cassini End-of-Mission Activities
================================================

Date: 2017-08-24 21:08:00

[![NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft is shown during its Sept. 15,
2017](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20170824/PIA21440-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-226&rn=news.xml&rst=6930)\
\
On Sept. 15, NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft will complete its remarkable
story of exploration with an intentional plunge into Saturn\'s
atmosphere, ending its mission after nearly 20 years in space.
