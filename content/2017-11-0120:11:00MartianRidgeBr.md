Martian Ridge Brings Out Rover\'s Color Talents
===============================================

Date: 2017-11-01 20:11:00

[![image from the Mast Camera (Mastcam) on NASA\'s Curiosity rover

using on Mars since 2012 are proving particularly helpful on a
mountainside ridge the rover is now climbing.
