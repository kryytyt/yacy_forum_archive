Scientists Find Evidence of \'Diving\' Tectonic Plates on Europa
================================================================

Date: 2014-09-08 21:09:00

[![Conceptual illustration of the subduction
process](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/europa/20140908/europa20140908-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-300&rn=news.xml&rst=4285)\
\
Scientists have found evidence of plate tectonics on Jupiter\'s moon
Europa. This indicates the first sign of this type of surface-shifting
geological activity on a world other than Earth.
