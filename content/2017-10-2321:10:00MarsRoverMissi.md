Mars Rover Mission Progresses Toward Resumed Drilling
=====================================================

Date: 2017-10-23 21:10:00

[![NASA\'s Curiosity Mars rover conducted a test on Oct. 17,
2017.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20171023/PIA22063-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-276&rn=news.xml&rst=6977)\
\
NASA\'s Mars rover Curiosity team is working to restore Curiosity\'s
sample-drilling capability using new techniques. The latest development
is a preparatory test on Mars.
