New NASA Insights into the Secret Lives of Plants
=================================================

Date: 2017-11-17 21:11:00

[![Kuskokwim River near McGrath,
Alaska](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20171117/alaska-20171117-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-2017-298&rn=news.xml&rst=7003)\
\
Three NASA missions launching in the next two years will look at
vegetation across our living planet to investigate the role of plants in
Earth\'s carbon and water cycles.
