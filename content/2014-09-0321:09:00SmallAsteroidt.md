Small Asteroid to Safely Pass Close to Earth Sunday
===================================================

Date: 2014-09-03 21:09:00

[![Asteroid 2014 Earth
Flyby](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/asteroid/20140903/asteroid20140903-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-295&rn=news.xml&rst=4277)\
\
A house-sized asteroid will safely fly past Earth this Sunday, Sept. 7.
The asteroid, 2014 RC, is about 60 feet (20 meters) wide.
