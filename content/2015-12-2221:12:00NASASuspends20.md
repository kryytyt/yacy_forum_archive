NASA Suspends 2016 Launch of InSight Mission to Mars
====================================================

Date: 2015-12-22 21:12:00

[![Artist\'s concept depicts NASA\'s InSight Mars
lander](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/insight/20151203/pia19811-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-385&rn=news.xml&rst=4805)\
\
After thorough examination, NASA managers have decided to suspend the
planned March 2016 launch of the Interior Exploration using Seismic
Investigations Geodesy and Heat Transport (InSight) mission.
