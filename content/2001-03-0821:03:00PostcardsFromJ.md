Postcards From Jupiter: New Aurora Details Seen
===============================================

Date: 2001-03-08 21:03:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion
Laboratory](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6236)\
\
Bright auroras on parts of Jupiter where those shimmering glows have not
previously been seen appear in new images taken from NASA\'s Hubble
Space Telescope by University of Michigan scientists.
