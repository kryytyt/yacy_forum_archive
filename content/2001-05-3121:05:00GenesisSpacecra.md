Genesis Spacecraft Arrives in Florida For Launch
================================================

Date: 2001-05-31 21:05:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion
Laboratory](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6199)\
\
NASA\'s Genesis spacecraft, to be launched aboard a Boeing Delta II
vehicle on July 30, arrived today at Florida\'s Kennedy Space Center
from Denver, Colo. aboard a U.S. Air Force C-17 aircraft.
