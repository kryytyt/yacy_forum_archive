Mars 2020 Rover\'s PIXL to Focus X-Rays on Tiny Targets
=======================================================

Date: 2014-07-31 21:07:00

[![Artist\'s concept of the payload for NASA\'s Mars 2020
rover](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/2020/20140731/PIXL-head-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-253&rn=news.xml&rst=4237)\
\
An X-ray instrument for NASA\'s next Mars rover combines a sharpshooting
spectrometer and a camera, to identify and map chemical elements in
targets at microscopic scale.
