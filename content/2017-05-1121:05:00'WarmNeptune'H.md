\'Warm Neptune\' Has Unexpectedly Primitive Atmosphere
======================================================

Date: 2017-05-11 21:05:00

[![Illustration of the atmosphere of the
distant](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/spitzer/20170511/spitzer20170511-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-138&rn=news.xml&rst=6843)\
\
A study combining observations from NASA\'s Hubble and Spitzer space
telescopes reveals that the distant planet HAT-P-26b has a primitive
atmosphere composed almost entirely of hydrogen and helium.
