Congressional Caucus for Women\'s Issues Honors JPL
===================================================

Date: 1988-05-13 21:05:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5819)\
\
The Jet Propulsion Laboratory, Pasadena, Calif., was honored last week
by the Congressional Caucus for Women\'s Issues for the Laboratory\'s
longstanding commitment to making quality child care available to its
employees.
