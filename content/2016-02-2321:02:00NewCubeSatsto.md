New CubeSats to Test Earth Science Tech in Space
================================================

Date: 2016-02-23 21:02:00

[![An illustration of
RainCube](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cubesat/20160223/raincube20160223-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-045&rn=news.xml&rst=5506)\
\
Four CubeSat projects, including two from JPL, will be developed, built
and launched into low-Earth orbit to test technologies that could enable
improve our understanding of Earth.
