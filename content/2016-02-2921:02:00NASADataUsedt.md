NASA Data Used to Track Groundwater in Pakistan
===============================================

Date: 2016-02-29 21:02:00

[![Pakistan water managers used NASA GRACE satellite data to produce
this
map](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/grace/20160229/grace20160229-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-055&rn=news.xml&rst=5584)\
\
Pakistan water managers are using satellite data from NASA\'s Gravity
Recovery and Climate Experiment to more effectively monitor and manage
the nation\'s groundwater.
