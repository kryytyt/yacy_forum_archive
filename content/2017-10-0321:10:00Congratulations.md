Congratulations to LIGO Scientists For Nobel Prize in Physics
=============================================================

Date: 2017-10-03 21:10:00

[![This computer simulation shows the collision of two black holes,
which produces gravitational

The first direct observations of gravitational waves have earned the
Nobel Prize in Physics for three key players in the LIGO collaboration.
