Earth Might Have Hairy Dark Matter
==================================

Date: 2015-11-23 21:11:00

[![This illustration shows Earth surrounded by theoretical filaments of
dark matter
called](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20151120/PIA20176-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-354&rn=news.xml&rst=4774)\
\
Dense filaments of dark matter particles, called \"hairs,\" are
sprouting from Earth, according to a new study based on computer
simulations.
