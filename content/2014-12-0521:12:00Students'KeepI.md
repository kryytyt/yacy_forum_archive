Students \'Keep It Moving\' for Contest at JPL
==============================================

Date: 2014-12-05 21:12:00

[![Crescenta Valley High School and their contraption placed second in
the 2014 JPL Invention
Challenge.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/ic/20141205/ic20141205-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-418&rn=news.xml&rst=4400)\
\
Twenty teams of students from Southern California middle and high
schools, plus seven JPL teams, competed in the JPL Invention Challenge,
called \"Keep It Moving.\"
