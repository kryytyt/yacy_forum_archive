NASA\'s Spitzer Confirms Closest Rocky Exoplanet
================================================

Date: 2015-07-30 21:07:00

[![This artist\'s conception shows the silhouette of a rocky planet,
dubbed HD
219134b](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/spitzer/20150730/pia19831-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-251&rn=news.xml&rst=4672)\
\
Using NASA\'s Spitzer Space Telescope, astronomers have confirmed the
discovery of the nearest rocky planet outside our solar system, larger
than Earth and a potential gold mine of science data.
