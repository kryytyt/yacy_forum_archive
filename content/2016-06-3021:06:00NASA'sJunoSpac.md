NASA\'s Juno Spacecraft Enters Jupiter\'s Magnetic Field
========================================================

Date: 2016-06-30 21:06:00

[![Data Recorded as Juno Crossed Jovian Bow
Shock](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/juno/20160630/PIA20753-226.gif){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-168&rn=news.xml&rst=6550)\
\
NASA\'s Jupiter-bound Juno spacecraft has entered the planet\'s
magnetosphere, where the movement of particles in space is controlled by
what\'s going on inside Jupiter.
