NASA Radio on Europe\'s New Mars Orbiter Aces Relay Test
========================================================

Date: 2016-11-29 21:11:00

[![Graphic depicts the geometry of the relay from Opportunity to the
orbiter](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/20161129/pia21139-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-305&rn=news.xml&rst=6685)\
\
Data from each of the two rovers active on Mars reached Earth last week
in the successful first relay test of a NASA radio aboard Europe\'s new
Trace Gas Orbiter.
