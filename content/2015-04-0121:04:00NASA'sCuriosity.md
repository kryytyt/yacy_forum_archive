NASA\'s Curiosity Eyes Prominent Mineral Veins on Mars
======================================================

Date: 2015-04-01 21:04:00

[![Prominent Veins at \'Garden City\' on Mount Sharp,
Mars](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20150401/pia19161-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-113&rn=news.xml&rst=4536)\
\
NASA\'s Curiosity Mars rover has climbed uphill from an outcrop it
studied for six months and found a site with two-tone mineral veins
forming \"ice-cream sandwich\" ridges.
