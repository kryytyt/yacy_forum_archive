JPL Spokesman Retires
=====================

Date: 1988-01-21 21:01:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5799)\
\
Frank J. Colella, principal spokesman for the Jet Propulsion Laboratory
of the California Institute of Technology for nearly quarter of century,
will retire from his post as manager of JPL\'s Public Affairs Office on
January 31.
