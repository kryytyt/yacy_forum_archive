NASA Radar Spots Relatively Large Asteroid Prior to Flyby
=========================================================

Date: 2017-04-18 21:04:00

[![Radar Imagery of Asteroid 2014
JO25](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/asteroid/20170418/asteroidJO25-20170418-226.jpg){width="100"

with NASA\'s 70-meter antenna at the Goldstone Deep Space Communications
Complex in California.
