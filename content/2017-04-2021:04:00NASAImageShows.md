NASA Image Shows Earth Between the Rings of Saturn
==================================================

Date: 2017-04-20 21:04:00

[![NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft captured this view of planet
Earth.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20170420/PIA21445_figA-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-117&rn=news.xml&rst=6822)\
\
A new image from NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft shows planet Earth as a
point of light between the icy rings of Saturn.
