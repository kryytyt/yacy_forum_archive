Far Northern Permafrost May Unleash Carbon Within Decades
=========================================================

Date: 2018-03-05 21:03:00

[![Tundra polygons on Alaska\'s North
Slope](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20180302/permafrost20180301-226.jpg){width="100"

source of carbon to the atmosphere in 40 to 60 years.
