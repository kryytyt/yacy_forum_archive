NASA Study Challenges Long-held Tsunami Formation Theory
========================================================

Date: 2017-04-26 21:04:00

[![Tsunami waves hitting the coast of Minamisoma in Fukushima
prefecture,
Japan](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20170426/tsunami20170426-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-124&rn=news.xml&rst=6829)\
\
A new NASA study is challenging a long-held theory that tsunamis form
and acquire their energy mostly from vertical movement of the seafloor.
