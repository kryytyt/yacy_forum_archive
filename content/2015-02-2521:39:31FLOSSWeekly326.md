Hosts: [Randal Schwartz](https://twitter.com/merlyn) and [Dan
Lynch](https://twitter.com/methoddan)

This week we are joined by Simon Phipps and Patrick Masson to talk about
the Open Source Initiative (OSI). The Open Source Initiative (OSI) is a
global non-profit that supports and promotes the open source movement.
Among other things, the OSI maintains the Open Source Definition, and a
list of licenses that comply with that definition.

Guests: [Patrick Masson](http://opensource.org/) and [Simon
Phipps](https://twitter.com/webmink)
