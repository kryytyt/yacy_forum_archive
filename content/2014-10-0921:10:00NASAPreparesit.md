NASA Prepares its Science Fleet for Oct. 19 Mars Comet Encounter
================================================================

Date: 2014-10-09 21:10:00

[![Mars Orbiters \'Duck and Cover\' for Comet Siding Spring
Flyby](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/20141009/pia18611-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-349&rn=news.xml&rst=4332)\
\
NASA\'s extensive fleet of science assets, particularly those orbiting
and roving Mars, have front row seats to image and study a
once-in-a-lifetime comet flyby on Sunday, Oct. 19.
