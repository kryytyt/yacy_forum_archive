NASA Asks Scientific Community to Think on Possible Europa Lander Instruments
=============================================================================

Date: 2017-05-17 21:05:00

[![selection of nine instruments for a future Europa
mission](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/europa/20150527/pia00502-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-142&rn=news.xml&rst=6847)\
\
NASA is asking scientists to consider what would be the best instruments
to include on a mission to land on Jupiter\'s icy moon, Europa.
