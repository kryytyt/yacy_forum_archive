NASA Space Telescopes Provide a 3-D Journey Through the Orion Nebula
====================================================================

Date: 2018-01-11 21:01:00

[![This image showcases both the visible and infrared visualizations of
the Orion
Nebula](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/orion/20180111/PIA22089-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2018-004&rn=news.xml&rst=7035)\
\
Data from NASA\'s Spitzer and Hubble space telescopes were combined to
make a three-dimensional, fly-through view of the picturesque Orion
Nebula.
