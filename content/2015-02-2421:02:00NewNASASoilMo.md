New NASA Soil Moisture Mapper Completes Key Milestone
=====================================================

Date: 2015-02-24 21:02:00

[![NASA\'s Soil Moisture Active Passive (SMAP) (Artist
Concept)](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/smap/20150224/smap20150224-226.jpg){width="100"

\
Mission controllers at JPL today sent commands to unfurl the massive
reflector antenna on NASA\'s new Soil Moisture Active Passive (SMAP)
observatory, launched Jan. 31.
