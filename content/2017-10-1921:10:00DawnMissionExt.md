Dawn Mission Extended at Ceres
==============================

Date: 2017-10-19 21:10:00

[![This artist concept shows NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft above dwarf planet
Ceres, as seen in images from the
mission.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20150717/pia19598-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-275&rn=news.xml&rst=6980)\
\
NASA has authorized a second extension of the Dawn mission at Ceres, the
largest object in the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter.
