NASA Rover Game Released for Curiosity\'s Anniversary
=====================================================

Date: 2016-08-04 21:08:00

[![NASA\'s Curiosity Mars rover began close-up investigation of a target
called \'Marimba,\' on lower Mount
Sharp.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20160803/pia20764-226.jpg){width="100"

\
Nearing the fourth anniversary of Curiosity landing on Mars, the rover
works on collecting a 17th sample, while Earthlings can play a new
social media game about Mars exploration.
