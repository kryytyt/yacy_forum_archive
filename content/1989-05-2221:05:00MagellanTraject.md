Magellan Trajectory Correction Maneuver
=======================================

Date: 1989-05-22 21:05:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5852)\
\
The Magellan spacecraft, responding to computer command, Sunday fired
four of its eight 100-pound thrusters to increase its velocity and
slightly alter its trajectory to Venus.
