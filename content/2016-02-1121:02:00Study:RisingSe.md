Study: Rising Seas Slowed by Increasing Water on Land
=====================================================

Date: 2016-02-11 21:02:00

[![Earth\'s land masses have stored increasing amounts of water in the
last decade, slowing the pace of sea level
rise.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/ocean/20160211/earth20160211-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-040&rn=news.xml&rst=5136)\
\
New NASA satellite data have allowed researchers to identify and
quantify, for the first time, how climate-driven increases of liquid
water storage on land have affected sea level rise.
