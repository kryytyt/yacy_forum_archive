Hosts: [Randal Schwartz](https://twitter.com/merlyn) and [Dan
Lynch](https://twitter.com/methoddan)

Evan Powell joins is this week to talk about StackStorm. StackStorm is
an operations automation software that infrastructure and application
environment so you can more easily automate that environment. With
StackStorm you define, and share -- as code -- your operational patterns
from events and triggers through to actions taken in response.

Guests: [Evan Powell](http://stackstorm.com/)
