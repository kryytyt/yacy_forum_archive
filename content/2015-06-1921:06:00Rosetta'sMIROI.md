Rosetta\'s MIRO Instrument Maps Comet Water
===========================================

Date: 2015-06-19 21:06:00

[![Rosetta Comet Marches
On](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/rosetta/20150619/rosetta20150619-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-214&rn=news.xml&rst=4632)\
\
Data from NASA\'s MIRO instrument on board ESA\'s Rosetta spacecraft
have been used to generate maps of the distribution of water in the coma
of 67P/Churyumov-Gerasimenko.
