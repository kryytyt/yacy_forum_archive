NASA\'s Soil Moisture Mapper Takes First \'SMAPshots\'
======================================================

Date: 2015-03-09 20:03:00

[![First image from smap satellite from a test of its science
instruments](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/smap/20150309/smap20150309-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-083&rn=news.xml&rst=4506)\
\
NASA\'s new satellite mission to map the water in the soil under our
feet has successfully tested its science instruments for the first time.
