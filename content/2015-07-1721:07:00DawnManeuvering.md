Dawn Maneuvering to Third Science Orbit
=======================================

Date: 2015-07-17 21:07:00

[![Artist\'s concept of Dawn flying over
Ceres](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20150717/pia19598-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-238&rn=news.xml&rst=4659)\
\
NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft is using its ion propulsion system to descend to
its third mapping orbit at Ceres, and all systems are operating well.
