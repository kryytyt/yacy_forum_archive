> clearly shown, it is ever changing until the instance that it breaks
> untimely. The sun moves, surely this is known, in a single day and
> still its methods are unknown to me. I place my future in your hands,
> please I beg you show me the way. The perception of the vibrations has
> left me. The Goddess is spiteful. The pain will only amplify from now
> on if I continue on this path. I must ask for forgiveness, but how
> does one make an appeal to the Goddess? Please tell me what I must do
> to please her so that she may forgive me!
