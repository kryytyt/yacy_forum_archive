NASA-funded Study Explains Saturn\'s Epic Tantrums
==================================================

Date: 2015-04-13 21:04:00

[![This series of images from NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft shows the
development of a huge
storm](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20150413/pia14905-226.jpg){width="100"

The long-standing mystery of why Saturn seethes with enormous storms
every 30 years may have been solved by scientists working with data from
NASA\'s Cassini mission.
