California \'Rain Debt\' Equal to Average Full Year of Precipitation
====================================================================

Date: 2015-07-30 21:07:00

[![California\'s accumulated
precipitation](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20150730/earth20150730-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-252&rn=news.xml&rst=4673)\
\
A new NASA study has concluded California accumulated a debt of about 20
inches of precipitation between 2012 and 2015.
