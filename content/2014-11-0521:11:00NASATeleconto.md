NASA Telecon to Discuss Mars Comet Flyby Science
================================================

Date: 2014-11-05 21:11:00

[![These images were taken of comet C/2013 A1 Siding Spring by NASA\'s
Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter on Oct. 19,
2014](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20141020/pia18618-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-381&rn=news.xml&rst=4362)\
\
A NASA media telecon on Friday, Nov. 7, will provide initial science
observations of comet C/2013 A1 Siding Spring\'s close flyby of Mars and
the impact on the Martian atmosphere.
