Five Things about NASA\'s SMAP
==============================

Date: 2015-01-22 21:01:00

[![Satellite in a
Can](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/smap/20150122/pia19135-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-028&rn=news.xml&rst=4454)\
\
The Soil Moisture Active Passive (SMAP) mission, scheduled for launch on
Jan. 29, will measure the moisture in Earth\'s soil with greater
accuracy and higher resolution than any preceding mission, producing a
global map of soil moisture every three days.
