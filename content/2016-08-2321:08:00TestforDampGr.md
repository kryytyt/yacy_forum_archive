Test for Damp Ground at Mars\' Seasonal Streaks Finds None
==========================================================

Date: 2016-08-23 21:08:00

[![Sites with Seasonal Streaks on Slopes in Mars
Canyons](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20160707/pia20756-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-215&rn=news.xml&rst=6597)\
\
Seasonal dark streaks on Mars that have become one of the hottest topics
in interplanetary research don\'t hold much water, according to new
findings from a NASA Mars orbiter.
