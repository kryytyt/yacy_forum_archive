Study Maps Hidden Water Pollution in U.S. Coastal Areas
=======================================================

Date: 2016-08-04 21:08:00

[![U.S. coastal areas where freshwater and seawater mix unseen below
ground.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/ocean/20160804/earth20160804b-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-205&rn=news.xml&rst=6587)\
\
A new study finds coastal waters and water supplies along a fifth of
U.S. coasts are vulnerable to pollution from hidden underground water
transfers between oceans and land.
