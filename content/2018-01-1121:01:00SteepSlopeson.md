Steep Slopes on Mars Reveal Structure of Buried Ice
===================================================

Date: 2018-01-11 21:01:00

[![Pit on
Mars](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20180111/PIA22077-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2018-007&rn=news.xml&rst=7038)\
\
Researchers using NASA\'s Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter (MRO) have found
eight sites where thick deposits of ice beneath Mars\' surface are
exposed in faces of eroding slopes.
