Sun Sizzles in High-Energy X-Rays
=================================

Date: 2014-12-22 21:12:00

[![X-rays stream off the
sun](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/nustar/20141222/pia18906-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-440&rn=news.xml&rst=4422)\
\
For the first time, a mission designed to set its eyes on black holes
and other objects far from our solar system has turned its gaze back
closer to home, capturing images of our sun.
