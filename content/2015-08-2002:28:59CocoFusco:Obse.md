> For this performance, Zira shares her observations of human predation
> with a lecture followed by a question and answer session.
>
> Zira is an expert in human behavior. In Ape City she conducted
> experiments on human subjects and dissected their brains. Her
> civilization was designed to avoid the human forms of aggression that
> lead to calamities, and her behavioral studies were key to forging
> that split between those hominids who destroy each other and those who
> would not.
