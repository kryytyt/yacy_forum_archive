Mini NASA Methane Sensor Makes Successful Flight Test
=====================================================

Date: 2016-03-28 21:03:00

[![A JPL mini methane gas sensor is flight tested on a small unmanned
aerial](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20160328/UAVmethane20160328-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-088&rn=news.xml&rst=6192)\
\
As part of a project to improve energy pipeline industry safety,
researchers have successfully flight tested a mini JPL methane gas
sensor on a small unmanned aerial system.
