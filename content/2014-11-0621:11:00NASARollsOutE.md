NASA Rolls Out Enhanced, Mobile-Friendly Climate Site
=====================================================

Date: 2014-11-06 21:11:00

[![NASA\'s Global Climate Change website tracks key indicators of
climate
change](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/climate/20141106/seaice-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-384&rn=news.xml&rst=4365)\
\
NASA has relaunched its Webby Award-winning website, Global Climate
Change, with enhanced interactive features that play on any mobile
device, state-of-the-art visuals, and new sections on climate change
solutions and the people behind the science.
