They\'re Off and Running for NASA Rover Mars Marathon
=====================================================

Date: 2015-04-09 21:04:00

[![Runners at JPL are celebrating the first Martian
marathon](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mer/2015-04-09/marathon040915-16.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-122&rn=news.xml&rst=4544)\
\
Runners at JPL have begun the first of 22 laps in a marathon-length
relay today to celebrate the Opportunity Mars rover\'s achievement of
driving farther than a marathon on Mars.
