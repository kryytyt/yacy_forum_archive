NASA Carbon Counter Reaches Final Orbit, Returns Data
=====================================================

Date: 2014-08-11 21:08:00

[![Artist\'s rendering of NASA\'s Orbiting Carbon Observatory
(OCO)-2](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/oco/20140811/pia17800-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-272&rn=news.xml&rst=4255)\
\
NASA\'s first spacecraft dedicated to studying atmospheric carbon
dioxide has reached its final orbit and returned its first science data.
