Cassini Begins Epic Final Year at Saturn
========================================

Date: 2016-09-15 21:09:00

[![Saturn, Approaching Northern
Summer](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20160915/PIA21046-226.jpg){width="100"

Cassini spacecraft has entered the final year of its epic voyage.
