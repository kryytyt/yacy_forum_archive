NASA Holds Media Opportunities to Discuss Rising Sea Levels
===========================================================

Date: 2015-08-20 21:08:00

[![Rising Sea
Levels](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/ocean/20150820/earth20150820-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-275&rn=news.xml&rst=4696)\
\
In a series of media opportunities Wednesday, Aug. 26, through Friday,
Aug. 28, NASA experts will present an up-to-date global outlook on
current conditions and future projections of sea level rise.
