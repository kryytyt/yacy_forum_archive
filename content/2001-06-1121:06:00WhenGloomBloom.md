When Gloom Blooms in June, Is Catalina Eddy the Reason for the Season?
======================================================================

Date: 2001-06-11 21:06:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion
Laboratory](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6143)\
\
The elusive swirl of breezes called the Catalina Eddy, responsible for
helping cool the Los Angeles basin, is captured in a new animation of
sea-surface winds measured by the SeaWinds instrument on NASA\'s
QuikScat satellite.
