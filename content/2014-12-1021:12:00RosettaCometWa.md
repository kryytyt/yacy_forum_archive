Rosetta Comet Water Different Than Earth Water
==============================================

Date: 2014-12-10 21:12:00

[![This composite is a mosaic comprising four individual NAVCAM images

The composition of the water vapor on the target comet of the European
Space Agency\'s Rosetta mission is significantly different from that
found on Earth.
