Fuel Additive Could Lead to Safer Jet Fuel
==========================================

Date: 2015-10-23 21:10:00

[![This image compares a test with untreated jet fuel and jet fuel
treated with 0.3% polymer developed by the California Institute of
Technology.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/technology/20151001/fire20151007-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-327&rn=news.xml&rst=4729)\
\
A new study describes polymers that could potentially increase the
safety of aviation fuel, both during transportation and in the event of
a collision.
