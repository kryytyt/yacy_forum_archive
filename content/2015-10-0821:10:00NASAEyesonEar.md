NASA Eyes on Earth Aid Response to Carolina Flooding
====================================================

Date: 2015-10-08 21:10:00

[![Relative soil moisture over the Carolinas on Oct. 5, 2015, shown in
percent.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/smap/20151008/sport20151008MAIN-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-314&rn=news.xml&rst=4735)\
\
NASA is using data from Earth-observing satellites in space to aid in
the response to the devastating flooding affecting the Carolinas.
