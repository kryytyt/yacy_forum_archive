A First: NASA Spots Single Methane Leak from Space
==================================================

Date: 2016-06-14 21:06:00

[![Comparison of detected methane plumes over Aliso
Canyon](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20160614/hyperion20160614-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-152&rn=news.xml&rst=6535)\
\
For the first time, an instrument onboard an orbiting spacecraft has
measured the methane emissions from a single, specific leaking facility
on Earth\'s surface.
