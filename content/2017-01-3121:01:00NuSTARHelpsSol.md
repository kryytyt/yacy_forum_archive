NuSTAR Helps Solve \'Rapid Burster\' Mystery
============================================

Date: 2017-01-31 21:01:00

[![The Rapid
Burster](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/nustar/20170131/PIA21418-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-022&rn=news.xml&rst=6732)\
\
Scientists observing a neutron star in the \"Rapid Burster\" system may
have solved a 40-year-old mystery surrounding its puzzling X-ray bursts.
