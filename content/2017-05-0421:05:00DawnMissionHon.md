Dawn Mission Honored by RNASA Foundation
========================================

Date: 2017-05-04 21:05:00

[![Artist\'s concept of the Dawn spacecraft orbiting
Ceres.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20170504/PIA12031-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-132&rn=news.xml&rst=6837)\
\
Members of NASA\'s Dawn mission were honored with awards from the Rotary
National Award for Space Achievement (RNASA) Foundation at a banquet in
Houston on April 28, 2017.
