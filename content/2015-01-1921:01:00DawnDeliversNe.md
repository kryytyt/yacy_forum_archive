Dawn Delivers New Image of Ceres
================================

Date: 2015-01-19 21:01:00

[![The Dawn spacecraft observed Ceres for an hour on Jan. 13,
2015](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20150119/pia19168-226.gif){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-023&rn=news.xml&rst=4449)\
\
As NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft closes in on Ceres, new images show the dwarf
planet at 27 pixels across, about three times better than the
calibration images taken in early December.
