Six CubeSats with JPL Contributions Chosen for SLS Flight
=========================================================

Date: 2016-02-02 21:02:00

[![The Lunar Flashlight, flying as secondary payload on the first flight
of NASA\'s Space Launch
System](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cubesat/20160202/cubesat20160202-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-034&rn=news.xml&rst=4887)\
\
The first flight of NASA\'s new rocket, the Space Launch System (SLS),
will carry 13 low-cost CubeSats to test innovative ideas along with an
uncrewed Orion spacecraft in 2018.
