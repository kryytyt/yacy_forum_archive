New Curiosity \'Safe Mode\' Status Expected to be Brief
=======================================================

Date: 2013-03-18 08:00:00

[![This self-portrait of NASA\'s Mars rover Curiosity combines 66

Mars rover Curiosity to put itself into a precautionary standby status
over the weekend.
