New Study Maps Rate of New Orleans Sinking
==========================================

Date: 2016-05-16 21:05:00

[![Subsidence in Jefferson Parish, Louisiana, from June 2009 to July
2012.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/uavsar/20160516/fig_3_badroof-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-129&rn=news.xml&rst=6513)\
\
New Orleans and surrounding areas continue to sink due to a combination
of natural and human-induced processes, finds a new NASA/university
study using NASA airborne radar.
