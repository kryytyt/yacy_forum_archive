NASA\'s TES Satellite Instrument Gives New Insight into Water Cycle
===================================================================

Date: 2015-09-03 21:09:00

[![A new study clarifies what happens to precipitation that falls on
land.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20150903/earth20150903-226.jpg){width="100"

NASA satellite measurements have given scientists a better understanding
of what happens to rain and snow that fall on land, with a few
surprising findings.
