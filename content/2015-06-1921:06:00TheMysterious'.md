The Mysterious \'Lakes\' on Saturn\'s Moon Titan
================================================

Date: 2015-06-19 21:06:00

[![Radar images from NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft reveal many lakes on
Titan\'s
surface](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20150619/pia17655-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-213&rn=news.xml&rst=4635)\
\
A recent study finds that an Earth-like process may be responsible for
creating depressions resembling lakes in the surface of Saturn\'s moon
Titan.
