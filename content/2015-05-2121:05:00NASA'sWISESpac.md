NASA\'s WISE Spacecraft Discovers Most Luminous Galaxy in Universe
==================================================================

Date: 2015-05-21 21:05:00

[![Dusty \'Sunrise\' at Core of Galaxy (Artist\'s
Concept)](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/wise/20150521/pia19339-226.jpg){width="100"

\
A remote galaxy shining with the light of more than 300 trillion suns
has been discovered using data from NASA\'s Wide-field Infrared Survey
Explorer (WISE).
