Finland\'s Prime Minister to Visit JPL
======================================

Date: 1988-02-12 21:02:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5802)\
\
The Prime Minister of Finland, Harri Holkeri, will tour NASA\'s Jet
Propulsion Laboratory Wednesday, Feb. 24, during two-day visit to the
Los Angeles area.
