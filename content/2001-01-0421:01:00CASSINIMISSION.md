CASSINI MISSION STATUS
======================

Date: 2001-01-04 21:01:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion
Laboratory](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6140)\
\
NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft has continued collecting new scientific
information from Jupiter\'s environs every day since making its closest
approach to the giant planet on Dec. 30, 2000, and is scheduled to keep
studying the Jupiter system for another three months while proceeding on
toward Saturn.
