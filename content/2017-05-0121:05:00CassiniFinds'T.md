Cassini Finds \'The Big Empty\' Close to Saturn
===============================================

Date: 2017-05-01 21:05:00

[![Illustration: Cassini flys by
Saturn](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20170501/CassiniDive-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-127&rn=news.xml&rst=6832)\
\
As Cassini prepares for its second dive between Saturn and its rings,
engineers are pleased that data from the mission\'s April 26 dive show
the region is largely dust-free.
