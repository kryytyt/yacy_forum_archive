NASA InSight Mission to Mars Arrives at Launch Site
===================================================

Date: 2018-02-28 21:02:00

[![Personnel supporting NASA\'s InSight mission to Mars load the crated

\
NASA\'s InSight spacecraft has arrived at Vandenberg Air Force Base in
central California to begin final preparations for a launch this May.
