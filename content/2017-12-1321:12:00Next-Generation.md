Next-Generation GRACE Satellites Arrive at Launch Site
======================================================

Date: 2017-12-13 21:12:00

[![A crate containing one of the twin Gravity Recovery and Climate
Experiment Follow-On (GRACE-FO)
satellites](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/gracefo/20171213/grace20171213-226.jpg){width="100"

A pair of U.S./German Earth research satellites with some very big shoes
to fill is now at California \'s Vandenberg Air Force Base to begin
final preparations for launch.
