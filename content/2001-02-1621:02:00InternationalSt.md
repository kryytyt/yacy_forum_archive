International Students Make History Imaging Martian Terrain
===========================================================

Date: 2001-02-16 21:02:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion
Laboratory](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6390)\
\
Nine budding scientists can all pat themselves on the back, having
become the first-ever elementary and high school students to direct a
camera on NASA\'s Mars Global Surveyor spacecraft and image Martian
sites.
