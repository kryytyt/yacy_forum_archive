NASA Rover Finds Active and Ancient Organic Chemistry on Mars
=============================================================

Date: 2014-12-16 21:12:00

[![\'Cumberland\' Target Drilled by
Curiosity](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20141216/pia16936-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-432&rn=news.xml&rst=4413)\
\
NASA\'s Mars Curiosity rover has measured a tenfold spike in methane, an
organic chemical, in the atmosphere around it and detected other organic
molecules in a rock-powder sample collected by the robotic laboratory\'s
drill.
