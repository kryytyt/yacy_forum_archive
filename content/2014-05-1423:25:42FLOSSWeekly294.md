The Digital Public Library of America brings together the riches of
America's libraries, archives, and museums, and makes them freely
available to the world.

Mark A. Matienzo is the Director of Technology for the Digital Public
Library of America, Mark Breedlove is the Technical Specialist for the
Digital Public Library of America.
