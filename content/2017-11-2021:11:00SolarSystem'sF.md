Solar System\'s First Interstellar Visitor Dazzles Scientists
=============================================================

Date: 2017-11-20 21:11:00

[![Artist\'s concept of interstellar asteroid 1I/2017
U1](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/asteroid/20171120/eso1737a-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-300&rn=news.xml&rst=7006)\
\
New data reveal first detected interstellar object to be a rocky, and up
to one-quarter mile (400 meters) long and highly-elongated-perhaps 10
times as long as it is wide.
