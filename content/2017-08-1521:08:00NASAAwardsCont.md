NASA Awards Contract to Extend Operations of JPL
================================================

Date: 2017-08-15 21:08:00

[![](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/jpl/20170815/JPL_lab_daytime-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-218&rn=news.xml&rst=6923)\
\
NASA has awarded a contract to the California Institute of Technology
(Caltech) in Pasadena, California, to extend operations of the agency\'s
Jet Propulsion Laboratory, also in Pasadena, through Sept. 30, 2018.
