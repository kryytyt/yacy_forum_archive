Watch Martian Clouds Scoot, Thanks to NASA\'s Curiosity
=======================================================

Date: 2017-08-09 21:08:00

[![Clouds drift across the sky above a Martian
horizon](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20170809/PIA21841-226.gif){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-211&rn=news.xml&rst=6917)\
\
Wispy, early-season clouds resembling Earth\'s ice-crystal cirrus clouds
move across the Martian sky in some new image sequences from NASA\'s
Curiosity Mars rover.
