Kepler Wins National Air and Space Museum Trophy
================================================

Date: 2015-03-23 20:03:00

[![The artistic concept shows NASA\'s planet-hunting Kepler spacecraft
operating in a new mission profile called

\
The team in charge of NASA\'s Kepler mission will receive the
Smithsonian National Air and Space Museum\'s highest group honor at a
ceremony in Washington on March 25.
