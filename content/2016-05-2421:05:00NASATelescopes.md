NASA Telescopes Find Clues For How Giant Black Holes Formed So Quickly
======================================================================

Date: 2016-05-24 21:05:00

[![This illustration depicts a possible \'seed\' for the formation of a
supermassive black
hole.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/spitzer/20160524/spitzer20160524-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-133&rn=news.xml&rst=6517)\
\
Using data from NASA\'s Great Observatories, astronomers have found the
best evidence yet for cosmic seeds in the early universe that should
grow into supermassive black holes.
