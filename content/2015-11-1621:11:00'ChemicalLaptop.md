\'Chemical Laptop\' Could Search for Signs of Life Outside Earth
================================================================

Date: 2015-11-16 21:11:00

[![JPL researchers Jessica Creamer, Fernanda Mora and Peter Willis (left

acids and fatty acids, with potential applications in space.
