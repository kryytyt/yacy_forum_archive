For Moratorium on Sending Commands to Mars, Blame the Sun
=========================================================

Date: 2017-07-14 21:07:00

[![Geometry of Mars Solar
Conjunction](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/20150603/PIA16811b-226.jpg){width="100"

This month, movements of the planets will put Mars almost directly
behind the sun, from Earth\'s perspective, causing curtailed
communications between Earth and Mars.
