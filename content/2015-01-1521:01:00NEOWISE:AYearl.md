NEOWISE: A Yearlong Look at the Sky
===================================

Date: 2015-01-15 21:01:00

[![One Year of NEOWISE Observations
Mapped](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/wise/20150114/pia19101-226.jpg){width="100"

objects in one year and observed many others, including a comet that has
become this month\'s brightest.
