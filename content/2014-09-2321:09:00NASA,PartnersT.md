NASA, Partners Target Megacities Carbon Emissions
=================================================

Date: 2014-09-23 21:09:00

[![Artist\'s concept of the LA Megacities Carbon Project observing

in an international initiative to monitor the greenhouse gas emissions
of Earth\'s most populous cities.
