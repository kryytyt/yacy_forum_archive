NASA Mission Points to Origin of \'Ocean of Storms\' on Earth\'s Moon
=====================================================================

Date: 2014-10-01 21:10:00

[![A view of Earth\'s moon looking south across Oceanus
Procellarum](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/grail/20141001/grail20141001-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-333&rn=news.xml&rst=4317)\
\
Using data from NASA\'s Gravity Recovery and Interior Laboratory
(GRAIL), mission scientists have solved a lunar mystery almost as old as
the moon itself.
