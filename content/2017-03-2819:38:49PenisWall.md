Penis Wall
==========

Date: 2017-03-28 19:38:49

[81 erectable penises that respond to a viewer\'s
movements:](https://www.vimeo.com/140890989)

> The Penis Wall is made up of 3D printed penises that each have six
> segments driven by servo motors. Equipped with an ultrasonic distance
> sensor, each unit can respond to a viewer\'s movements. Moreover, the
> Penis Wall can also be used as a display to represent data, for
> instance, fluctuations in the stock market.
