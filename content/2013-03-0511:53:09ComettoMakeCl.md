Comet to Make Close Flyby of Red Planet in October 2014
=======================================================

Date: 2013-03-05 11:53:09

[![This computer graphic depicts the orbit of comet 2013 A1 (Siding

miles (300,000 kilometers) of Mars on Oct. 19, 2014.
