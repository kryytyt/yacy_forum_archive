NASA: Alaska Shows No Signs of Rising Arctic Methane
====================================================

Date: 2014-11-12 21:11:00

[![This photo taken during the CARVE experiment shows polygonal lakes
created by melting permafrost on Alaska\'s North
Slope.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/alaska/20141113/earth20141113-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-395&rn=news.xml&rst=4376)\
\
Despite recent Arctic warming, a NASA analysis finds that Alaskan soils
are not releasing methane at high rates. Changes in this region have not
yet affected global methane.
