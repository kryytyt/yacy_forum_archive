NuSTAR Spots Temperature Swings of Black Hole Winds
===================================================

Date: 2017-03-01 21:03:00

[![This artist\'s concept illustrates a supermassive black
hole.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/nustar/20170301/nustar20170301-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-055&rn=news.xml&rst=6762)\
\
For the first time, scientists have measured rapidly varying
temperatures in hot gas emanating from around a black hole.
