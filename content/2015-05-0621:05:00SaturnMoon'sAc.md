Saturn Moon\'s Activity Could Be \'Curtain Eruptions\'
======================================================

Date: 2015-05-06 21:05:00

[![Recent research suggests much of the eruption activity on the surface
of Saturn\'s moon
Enceladus](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20150506/pia19061a-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-154&rn=news.xml&rst=4575)\
\
New research using data from NASA\'s Cassini mission suggests most of
the eruptions from Saturn\'s moon Enceladus might be diffuse curtains
rather than discrete jets.
