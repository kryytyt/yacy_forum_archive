Remaining Martian Atmosphere Still Dynamic
==========================================

Date: 2013-04-08 21:04:00

[![This image shows the first holes into rock drilled by NASA\'s Mars

quite active, recent findings from NASA\'s Mars rover Curiosity
indicate.
