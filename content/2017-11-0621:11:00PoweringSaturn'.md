Powering Saturn\'s Active Ocean Moon
====================================

Date: 2017-11-06 21:11:00

[![A dramatic plume sprays water ice and vapor from the south polar
region of Saturn\'s moon
Enceladus.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20170216/PIA11688-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-290&rn=news.xml&rst=6995)\
\
A new study finds heat from friction could power hydrothermal activity
on Saturn\'s moon Enceladus for billions of years if the moon has a
highly porous core.
