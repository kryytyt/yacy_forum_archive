Viking 2 Oven Failed
====================

Date: 1976-01-23 21:01:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6042)\

One of three small ovens on an experiment aboard NASA\'s Viking 2 Lander
apparently did not operate during recent checkout tests of the
instrument.
