Mars Canyons Study Adds Clues about Possible Water
==================================================

Date: 2016-07-07 21:07:00

[![Sites with Seasonal Streaks on Slopes in Mars
Canyons](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20160707/pia20756-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-178&rn=news.xml&rst=6562)\
\
Puzzles persist about possible water at seasonally dark streaks on
Martian slopes, according to a new study of thousands of such features
in the Red Planet\'s largest canyon system.
