NASA, Microsoft Collaborate to Bring Science Fiction to Science Fact
====================================================================

Date: 2015-06-25 21:06:00

[![NASA and Microsoft engineers test Project Sidekick on NASA\'s
Weightless Wonder C9
jet.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20150625b/sidekick-226.jpg){width="100"

NASA and Microsoft are teaming up to develop Sidekick, a new project
using commercial technology to empower astronauts aboard the
International Space Station (ISS).
