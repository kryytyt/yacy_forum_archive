NASA Airborne Science Team Surveys California Fires
===================================================

Date: 2017-12-08 21:12:00

[![Ventura coastline is barely visible under a plume of
smoke](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20171208/aviris20171208-226.jpg){width="100"

NASA scientists are using a high-altitude aircraft and sophisticated JPL
imaging spectrometer to study environmental impacts from Southern
California\'s devastating wildfires.
