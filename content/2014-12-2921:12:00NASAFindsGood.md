NASA Finds Good News on Forests and Carbon Dioxide
==================================================

Date: 2014-12-29 21:12:00

[![Image of a tropical
forest](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20141229/tropicalforest-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-442&rn=news.xml&rst=4424)\
\
A NASA-led study suggests that tropical forests absorb more carbon
dioxide than scientists thought. That means, if left undisturbed, the
tropical trees should be able to continue reducing the rate of global
warming.
