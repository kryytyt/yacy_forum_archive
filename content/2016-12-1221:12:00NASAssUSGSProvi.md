NASA/USGS Provide Global View of Speed of Ice
=============================================

Date: 2016-12-12 21:12:00

[![Texture on the surface of flowing
ice](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/climate/20161212/earth20161212-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-316&rn=news.xml&rst=6698)\
\
Glaciers and ice sheets move in unique patterns, as evidenced by a new
capability that uses satellites to map ice flow speeds in Greenland,
Antarctica and mountain ranges.
