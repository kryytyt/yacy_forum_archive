> It\'s that time of year once again when couples are forced to spend
> lots of money on each other while the single folk get to laugh
> manically whilst eating alone in their dark, empty studio apartments.
> (More of a laugh-cry, really.) In either case, Valentine\'s Day is
> clearly a holiday for losers.
>
> To help make the day a little more bearable, I\'ve whipped up some
> free valentines for you to share, email, print out, burn, and curse.
