NASA Finds Methane Ice Cloud in Titan\'s Stratosphere
=====================================================

Date: 2014-10-24 21:10:00

[![This cloud in the stratosphere over the north pole of Titan is
similar to Earth\'s polar stratospheric
clouds.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20141024/pia09171-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-372&rn=news.xml&rst=4354)\
\
NASA scientists have identified an unexpected ice cloud on Saturn\'s
moon Titan that is similar to exotic clouds found far above Earth\'s
poles.
