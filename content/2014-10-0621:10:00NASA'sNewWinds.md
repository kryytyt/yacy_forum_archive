NASA\'s New Winds Mission Installed, Gathers First Data
=======================================================

Date: 2014-10-06 21:10:00

[![Launched Sept. 21, 2014, to the International Space
Station](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/rapidscat/20141006/pia18824-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-342&rn=news.xml&rst=4325)\
\
NASA\'s newest Earth observing mission, ISS-RapidScat, is collecting its
first science data on ocean wind speeds and direction following its
successful installation on the International Space Station.
