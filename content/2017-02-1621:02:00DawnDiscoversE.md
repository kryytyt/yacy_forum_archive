Dawn Discovers Evidence for Organic Material on Ceres
=====================================================

Date: 2017-02-16 21:02:00

[![This enhanced color composite image, made with data from the framing
camera aboard NASA\'s Dawn
spacecraft](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20170216/PIA21419-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-042&rn=news.xml&rst=6751)\
\
NASA\'s Dawn mission has found evidence for organic material on Ceres, a
dwarf planet and the largest body in the main asteroid belt between Mars
and Jupiter.
