JPL Technology Inducted Into U.S. Space Foundation Hall of Fame
===============================================================

Date: 2001-04-09 21:04:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion
Laboratory](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6395)\
\
An advanced sensor developed at NASA\'s Jet Propulsion Laboratory,
Pasadena, Calif., will be inducted into the U.S. Space Foundation Hall
of Fame on April 12 in recognition of its potential uses in medicine,
firefighting and industry, as well as astronomy.
