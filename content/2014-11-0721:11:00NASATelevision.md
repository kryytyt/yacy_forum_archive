NASA Television to Provide Coverage of European Mission Comet Landing
=====================================================================

Date: 2014-11-07 21:11:00

[![Philae\'s descent and science of the
surface](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/rosetta/20141107/rosetta20141107-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-386&rn=news.xml&rst=4367)\
\
NASA Television and the agency\'s website will provide live coverage
from 6-8:30 a.m. PST (9-11:30 a.m. EST) of the European Space Agency
(ESA) Rosetta mission\'s scheduled landing of a probe on a comet on
Wednesday, Nov. 12.
