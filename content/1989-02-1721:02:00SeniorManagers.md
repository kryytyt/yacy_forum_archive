Senior Managers Elected to National Academy of Engineering,
===========================================================

Date: 1989-02-17 21:02:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"

\
Two senior managers at the Jet Propulsion Laboratory have been elected
to membership in the National Academy of Engineering, it has been
announced.
