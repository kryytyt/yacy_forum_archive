nur miteinander in einem geschlossenen Netzwerk communizieren anstatt

I think this could work:\
\
Replace Yacy\'s list of seednodes with only the local IPs of the
computers in your network.\
\
If your Yacy install is fresh and has not collected any other peers yet,
then you should have the desired effect. Your 3 local Yacy clients will
only communicate with each other because they don\'t know anyone else
yet and won\'t be able to find anyone else.
