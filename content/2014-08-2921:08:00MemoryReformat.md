Memory Reformat Planned for Opportunity Mars Rover
==================================================

Date: 2014-08-29 21:08:00

[![Opportunity\'s Rear-Facing View Ahead After a
Drive](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mer/2014-08-29/pia18598-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-292&rn=news.xml&rst=4275)\
\
An increasing frequency of computer resets on NASA\'s Mars Exploration
Rover Opportunity has prompted the rover team to make plans to reformat
the rover\'s flash memory.
