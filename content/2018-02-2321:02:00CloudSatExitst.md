CloudSat Exits the \'A-Train\'
==============================

Date: 2018-02-23 21:02:00

[![Artist\'s illustration of NASA\'s Cloudsat
satellite](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cloudsat/20180223/PIA03012-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2018-038&rn=news.xml&rst=7068)\
\
NASA\'s nearly 12-year-old CloudSat satellite has exited the Afternoon
Constellation, or A-Train, of Earth monitoring satellites, but its
science mission will continue.
