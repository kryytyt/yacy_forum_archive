NASA to Host News Conference on Discovery Beyond Our Solar System
=================================================================

Date: 2017-02-20 21:02:00

[![Artist\'s illustration of an
exoplanet](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/spitzer/20170220/kepler452b2-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-044&rn=news.xml&rst=6753)\
\
NASA will hold a news conference at 10 a.m. PST (1 p.m. EST) Wednesday,
Feb. 22, to present new findings on planets that orbit stars other than
our sun, known as exoplanets.
