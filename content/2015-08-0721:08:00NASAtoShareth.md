NASA to Share the Universe with Tumblr Users
============================================

Date: 2015-08-07 21:08:00

[![NASA Tumblr
ad](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20150807/tumblr20150807-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-262&rn=news.xml&rst=4682)\
\
NASA is launching an official Tumblr profile that will give Tumblr users
a regular dose of space in a blog-like format through text, photos,
videos and more.
