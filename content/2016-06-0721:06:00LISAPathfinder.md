LISA Pathfinder Mission Paves Way for Space-based Detection of Gravitational Waves
==================================================================================

Date: 2016-06-07 21:06:00

[![The LISA Pathfinder spacecraft will help pave the way for a mission
to detect gravitational
waves.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/lisa/20160121/lisa20160121c-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-143&rn=news.xml&rst=6526)\
\
LISA Pathfinder, a mission led by the European Space Agency (ESA) with
contributions from NASA, has successfully tested a key technology needed
to build a space-based observatory for detecting gravitational waves.
