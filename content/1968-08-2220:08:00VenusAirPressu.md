Venus Air Pressure
==================

Date: 1968-08-22 20:08:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6100)\
\
The surface air pressure on the planet Venus may be 75 or 100 times that
on Earth\--or four to five times greater than the Venus pressure
reported recently by Soviet scientists\--Jet Propulsion Laboratory
researchers have revealed.
