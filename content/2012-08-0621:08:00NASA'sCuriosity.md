NASA\'s Curiosity Rover Caught in the Act of Landing
====================================================

Date: 2012-08-06 21:08:00

[![NASA\'s Curiosity rover and its parachute were spotted by NASA\'s
Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter as Curiosity descended to the surface on
Aug. 5 PDT (Aug. 6
EDT).](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20120806/PIA15978-th.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.cfm?release=2012-232&rn=news.xml&rst=3461)\
\
An image from the High Resolution Imaging Science Experiment (HiRISE)
camera aboard NASA\'s Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter captured the Curiosity
rover still connected to its 51-foot-wide (almost 16 meter) parachute as
it descended towards its landing site at Gale Crater.
