West Antarctic Melt Rate Has Tripled: NASA-UC Irvine
====================================================

Date: 2014-12-02 21:12:00

[![Glaciers seen during NASA\'s Operation IceBridge research flight to
West Antarctica on Oct. 29,
2014.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/antarctica/20141202/earth20141201-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-412&rn=news.xml&rst=4393)\
\
A comprehensive, 21-year analysis of the fastest-melting region of
Antarctica has found that the melt rate of glaciers there has tripled
during the last decade.
