NASA Confirms Evidence That Liquid Water Flows on Today\'s Mars
===============================================================

Date: 2015-09-28 21:09:00

[![Martian
slopes](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20150928/pia19916-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-302&rn=news.xml&rst=4722)\
\
New findings from NASA\'s Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter (MRO) provide the
strongest evidence yet that liquid water flows intermittently on
present-day Mars.
