Ice in Ceres\' Shadowed Craters Linked to Tilt History
======================================================

Date: 2017-03-22 20:03:00

[![Ceres](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20170322/PIA21469-226.gif){width="100"

regions, research from NASA\'s Dawn mission suggests.
