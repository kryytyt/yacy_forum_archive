[RIP Basil
Gogos.](http://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3459254/r-p-legendary-monster-artist-basil-gogos/)

> Some of the most iconic pieces of classic monster art were found on
> the front covers of Famous Monsters of Filmland magazine throughout
> the \'60s and \'70s, that art no doubt responsible for countless
> monster kids being bitten by the proverbial bug. Vibrant and
> eye-catching, the magazine\'s cover art made horror stylish, beautiful
> and cool.
>
> Those paintings were the work of illustrator Basil Gogos, who we\'re
> sad to report is the latest in a long line of true horror legends who
> have recently left us.
