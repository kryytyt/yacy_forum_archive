Europa\'s Ocean May Have An Earthlike Chemical Balance
======================================================

Date: 2016-05-17 21:05:00

[![Europa\'s varied surface
features](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/europa/20160517/PIA20028-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-231&rn=news.xml&rst=6514)\
\
A NASA modeling study suggests the necessary balance of chemical energy
for life could exist in the ocean of Jupiter\'s moon Europa, even
without volcanic hydrothermal activity.
