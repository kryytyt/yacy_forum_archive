Dr. King Appointed Deputy Assistant Laboratory Director
=======================================================

Date: 1988-01-04 09:00:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5797)\
\
Dr. James King Jr. has been appointed Deputy Assistant Laboratory
Director, Office of Technical Divisions of NASA\'s Jet Propulsion
Laboratory, JPL Director Dr. Lew Allen has announced.
