NASA\'s Spitzer Spots Planet Deep Within Our Galaxy
===================================================

Date: 2015-04-14 21:04:00

[![Map of Exoplanets Found in Our
Galaxy](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/spitzer/20150414/pia19333-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-128&rn=news.xml&rst=4550)\
\
NASA\'s Spitzer Space Telescope has teamed up with a telescope on the
ground to find a remote gas planet about 13,000 light-years away, making
it one of the most distant planets known.
