Mars Orbiter Views Curiosity Rover in \'Artist\'s Drive\'
=========================================================

Date: 2015-04-22 21:04:00

[![Mars Orbiter Sees Curiosity Rover in \'Artist\'s
Drive\'](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20150422/pia19392-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-141&rn=news.xml&rst=4563)\
\
A view from NASA\'s Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter on April 8, 2015,
catches sight of NASA\'s Curiosity Mars rover passing through a valley
called \"Artist\'s Drive\" on the lower slope of Mount Sharp.
