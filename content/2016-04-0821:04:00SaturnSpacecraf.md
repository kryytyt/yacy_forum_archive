Saturn Spacecraft Not Affected by Hypothetical Planet 9
=======================================================

Date: 2016-04-08 21:04:00

[![Cassini project manager Earl Maize reviews post-maneuver
data.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20160408/PIA11141-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-101&rn=news.xml&rst=6200)\
\
Contrary to recent reports, NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft is not
experiencing unexplained deviations in its orbit around Saturn,
according to mission managers and orbit determination experts at NASA\'s
Jet Propulsion Laboratory in Pasadena, California.
