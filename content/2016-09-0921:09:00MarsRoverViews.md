Mars Rover Views Spectacular Layered Rock Formations
====================================================

Date: 2016-09-09 21:09:00

[![Mars](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20160909/PIA21044-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-236&rn=news.xml&rst=6618)\
\
The layered geologic past of Mars is revealed in stunning detail in new
color images returned by NASA\'s Curiosity Mars rover of the \"Murray
Buttes\" region on Mount Sharp.
