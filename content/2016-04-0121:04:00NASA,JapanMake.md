NASA, Japan Make ASTER Earth Data Available At No Cost
======================================================

Date: 2016-04-01 21:04:00

[![In March 2016, ASTER captured the eruption of Nicaragua\'s Momotombo
volcano with its visible and thermal infrared
bands.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/aster/20160401/PIA20475-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-093&rn=news.xml&rst=6253)\
\
Beginning today, all Earth imagery from a prolific Japanese remote
sensing instrument on NASA\'s Terra spacecraft since 1999 is now
available to users everywhere at no cost.
