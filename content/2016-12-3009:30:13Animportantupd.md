An important update from the International Earth Rotation Service
=================================================================

Date: 2016-12-30 09:30:13

[![](https://www.jwz.org/images/leap.jpg)](https://datacenter.iers.org/eop/-/somos/5Rgv/latest/16)[**Bulletin
C 52:**\
To: authorities responsible for the measurement and distribution of
time](https://datacenter.iers.org/eop/-/somos/5Rgv/latest/16)

> A positive leap second will be introduced at the end of December 2016.
> The sequence of dates of the UTC second markers will be:
>
> ::: {style="margin-left: 1em"}
> `2016 December 31, 23h 59m 59s 2016 December 31, 23h 59m 60s 2017 January  1,  0h  0m  0s`
> :::
>
> The difference between UTC and the International Atomic Time TAI is:
>
> ::: {style="margin-left: 1em"}
> ` from 2015 July 1, 0h UTC, to 2017 January 1 0h UTC: UTC-TAI = - 36s from 2017 January 1, 0h UTC, until further notice : UTC-TAI = - 37s`
> :::

This means that tomorrow, 3:59:59 PM PST will be followed by 3:59:60 PM
PST prior to the advent of 4:00:00 PM PST.

The leap second\'s stay of execution has been [extended until at least
2023.](http://www.nature.com/news/leap-second-decision-delayed-by-eight-years-1.18855)
Whew!
