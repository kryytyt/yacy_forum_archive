Newest NASA Mars Orbiter Demonstrates Relay Prowess
===================================================

Date: 2014-11-10 21:11:00

[![Image Relayed by MAVEN Mars Orbiter from Curiosity Mars
Rover](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/maven/20141110/pia18866-226.jpg){width="100"

The relay radio on NASA\'s MAVEN spacecraft has completed a successful
test of receiving Mars rover data, which MAVEN then forwarded to Earth.
