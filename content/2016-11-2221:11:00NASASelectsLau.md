NASA Selects Launch Services for Global Surface Water Survey Mission
====================================================================

Date: 2016-11-22 21:11:00

[![Artist\'s rendering of the Surface Water and Ocean Topography (SWOT)
spacecraft.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/ocean/20161122/earth20161122-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-302&rn=news.xml&rst=6682)\
\
NASA has selected SpaceX of Hawthorne, California, to provide launch
services for the agency\'s Surface Water and Ocean Topography (SWOT)
mission.
