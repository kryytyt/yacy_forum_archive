Cassini Makes its \'Goodbye Kiss\' Flyby of Titan
=================================================

Date: 2017-09-11 21:09:00

[![Cassini made its final, distant flyby of Saturn\'s moon Titan on

NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft is headed toward its Sept. 15 plunge into
Saturn, following a final, distant flyby of the planet\'s giant moon
Titan.
