**Hosts:** [Dan Lynch](http://danlynch.org) and Guillermo Amaral.

RStudio today develops free and open tools for the R community. These
include the RStudio development environment as well as the shiny, ggvis,
and dplyr packages. RStudio added enterprise-ready professional products
to make it easier for teams to scale RStudio deployments and share work.
