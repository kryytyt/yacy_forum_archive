Dawn Snaps Its Best-Yet Image of Dwarf Planet Ceres
===================================================

Date: 2014-12-05 21:12:00

[![Enhanced Early View of Ceres from
Dawn](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20141202/pia19049b-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-414&rn=news.xml&rst=4395)\
\
The Dawn spacecraft has delivered a glimpse of Ceres, the largest body
in the main asteroid belt, in a new image taken 740,000 miles (1.2
million kilometers) from the dwarf planet.
