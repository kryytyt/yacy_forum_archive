NASA to Launch Fleet of Hurricane-Tracking SmallSats
====================================================

Date: 2016-11-10 21:11:00

[![Artist\'s concept of one of the eight Cyclone Global Navigation
Satellite System
satellites](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20161110/cygnss-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-293&rn=news.xml&rst=6674)\
\
NASA is set to launch its first Earth science small-satellite
constellation, which will help improve hurricane intensity, track and
storm surge forecasts, on Dec. 12.
