Bad Hair, Incorrect Feathering, and Missing Skin Flaps
======================================================

Date: 2017-09-24 05:17:45

[C.M.
Kosemen:](http://www.atlasobscura.com/articles/dinosaurs-art-paleoartists-mistakes)

> Our world is full of unique animals that have squat fatty bodies, with
> all kinds of soft tissue features that are unlikely to have survived
> in fossils, such as pouches, wattles, or skin flaps. \"\[\...\] \"The
> biggest thing is teeth and facial fat. Readers have to be aware that
> all dinosaurs they see in all media, and especially in popular
> culture, seem to have their heads flensed. They\'ve always got these
> weird grins with only the teeth visible.\" As he points out, most
> animals have lips and gums and lumps of facial fat that change the
> profile of the head, and cover the teeth.
