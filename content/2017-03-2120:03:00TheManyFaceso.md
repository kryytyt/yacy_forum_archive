The Many Faces of Rosetta\'s Comet 67P
======================================

Date: 2017-03-21 20:03:00

[![A 100 foot-wide (30 meter), 28-million-pound (12.8-million-kilogram)
boulder.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/rosetta/20170321/PIA21565-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-080&rn=news.xml&rst=6786)\
\
Images from ESA Rosetta indicate that the surface of its target comet
has had growing fractures, collapsing cliffs and massive rolling
boulders.
