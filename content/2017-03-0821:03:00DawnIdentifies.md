Dawn Identifies Age of Ceres\' Brightest Area
=============================================

Date: 2017-03-08 21:03:00

[![Center of Occator Crater (Enhanced
Color)](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20160322/PIA20355-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-059&rn=news.xml&rst=6766)\
\
The bright central area of Ceres\' Occator Crater, known as Cerealia
Facula, is approximately 30 million years younger than the crater in
which it lies, according to a new study in the Astronomical Journal.
