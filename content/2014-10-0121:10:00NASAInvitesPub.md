NASA Invites Public to Join \#SkyScience Cloud Study
====================================================

Date: 2014-10-01 21:10:00

[![NASA is inviting people everywhere to become cloud-studying citizen
scientists](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/clouds/20141001/earth20141001-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-332&rn=news.xml&rst=4316)\
\
NASA is inviting people around the globe to step outside during Earth
Science Week, Oct. 12-18, observe the sky and share their observations
as citizen scientists.
