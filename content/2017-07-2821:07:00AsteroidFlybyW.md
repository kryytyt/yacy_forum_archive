Asteroid Flyby Will Benefit NASA Detection and Tracking Network
===============================================================

Date: 2017-07-28 21:07:00

[![Animation of asteroid 2012
TC4](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/asteroid/20170728/asteroid-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-201&rn=news.xml&rst=6906)\
\
Scientists plan to use a small asteroid\'s close approach to Earth this
October to test NASA\'s network of observatories and scientists who work
with planetary defense.
