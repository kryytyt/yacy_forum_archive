Cassini Catches Titan Naked in the Solar Wind
=============================================

Date: 2015-01-28 21:01:00

[![Titan Observed Naked in the Solar
Wind](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20150126/pia19055-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-038&rn=news.xml&rst=4460)\
\
Researchers studying data from NASA\'s Cassini mission have observed
that Saturn\'s largest moon, Titan, behaves much like Venus, Mars or a
comet when exposed to the raw power of the solar wind.
