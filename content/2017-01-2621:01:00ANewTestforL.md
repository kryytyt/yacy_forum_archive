A New Test for Life on Other Planets
====================================

Date: 2017-01-26 21:01:00

[![Mono Lake,
California](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/technology/20170126/chemistry20170126-226.jpg){width="100"

On an otherworldly landscape in Mono Lake, California, scientists have
tested a new method for potentially detecting chemical signatures of
life on another planet.
