Associate Director for Institutional Affairs Retires
====================================================

Date: 1987-05-21 21:05:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5770)\
\
Fred H. Felberg, JPL\'s associate director for institutional affairs,
will retire July 31, 1987, JPL Director Dr. Lew Allen has announced.
