NASA Mission Provides Its First Look at Martian Upper Atmosphere
================================================================

Date: 2014-10-14 21:10:00

[![Ultraviolet Views of Martian
Atmosphere](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/maven/20141014/maven20141014-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-351&rn=news.xml&rst=4334)\
\
NASA\'s MAVEN spacecraft orbiting Mars sees a solar-particle storm, gets
ultraviolet images of tenuous oxygen, hydrogen and carbon coronas, and
yields a map of variable ozone.
