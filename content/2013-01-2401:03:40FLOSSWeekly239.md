Concrete5 is an easy to edit CMS designed to serve the relationship
between site developers and site owners. Concrete5 makes running a
website easy. Go to any page in your site, and a editing toolbar gives
you all the controls you need to update your website. No intimidating
manuals, no complicated administration interfaces - just point and
click.

Hosts: [Randal Schwartz](http://www.stonehenge.com/merlyn/) and [Simon
Phipps](http://webmink.com).
