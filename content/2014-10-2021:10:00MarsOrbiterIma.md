Mars Orbiter Image Shows Comet Nucleus is Small
===============================================

Date: 2014-10-20 21:10:00

[![These images were taken of comet C/2013 A1 Siding Spring by NASA\'s
Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter on Oct. 19,
2014](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20141020/pia18618-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-366&rn=news.xml&rst=4349)\
\
A camera on NASA\'s Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter has taken the
highest-resolution image ever made of an Oort Cloud comet, taking
advantage of the comet\'s Mars flyby.
