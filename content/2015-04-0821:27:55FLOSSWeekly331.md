Hosts: [Randal Schwartz](https://twitter.com/merlyn) and [Dan
Lynch](https://twitter.com/methoddan)

Randi Harper, Founder of the [Online Abuse Prevention Initiative
(OAPI)](http://onlineabuseprevention.org/) joins us to talk about online
abuse and OAPI. OAPI is a nonprofit organization dedicated to reducing
and mitigating online abuse through the study and analysis of abuse
patterns, the creation of anti-harassment tools and resources, and
collaboration with tech companies seeking to better support their
communities.
