NASA\'s Juno Probes the Depths of Jupiter\'s Great Red Spot
===========================================================

Date: 2017-12-11 21:12:00

[![animation simulates the motion of clouds in Jupiter\'s Great Red
Spot](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/juno/20171211/PIA22176-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-316&rn=news.xml&rst=7021)\
\
Data collected by NASA\'s Juno spacecraft during its first pass over
Jupiter\'s Great Red Spot in July 2017 indicate that this iconic feature
penetrates well below the clouds.
