Exotic Cosmic Locales Available as Space Tourism Posters
========================================================

Date: 2016-02-10 21:02:00

[![A set of travel posters from NASA/JPL depicts various cosmic
destinations.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/solarsystem/20160210/posters20160210-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-039&rn=news.xml&rst=5052)\
\
Fourteen space travel posters of colorful, exotic cosmic settings are
now available free for downloading and printing.
