New, Space-Based View of Human-Made Carbon Dioxide
==================================================

Date: 2016-11-01 20:11:00

[![Human carbon dioxide emissions over Europe, the Middle East and
northern
Africa.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/oco/20161101/earth20161101b-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-286&rn=news.xml&rst=6666)\
\
Scientists have produced the first global maps of human emissions of
carbon dioxide ever made solely from satellite observations, using data
from NASA\'s OCO-2.
