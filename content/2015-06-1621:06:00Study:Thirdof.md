Study: Third of Big Groundwater Basins in Distress
==================================================

Date: 2015-06-16 21:06:00

[![UC Irvine groundwater storage trends from NASA\'s GRACE (2003-2013)
for Earth\'s 37 largest
aquifers.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/grace/20150616/grace20150616-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-206&rn=news.xml&rst=4626)\
\
UC Irvine studies using NASA GRACE data find a third of Earth\'s largest
groundwater basins are being rapidly depleted by human use, despite
little data about how much water remains.
