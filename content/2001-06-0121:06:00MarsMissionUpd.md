Mars Mission Update Via Webcast
===============================

Date: 2001-06-01 21:06:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion
Laboratory](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6256)\
\
Mars scientists from NASA\'s Jet Propulsion Laboratory, Pasadena,
Calif., will give the latest report about the 2001 Mars Odyssey mission,
now en route to the mysterious red planet, in a webcast available for
viewing starting June 7, 2001 at 11 a.m. Pacific Time.
