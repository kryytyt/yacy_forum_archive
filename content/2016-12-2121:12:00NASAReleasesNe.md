NASA Releases New, Detailed Greenland Glacier Data
==================================================

Date: 2016-12-21 21:12:00

[![Greenland\'s coastal
glaciers](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/omg/20161221/omg20161221-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-327&rn=news.xml&rst=6710)\
\
NASA\'s Oceans Melting Greenland (OMG) mission has released preliminary
data on the heights of Greenland coastal glaciers from its first
airborne campaign in March 2016.
