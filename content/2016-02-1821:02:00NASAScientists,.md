NASA Scientists, Engineers Receive Presidential Early Career Awards
===================================================================

Date: 2016-02-18 21:02:00

[![Dr. James
Benardini](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/awards/20160218/awards20160218-226.jpg){width="100"

President Obama Thursday named six NASA researchers, including one from
JPL, as recipients of the 2016 Presidential Early Career Award for
Scientists and Engineers.
