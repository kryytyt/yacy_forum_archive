Asteroid 2002 AJ129 to Fly Safely Past Earth February 4
=======================================================

Date: 2018-01-19 21:01:00

[![Illustration of Asteroid 2002
AJ129](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/asteroid/20180118/asteroidstill20180118-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2018-011&rn=news.xml&rst=7042)\
\
Asteroid 2002 AJ129 will make a close approach to Earth on Feb. 4, 2018,
but no closer than 10 times the distance between Earth and the moon.
