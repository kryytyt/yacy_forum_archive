Nicht alle negativen Schlagzeilen über die Kernkraft \...
=========================================================

Date: 2017-08-03 23:34:02

Nicht alle negativen Schlagzeilen über die Kernkraft sind der Sicherheit
geschuldet. Manchmal [ist es auch ganz normales \"Großprojekte der
öffentlichen
Hand\"-Versagen](https://www.nytimes.com/2017/07/31/climate/nuclear-power-project-canceled-in-south-carolina.html).

> In a major blow to the future of nuclear power in the United States,
> two South Carolina utilities said on Monday that they would abandon
> two unfinished nuclear reactors in the state, putting an end to a
> project that was once expected to showcase advanced nuclear technology
> but has since been plagued by delays and cost overruns.
>
> The two reactors, which have cost the utilities roughly \$9 billion,
> remain less than 40 percent built.
