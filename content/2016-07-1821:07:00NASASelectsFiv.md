NASA Selects Five Mars Orbiter Concept Studies
==============================================

Date: 2016-07-18 21:07:00

[![About 1000 Viking Orbiter red- and violet-filter images have been
processed to provide global color coverage of Mars at a scale of 1
km/pixel.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/20160421/PIA00407-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-190&rn=news.xml&rst=6565)\
\
NASA has selected five U.S. aerospace companies to conduct concept
studies for a potential future Mars orbiter mission.
