Recurring Martian Streaks: Flowing Sand, Not Water?
===================================================

Date: 2017-11-20 21:11:00

[![Inner slope of a Martian
crater](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20171120/PIA22070-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-299&rn=news.xml&rst=7005)\
\
Seasonal dark streaks on Mars that previously were described as possible
signs of flowing water have steep slopes that are better matches to dry
flow processes.
