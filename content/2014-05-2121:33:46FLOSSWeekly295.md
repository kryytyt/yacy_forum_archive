**Hosts:** [Randal Schwartz](http://twit.tv/floss) and [Randi
Harper](https://twitter.com/freebsdgirl).

X2Go is a Remote Desktop solution which enables you to access a
graphical desktop of a computer over a low bandwidth (or high bandwidth)
connection.

Mike Gabriel handles development, coordination of development, bug
tracking and patch management, i18n team coordination, infrastructure
maintenance and release management for X2Go.

Mike DePaulo is a sysadmin at a large company by day, open source
developer by night. He contributes to X2Go in his free time and
primarily focuses on X2Go\'s Windows client.
