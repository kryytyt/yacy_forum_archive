Rover Rounds Martian Dune to Get to the Other Side
==================================================

Date: 2016-01-04 21:01:00

[![Full-Circle Panorama Beside \'Namib Dune\' on
Mars](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20160104/pia20284-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-001&rn=news.xml&rst=4810)\
\
NASA\'s Curiosity rover has driven to the downwind side of an active
sand dune and returned images of cascaded sand.
