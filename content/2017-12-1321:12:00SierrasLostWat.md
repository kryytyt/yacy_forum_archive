Sierras Lost Water Weight, Grew Taller During Drought
=====================================================

Date: 2017-12-13 21:12:00

[![Sierra Nevada
range](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20171213/sierra20171213-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-318&rn=news.xml&rst=7023)\
\
Loss of water from Sierra Nevada rocks caused the California mountains
to rise almost an inch during the recent drought.
