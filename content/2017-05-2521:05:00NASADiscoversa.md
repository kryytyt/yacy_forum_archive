NASA Discovers a New Mode of Ice Loss in Greenland
==================================================

Date: 2017-05-25 21:05:00

[![Rink Glacier in western Greenland, with a meltwater lake visible
center.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/glacier/20170525/glacier20170525-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-151&rn=news.xml&rst=6857)\
\
A NASA study finds during Greenland\'s two hottest summers on record,
ice in Rink Glacier didn\'t just melt faster, it slid through the
glacier\'s interior in a gigantic wave.
