> Lesser FX houses would just build a solid mannequin, slice it up, and
> paint the slices. Not these guys. These guys *built the body from the
> inside out* \-- viscera, skeleton, body fat, connective fascia,
> musculature \-- and *then* carved it up. I don\'t know if mere
> pictures can convey the icky verisimilitude of the result.
