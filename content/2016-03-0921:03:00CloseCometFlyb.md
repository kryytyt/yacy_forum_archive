Close Comet Flyby Threw Mars\' Magnetic Field Into Chaos
========================================================

Date: 2016-03-09 21:03:00

Just weeks before the historic encounter of comet C/2013 A1 (Siding
Spring) with Mars in October 2014, NASA\'s Mars Atmosphere and Volatile
Evolution (MAVEN) spacecraft entered orbit around the Red Planet.
