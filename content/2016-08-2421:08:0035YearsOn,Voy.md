35 Years On, Voyager\'s Legacy Continues at Saturn
==================================================

Date: 2016-08-24 21:08:00

[![Up-close views from the Voyagers, like this one from Voyager 2,

NASA\'s Voyager 2 spacecraft made its closest approach to Saturn 35
years ago. The Cassini mission has followed up on many of Voyager\'s
discoveries.
