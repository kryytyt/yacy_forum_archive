NASA Studies a Rarity: Growing Louisiana Deltas
===============================================

Date: 2017-02-08 21:02:00

[![False-color images of rising tide at Wax Lake Delta,
Louisiana](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20170208/Wax_Delta-226.gif){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-026&rn=news.xml&rst=6736)\
\
A NASA study of two growing Louisiana deltas can help scientists better
understand how coastal wetlands will respond to global sea level rise.
