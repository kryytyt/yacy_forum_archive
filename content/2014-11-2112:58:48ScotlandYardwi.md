Scotland Yard wird gerade von einem Haufen Journalisten \...
============================================================

Date: 2014-11-21 12:58:48

[Scotland Yard wird gerade von einem Haufen Journalisten
verklagt](http://www.theguardian.com/uk-news/2014/nov/20/police-legal-action-snooping-journalists).
Grund:

> The six journalists have obtained official files that reveal how
> police logged details of their work as they reported on protests. One
> photographer discovered that the Met police had more than 130 entries
> detailing his movements, including what he was wearing, at
> demonstrations he attended as a member of the press.

In der Datenbank stehen dann (über die Journalisten, nicht die
Extremisten!) auch Details über Aussehen, Kindheit, die
Krankengeschichte von Familienangehörigen (!?!?).
