NASA\'s WISE, Fermi Missions Reveal a Surprising Blazar Connection
==================================================================

Date: 2016-08-24 21:08:00

[![Black-hole-powered galaxies called blazars are the most common
sources detected by NASA\'s Fermi Gamma-ray Space
Telescope.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/wise/20160824/PIA20912-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-216&rn=news.xml&rst=6598)\
\
Astronomers studying distant galaxies powered by monster black holes
have uncovered an unexpected link between two very different wavelengths
of the light they emit, the mid-infrared and gamma rays.
