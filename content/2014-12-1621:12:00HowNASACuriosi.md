How NASA Curiosity Instrument Made First Detection of Organic Matter on Mars
============================================================================

Date: 2014-12-16 21:12:00

[![Updated Curiosity Self-Portrait at \'John
Klein\'](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20141216/pia16937-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-433&rn=news.xml&rst=4414)\
\
The team responsible for the Sample Analysis at Mars (SAM) instrument
suite on NASA\'s Curiosity rover has made the first definitive detection
of organic molecules at Mars.
