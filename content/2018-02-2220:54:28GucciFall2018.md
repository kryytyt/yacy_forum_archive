Gucci Fall 2018 Ready-to-Wear
=============================

Date: 2018-02-22 20:54:28

[Alessandro
Michele:](https://www.vogue.com/fashion-shows/fall-2018-ready-to-wear/gucci)

> A procession of transhumans, walking in trancelike step through a
> suite of operating theaters: Bolted together from the clothing of many
> cultures, they were Alessandro Michele\'s metaphor for how people
> today construct their identities \-- a population undergoing
> self-regeneration through the powers of tech, Hollywood, Instagram,
> and Gucci. \"We are the Dr. Frankenstein of our lives,\" said Michele.
> \"There\'s a clinical clarity about what I am doing.\" Someone was
> cradling a baby dragon. A couple of people had replicas of their own
> heads tucked under their arms.
