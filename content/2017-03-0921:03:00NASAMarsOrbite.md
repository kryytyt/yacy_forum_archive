NASA Mars Orbiter Tracks Back-to-Back Regional Storms
=====================================================

Date: 2017-03-09 21:03:00

[![Back-to-Back Martian Dust
Storms](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20170309/pia21484-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-064&rn=news.xml&rst=6771)\
\
A regional dust storm currently swelling on Mars follows unusually
closely on one that blossomed less than two weeks earlier and is now
dissipating, as seen in daily global weather monitoring by NASA\'s Mars
Reconnaissance Orbiter.
