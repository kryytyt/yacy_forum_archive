NASA Rover\'s Sand-Dune Studies Yield Surprise
==============================================

Date: 2016-06-30 21:06:00

[![Two Sizes of Ripples on Surface of Martian Sand
Dune](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20160630/PIA20755-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-169&rn=news.xml&rst=6551)\
\
Some of the wind-sculpted sand ripples on Mars are a type not seen on
Earth, and their relationship to the thin Martian atmosphere today
provides new clues about the atmosphere\'s history.
