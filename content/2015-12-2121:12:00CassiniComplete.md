Cassini Completes Final Close Enceladus Flyby
=============================================

Date: 2015-12-21 21:12:00

[![NASA/JPL-Caltech/Space Science
Institute](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20151221/PIA17210-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-382&rn=news.xml&rst=4803)\
\
NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft has begun transmitting data and images from
the mission\'s final close flyby of Saturn\'s active moon Enceladus,
which took place on Saturday, Dec. 19.
