SPIDER Experiment Touches Down in Antarctica
============================================

Date: 2015-01-21 21:01:00

[![Jeff Filippini, a postdoctoral scholar who worked on the SPIDER
receiver team at
Caltech](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/universe/20150121/pia19177-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-026&rn=news.xml&rst=4452)\
\
An instrument called SPIDER just landed after 16 days drifting in the
wind above Antarctica, searching for signs of inflation in the earliest
moments of the universe.
