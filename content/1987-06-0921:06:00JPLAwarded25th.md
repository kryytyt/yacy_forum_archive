JPL Awarded 25th Annual Crown City Award
========================================

Date: 1987-06-09 21:06:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5774)\
\
The Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL) has been awarded the Pasadena
Chamber of Commerce\'s 25th annual Crown City Award in recognition of
JPL\'s involvement with the local community and contributions to
Pasadena\'s prestige.
