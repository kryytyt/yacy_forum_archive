Cassini to Make Last Close Flyby of Saturn Moon Dione
=====================================================

Date: 2015-08-13 21:08:00

[![A view of Saturn\'s moon Dione captured by NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft
during a close flyby on June
16](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20150813/cassini20150813-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-269&rn=news.xml&rst=4689)\
\
NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft will zip past Saturn\'s moon Dione on Monday,
Aug. 17 \-- the final close flyby of this icy satellite during the
spacecraft\'s long mission.
