NASA Media Telecon on Status of LDSD Hawaiian Test Flight
=========================================================

Date: 2014-06-11 21:06:00

[![This artist\'s concept shows the test vehicle for NASA\'s Low-Density
Supersonic Decelerator

NASA will hold a media teleconference at 10 a.m. PDT (1 p.m. EDT) on
Thursday, June 12 to discuss what this delay in the LDSD testing means
and possible next steps for the project.
