NASA Holds Briefing on Early Test Results for New Planetary Landing Technology
==============================================================================

Date: 2014-08-05 21:08:00

[![Saucer Out of the
Drink](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/ldsd/20140629/ldsd20140629-226.jpg){width="100"

near-space in June. An Aug. 8 news conference at 9 a.m. PDT will feature
new video and early mission results.
