Aus der beliebten Kategorie \"Zwei Dinge sind unendlich\", \...
===============================================================

Date: 2015-04-13 18:08:54

Aus der beliebten Kategorie \"Zwei Dinge sind unendlich\", heute: [Es
gibt wirklich Menschen, die die Apple Watch
kaufen](http://intelligence.slice.com/first-apple-watch-data-one-just-isnt-enough/).

> Slice Intelligence estimates that 957,000 people in the U.S.
> pre-ordered an Apple Watch on Friday, the first day the watch was
> available for sale. According to ereceipt data from a panel of two
> million online shoppers, each Apple Watch buyer ordered an average of
> 1.3 watches, spending \$503.83 per watch.
