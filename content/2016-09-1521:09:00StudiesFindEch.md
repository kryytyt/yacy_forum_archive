Studies Find Echoes of Black Holes Eating Stars
===============================================

Date: 2016-09-15 21:09:00

[![Infrared Echoes of a Black Hole Eating a
Star](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/wise/20160915/PIA20027-226.jpg){width="100"

Supermassive black holes, with their immense gravitational pull, are
notoriously good at clearing out their immediate surroundings by eating
nearby objects.
