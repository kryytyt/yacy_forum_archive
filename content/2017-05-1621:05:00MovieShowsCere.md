Movie Shows Ceres at Opposition from Sun
========================================

Date: 2017-05-16 21:05:00

[![Movie is made of images taken by NASA\'s Dawn
spacecraft](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20170516/PIA21405-226.gif){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-140&rn=news.xml&rst=6845)\
\
NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft successfully observed Ceres at opposition on
April 29, taking images from a position exactly between the sun and
Ceres\' surface.
