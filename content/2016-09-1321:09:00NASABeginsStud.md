NASA Begins Study of Australia\'s Great Barrier Reef
====================================================

Date: 2016-09-13 21:09:00

[![The Gulfstream III carrying NASA\'s PRISM instrument being readied
for science flights from Cairns,
Australia.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/climate/20160913/coral20160913-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-238&rn=news.xml&rst=6620)\
\
A NASA airborne mission designed to transform our understanding of
Earth\'s ecologically sensitive coral reefs has arrived in Australia to
investigate the Great Barrier Reef.
