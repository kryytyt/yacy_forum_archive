Is Climate Changing Cloud Heights? Too Soon to Say
==================================================

Date: 2017-05-05 21:05:00

[![Climate change may eventually change global cloud
heights.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/misr/20170505/MISR20170505-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-133&rn=news.xml&rst=6838)\
\
With 10 years of MISR satellite data, scientists suspected global cloud
heights might be getting lower. With 15 years of data, they\'ve found
it\'s too early to say for sure.
