Inerruption of Voyager 2 Telemetry Signal
=========================================

Date: 1989-10-26 20:10:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5875)\
\
NASA\'s Voyager 1 spacecraft experienced an interruption of its
telemetry signal between Monday afternoon and Tuesday midnight, and
Wednesday resumed normal communications with Earth.
