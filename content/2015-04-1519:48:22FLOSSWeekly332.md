Hosts: [Randal Schwartz](https://twitter.com/merlyn) and [Guillermo
Amaral](https://twitter.com/gamaral)

This week we talk about MOHID. MOHID is a three-dimensional water
modelling system developed by MARETEC. MOHID has been applied to
different study cases, as coastal and estuarine areas, as well as
oceanic processes and reservoirs, and it has showed its ability to
simulate complex features of the flows.

Guests: [Ramiro Neves](http://www.mohid.com/) and [Ricardo
Miranda](http://www.mohid.com/)
