NASA Gives Go-ahead To Build \'Deep Impact\' Spacecraft
=======================================================

Date: 2001-05-24 21:05:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion
Laboratory](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6152)\
\
The Deep Impact mission, the first mission to ever attempt to impact a
comet nucleus in order to answer basic questions about the nature of
comets, has successfully completed.
