NASA Hosts Nov. 15 Telecon About Mars Rover Progress
====================================================

Date: 2012-11-13 21:11:00

[![This artist\'s concept features NASA\'s Mars Science Laboratory
Curiosity
rover](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20120607/pia15791-th.jpg){width="100"

NASA will host a media teleconference at 11 a.m. PST (2 p.m. EST) on
Thursday, Nov. 15, to provide an update about the Curiosity rover\'s
mission to Mars\' Gale Crater.
