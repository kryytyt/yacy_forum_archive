Scientists Pinpoint Saturn With Exquisite Accuracy
==================================================

Date: 2015-01-08 21:01:00

[![Researchers have determined the location of the Saturn system\'s
center of
mass](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20150108/pia08387-226.jpg){width="100"

continent-spanning telescope array yield greatly improved knowledge of
the \"true\" center of the Saturn system.
