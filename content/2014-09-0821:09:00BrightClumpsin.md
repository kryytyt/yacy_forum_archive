Bright Clumps in Saturn Ring Now Mysteriously Scarce
====================================================

Date: 2014-09-08 21:09:00

[![Shadows and
Rings](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20140908/pia17154-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-302&rn=news.xml&rst=4279)\
\
A NASA-funded study finds a drop in the number of bright clumps in one
of Saturn\'s rings over the past 30 years, suggesting small moonlets
there have been on the decline.
