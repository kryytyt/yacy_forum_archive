Study of Complex 2016 Quake May Alter Hazard Models
===================================================

Date: 2017-03-23 20:03:00

[![Two ALOS-2 satellite images show ground displacements from the Nov.
2016 Kaikoura
earthquake](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20170323/earth20170323-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-084&rn=news.xml&rst=6790)\
\
A new study finds last year\'s magnitude 7.8 New Zealand earthquake was
so complex and unusual it is likely to change how scientists think about
quake hazards around the world.
