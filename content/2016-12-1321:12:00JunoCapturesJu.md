Juno Captures Jupiter \'Pearl\'
===============================

Date: 2016-12-13 21:12:00

[![This image, taken by the JunoCam imager on NASA\'s Juno
spacecraft.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/juno/20161213/pearl-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-320&rn=news.xml&rst=6702)\
\
A new image from NASA\'s Juno spacecraft highlights the seventh of
Jupiter\'s eight \'string of pearls\' \-- huge counterclockwise rotating
storms that appear as white ovals.
