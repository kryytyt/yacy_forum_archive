What\'s Inside Ceres? New Findings from Gravity Data
====================================================

Date: 2016-08-03 21:08:00

[![This artist\'s concept shows a diagram of how the inside of Ceres
could be
structured.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20160803/PIA20867-226.jpg){width="100"

A new study suggests Ceres has a weak interior, and that water and other
light materials separated from rock during a heating phase early in its
history.
