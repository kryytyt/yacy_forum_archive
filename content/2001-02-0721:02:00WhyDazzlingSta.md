Why Dazzling Stars are Given Boring but Useful Names
====================================================

Date: 2001-02-07 21:02:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion
Laboratory](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6397)\
\
Of the 100 billion stars in our Milky Way galaxy, only a handful have
colorful names, while the rest are designated by letters and numbers
that are the stellar equivalent of a Social Security card.
