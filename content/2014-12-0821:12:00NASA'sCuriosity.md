NASA\'s Curiosity Rover Finds Clues to How Water Helped Shape Martian Landscape
===============================================================================

Date: 2014-12-08 21:12:00

[![Sedimentary Signs of a Martian
Lakebed](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20141208/pia19074-16.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-419&rn=news.xml&rst=4398)\
\
Observations by NASA\'s Curiosity Rover indicate Mars\' Mount Sharp was
built by sediments deposited in a large lake bed over tens of millions
of years.
