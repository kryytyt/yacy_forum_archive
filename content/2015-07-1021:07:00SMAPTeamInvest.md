SMAP Team Investigating Radar Instrument Anomaly
================================================

Date: 2015-07-10 21:07:00

[![NASA\'s Soil Moisture Active Passive (SMAP)
mission](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/smap/20150224/smap20150224-226.jpg){width="100"

\
Mission managers at NASA\'s Jet Propulsion Laboratory, Pasadena,
California, are assessing an anomaly with the radar instrument on
NASA\'s Soil Moisture Active Passive (SMAP) satellite observatory.
