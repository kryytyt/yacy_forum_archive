NASA to Host Telecon About Curiosity Rover Progress
===================================================

Date: 2012-09-05 21:09:00

[![Mars Rover Curiosity in Artist\'s Concept,
Close-up](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20110624/pia14175-th.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.cfm?release=2012-276&rn=news.xml&rst=3506)\
\
NASA will host a media teleconference at 10 a.m. PDT (1 p.m. EDT) on
Thursday, Sept. 6, to provide a status update on the Curiosity rover\'s
mission to Mars\' Gale Crater.
