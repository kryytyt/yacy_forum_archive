Experiments Show Titan Lakes May Fizz with Nitrogen
===================================================

Date: 2017-03-15 20:03:00

[![Cassini captured this mosaic of images showing the northern lakes and
seas of Saturn\'s moon
Titan.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20170315/PIA21434-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-071&rn=news.xml&rst=6778)\
\
A recent NASA-funded study has shown how the hydrocarbon lakes and seas
of Saturn\'s moon Titan might occasionally erupt with dramatic patches
of bubbles.
