NASA to Hold Dec. 8 Media Teleconference on Mars Rover Curiosity Observations
=============================================================================

Date: 2014-12-03 21:12:00

[![Curiosity Self-Portrait at \'Windjana\' Drilling
Site](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20141203/pia18390-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-415&rn=news.xml&rst=4396)\
\
NASA will host a media teleconference at 9 a.m. PST (noon EST) Monday,
Dec. 8, to discuss geological observations made by NASA\'s Mars rover
Curiosity.
