NASA Rover Samples Active Linear Dune on Mars
=============================================

Date: 2017-05-04 21:05:00

[![Textures Where Curiosity Rover Studied a Martian
Dune](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20170504/pia11242-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-130&rn=news.xml&rst=6835)\
\
As it drives uphill from a band of rippled sand dunes, NASA\'s Curiosity
Mars rover is toting a fistful of dark sand for onboard analysis that
will complete its investigation of those dunes.
