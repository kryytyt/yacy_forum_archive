Sandy Selfie Sent from NASA Mars Rover
======================================

Date: 2016-01-29 21:01:00

[![Curiosity Self-Portrait at \'Big Sky\' Drilling
Site](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20160129/pia20316-226.jpg){width="100"

The latest self-portrait from NASA\'s Curiosity Mars rover shows the
car-size mobile laboratory beside a dark dune where it has been scooping
and sieving samples of sand.
