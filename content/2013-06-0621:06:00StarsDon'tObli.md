Stars Don\'t Obliterate Their Planets (Very Often)
==================================================

Date: 2013-06-06 21:06:00

[![On the Road Toward a Star, Planets Halt Their
Migration](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/kepler/20130606/pia17242-th.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.cfm?release=2013-190&rn=news.xml&rst=3820)\
\
A new study using data from NASA\'s Kepler mission shows that migrating
planets cease their travels before being consumed by their stars.
