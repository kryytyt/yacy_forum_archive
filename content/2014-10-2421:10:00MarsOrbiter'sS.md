Mars Orbiter\'s Spectrometer Shows Oort Comet\'s Coma
=====================================================

Date: 2014-10-24 21:10:00

[![Images From Mars-Orbiting Spectrometer Show Comet\'s
Coma](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20141024/pia15291-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-372&rn=news.xml&rst=4353)\
\
The imaging spectrometer on NASA\'s Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter has
provided an image of the coma surrounding a comet that flew near Mars
this week.
