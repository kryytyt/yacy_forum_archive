Mars Volcano, Earth\'s Dinosaurs Went Extinct About the Same Time
=================================================================

Date: 2017-03-20 20:03:00

[![Arsia Mons Volcano on
Mars](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/20170320b/PIA00177-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-076&rn=news.xml&rst=6783)\
\
A giant Martian volcano ended its activity about 50 million years ago,
which happened to be around the same time dinosaurs went extinct on
Earth.
