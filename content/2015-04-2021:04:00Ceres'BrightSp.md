Ceres\' Bright Spots Come Back Into View
========================================

Date: 2015-04-20 21:04:00

[![Settling in at
Ceres](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20150420/pia19064-16.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-136&rn=news.xml&rst=4558)\
\
The two brightest spots on dwarf planet Ceres, which have fascinated
scientists for months, are back in view in the newest images from
NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft.
