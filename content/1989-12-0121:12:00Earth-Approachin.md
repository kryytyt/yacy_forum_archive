Earth-Approaching Asteroid Shape Determined
===========================================

Date: 1989-12-01 21:12:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5878)\
\
Scientists at NASA\'s Jet Propulsion Laboratory developed new approach
to old radar data and determined the shape of the large
Earth-approaching asteroid, Eros.
