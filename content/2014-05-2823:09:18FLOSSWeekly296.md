**Hosts:** [Randal Schwartz](http://twit.tv/floss) and [Aaron
Newcomb](http://thesourceshow.org/).

Bro is a powerful network analysis framework that is much different from
the typical IDS you may know.

As one of the lead engineers, Seth Hall works on the design, development
and implementation of the Bro Programming Language and the Bro Platform.

As the lead trainer and community coordinator Liam has worked with a
large number of organizations to apply the new capabilities of the Bro
Platform in novel and unexpected ways.

**Guests:** [Liam Randall](http://bro.org/) and [Seth
Hall](http://bro.org/)
