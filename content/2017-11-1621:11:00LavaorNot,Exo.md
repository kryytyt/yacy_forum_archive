Lava or Not, Exoplanet 55 Cancri e Likely to have Atmosphere
============================================================

Date: 2017-11-16 21:11:00

[![Illustration of super-Earth exoplanet 55 Cancri
e](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/exoplanet/20171116/pia22069-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-296&rn=news.xml&rst=7001)\
\
A super-Earth exoplanet thought to sport lava lakes on its hot side, 55
Cancri e, likely also wears an atmosphere with ingredients similar to
Earth\'s, a new study indicates.
