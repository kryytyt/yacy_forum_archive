Team Evaluates New Data Collection Method After Age-Related Issue
=================================================================

Date: 2016-10-11 21:10:00

[![Technicians install NASA\'s Tropospheric Emission Spectrometer (TES)
instrument on NASA\'s Aura spacecraft prior to
launch](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/tes/TES_on_Shaker1-1041-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-262&rn=news.xml&rst=6644)\
\
Managers are evaluating another way to collect and process data from the
Tropospheric Emission Spectrometer on NASA\'s Aura satellite after an
age-related TES component failure.
