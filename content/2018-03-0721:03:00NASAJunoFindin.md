NASA Juno Findings - Jupiter\'s Jet-Streams Are Unearthly
=========================================================

Date: 2018-03-07 21:03:00

[![Composite image, derived from data collected by the Jovian Infrared
Auroral Mapper (JIRAM) instrument aboard NASA\'s Juno mission to
Jupiter](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/juno/20180307/PIA22335-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2018-044&rn=news.xml&rst=7075)\
\
Data collected by NASA\'s Juno mission indicate that the atmospheric
winds of Jupiter run deep into its atmosphere and last longer than
similar atmospheric processes on Earth.
