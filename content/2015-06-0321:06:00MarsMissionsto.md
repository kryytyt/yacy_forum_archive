Mars Missions to Pause Commanding in June, Due to Sun
=====================================================

Date: 2015-06-03 21:06:00

[![Geometry of Mars Solar
Conjunction](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/20150603/pia16811-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-190&rn=news.xml&rst=4611)\
\
In June 2015, Mars will swing almost directly behind the sun from
Earth\'s perspective, and this celestial geometry will lead to
diminished communications with spacecraft at Mars.
