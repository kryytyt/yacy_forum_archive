Ministry of Culture said last month that it was targeting \"striptease\"
and other \"obscene, pornographic, and vulgar performances\" at
funerals, weddings and traditional Chinese New Year public
gatherings.](http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/2018/02/20/china-wages-war-funeral-strippers/)

> Some communities in rural China reportedly believe that bigger
> attendances at funerals help to honour the dead and bring them good
> fortune. But some experts say the erotic shows pay tribute to
> fertility. \[\...\]
>
> Reports say the new campaign involves people being offered financial
> rewards to contact a special \"hotline\" to report \"funeral
> misdeeds\". \[\...\]
>
> \"In recent decades,\" the Global Times said on Tuesday, \"Chinese
> rural households are more inclined to show off their disposable
> incomes by paying out several times their annual income for actors,
> singers, comedians, and - most recently, strippers - to comfort the
> bereaved and entertain the mourners.\"

I am of course immediately reminded of *Harvey Pitnik\'s Funeral* from
*Amazon Women on the Moon.* \"Held over! Second big week!\"
