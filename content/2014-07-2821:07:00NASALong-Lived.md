NASA Long-Lived Mars Opportunity Rover Passes 25 Miles of Driving
=================================================================

Date: 2014-07-28 21:07:00

[![\'Lunokhod 2\' Crater on
Mars](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mer/2014-07-28/pia18416-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-245&rn=news.xml&rst=4232)\
\
NASA\'s Opportunity Mars rover, which landed on the Red Planet in 2004,
now holds the off-Earth roving distance record after accruing 25 miles
(40 kilometers) of driving.
