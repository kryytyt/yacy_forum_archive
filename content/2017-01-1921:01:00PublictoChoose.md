Public to Choose Jupiter Picture Sites for NASA Juno
====================================================

Date: 2017-01-19 21:01:00

[![NASA\'s Juno spacecraft performed its third close flyby of
Jupiter.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/juno/20170119/PIA21377-226.jpg){width="100"

spacecraft\'s JunoCam imager takes of Jupiter.
