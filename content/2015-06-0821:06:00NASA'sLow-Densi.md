NASA\'s Low-Density Supersonic Decelerator Set to Blast Off
===========================================================

Date: 2015-06-08 21:06:00

[![LDSD on the Launch
Tower](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/ldsd/20150603/pia19343-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-193&rn=news.xml&rst=4613)\
\
A balloon carrying a test vehicle for NASA\'s Low-Density Supersonic
Decelerator (LDSD) project is scheduled to lift off no earlier than
10:30 a.m. PDT (1:30 p.m. EDT/ 7:30 a.m. HST) Monday, June 8, from
Kauai, Hawaii.
