Fourth Series Surveyor Launch
=============================

Date: 1967-07-11 20:07:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6090)\
\
The fourth of a series of Surveyor spacecraft, designed to soft land on
the moon, will be launched from Cape Kennedy during a launch period
beginning Thursday, July 13, and ending Monday, July 17.
