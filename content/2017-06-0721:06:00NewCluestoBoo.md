New Clues to Boomerang Nebula Mystery
=====================================

Date: 2017-06-07 21:06:00

[![This composite image shows the Boomerang Nebula, a pre-planetary
nebula produced by a dying
star.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/universe/20170607/universe20170607-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-162&rn=news.xml&rst=6869)\
\
An ancient, red giant star in the throes of a frigid death has produced
the coldest known object in the cosmos: the Boomerang Nebula.
