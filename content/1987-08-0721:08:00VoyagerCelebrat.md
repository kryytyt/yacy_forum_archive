Voyager Celebrates Its 10th Anniversary
=======================================

Date: 1987-08-07 21:08:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5778)\
\
With one of its two spacecraft en route to distant Neptune and the other
exploring the outer solar system, the Voyager Mission celebrates its
10th anniversary of launch on Aug. 20.
