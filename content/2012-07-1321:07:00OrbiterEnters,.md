Orbiter Enters, Then Exits, Standby Safe Mode
=============================================

Date: 2012-07-13 21:07:00

[![NASA\'s Mars Odyssey spacecraft passes above Mars\' south pole in
this artist\'s concept
illustration.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/odyssey/20081009/pia04816-th.jpg){width="100"

\
NASA\'s Mars Odyssey orbiter experienced about 21 hours in a
reduced-activity precautionary status ending at about 10 a.m. PDT (1
p.m. EDT) on Thursday, July 12.
