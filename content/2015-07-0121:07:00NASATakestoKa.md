NASA Takes to Kansas Skies to Study Nighttime Thunderstorms
===========================================================

Date: 2015-07-01 21:07:00

[![NASA\'s DC-8 airborne
laboratory](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20150701/earth20150701-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-225&rn=news.xml&rst=4646)\
\
In most of the U.S., summer thunderstorms form on hot days. In the Great
Plains, they often form at night. NASA is joining a multi-agency field
campaign to learn why.
