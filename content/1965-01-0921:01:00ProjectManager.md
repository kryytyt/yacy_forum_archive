Project Manager Appointed for Next Mariner-Series Mission
=========================================================

Date: 1965-01-09 21:01:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6077)\
\
Appointment of project managers for the two forthcoming Mariner-series
deep space exploration mission to Venus and Mars has been announced by
Gen. A. R. Luedecke, Deputy Director of the Jet Propulsion Laboratory of
the California Institute of Technology.
