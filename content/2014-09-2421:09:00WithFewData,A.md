With Few Data, Arctic Carbon Models Lack Consensus
==================================================

Date: 2014-09-24 21:09:00

[![Alaskan tundra is showing the effects of melting
permafrost.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/arctic/20140924/earth20140924-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-323&rn=news.xml&rst=4307)\
\
A new NASA study shows how much work is still needed to reach consensus
on the most basic questions about carbon in the Arctic, where global
warming is hitting hard.
