New Gravity Map Gives Best View Yet Inside Mars
===============================================

Date: 2016-03-21 20:03:00

[![Newly Detailed Map of Mars\' Crustal
Thickness](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/20160321/pia20326-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-079&rn=news.xml&rst=6165)\
\
A new map of Mars\' gravity made with three NASA spacecraft is the most
detailed to date, providing a revealing glimpse into the hidden interior
of the Red Planet.
