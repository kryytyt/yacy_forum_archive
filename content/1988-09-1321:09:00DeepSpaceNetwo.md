Deep Space Network Will Support the STS-26 Flight and TDRS-C
============================================================

Date: 1988-09-13 21:09:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5829)\
\
The Deep Space Network, operated for NASA by the Jet Propulsion
Laboratory, will provide tracking support for the STS-26 flight of the
space shuttle Discovery and for the deployment and on-orbit checkout of
Discovery\'s payload, the Tracking and Data Relay Satellite-C (TDRS-C).
