Curiosity Mars Rover Begins Study of Ridge Destination
======================================================

Date: 2017-07-11 21:07:00

[![View Toward \'Vera Rubin Ridge\' on Mount Sharp,
Mars](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20170711/pia21716-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-189&rn=news.xml&rst=6894)\
\
The car-size NASA rover on a Martian mountain, Curiosity, has begun its
long-anticipated study of an iron-bearing ridge forming a distinctive
layer on the mountain\'s slope.
