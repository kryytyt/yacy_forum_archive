Opportunity\'s Devilish View from on High
=========================================

Date: 2016-04-04 21:04:00

[![Opportunity\'s Devilish View from on
High](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mer/2016-04-04/PIA20012-226.jpg){width="100"

Opportunity recorded an image of a Martian dust devil twisting through
the valley below.
