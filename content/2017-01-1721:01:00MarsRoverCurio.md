Mars Rover Curiosity Examines Possible Mud Cracks
=================================================

Date: 2017-01-17 21:01:00

[![Possible Mud Cracks Preserved in Martian
Rock](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20170117/pia21261-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-009&rn=news.xml&rst=6721)\
\
Scientists used NASA\'s Curiosity Mars rover in recent weeks to examine
slabs of rock cross-hatched with shallow ridges that likely originated
as cracks in drying mud.
