NASA\'s Curiosity Mars Rover Studies Rock-Layer Contact Zone
============================================================

Date: 2015-07-01 21:07:00

[![Geological Contact Zone Near \'Marias Pass\' on
Mars](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20150701/pia19676-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-223&rn=news.xml&rst=4645)\
\
As Mars emerges from passing nearly behind the sun, NASA has resumed
full operations of the Curiosity rover, which has reached a site where
at least two rock types meet.
