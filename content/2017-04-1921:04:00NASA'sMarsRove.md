NASA\'s Mars Rover Opportunity Leaves \'Tribulation\'
=====================================================

Date: 2017-04-19 21:04:00

[![Mars Rover Opportunity\'s Panorama of
\'Rocheport\'](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mer/2017-04-19/PIA21493-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-113&rn=news.xml&rst=6819)\
\
NASA\'s senior Mars rover, Opportunity, is departing \"Cape
Tribulation,\" a crater-rim segment it has explored since late 2014, and
is heading for \"Perseverance Valley.\"
