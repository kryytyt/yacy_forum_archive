Cassini Begins Series of Flybys with Close-up of Saturn Moon Enceladus
======================================================================

Date: 2015-10-13 21:10:00

[![Artist\'s concept of Cassini\'s
flyby](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20151013/enceladus20151013-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-315&rn=news.xml&rst=4736)\
\
NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft will wrap up its time in the region of
Saturn\'s large, icy moons with a series of three close encounters with
Enceladus starting Wednesday, Oct. 14.
