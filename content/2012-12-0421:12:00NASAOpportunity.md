NASA Opportunity Rover Finishes Walkabout on Mars Crater Rim
============================================================

Date: 2012-12-04 21:12:00

[![Opportunity Rover\'s Fall 2012
Walkabout](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mer/2012-12-04/mer20121204-th.jpg){width="100"
