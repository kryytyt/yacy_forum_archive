Rewrite of Onboard Memory Planned for NASA Mars Orbiter
=======================================================

Date: 2015-10-29 20:10:00

[![Artist concept of Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter. Image credit:
NASA/JPL](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20081126/mro-concept-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-335&rn=news.xml&rst=4757)\
\
NASA\'s Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter will suspend activities next week to
perform a multi-step update of onboard memory that is crucial in case of
an unplanned computer reboot.
