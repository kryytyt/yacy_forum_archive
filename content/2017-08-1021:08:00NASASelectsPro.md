NASA Selects Proposals to Study Galaxies, Stars, Planets
========================================================

Date: 2017-08-10 21:08:00

[![NASA is exploring our solar system and beyond to understand the
workings of the universe, searching for water and life among the
stars.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/solarsystem/20150407/oceanworlds-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-213&rn=news.xml&rst=6918)\
\
NASA has selected six astrophysics Explorers Program proposals for
concept studies, three of which are managed by JPL.
