Arecibo Radar Returns with Asteroid Phaethon Images
===================================================

Date: 2017-12-22 21:12:00

[![Radar images of near-Earth asteroid 3200
Phaethon](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/asteroid/20171222/PIA22185-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-325&rn=news.xml&rst=7030)\
\
Months after Hurricane Maria, the Arecibo Observatory Planetary Radar
has resumed normal operations, providing high-resolution images of the
asteroid 3200 Phaethon Earth flyby.
