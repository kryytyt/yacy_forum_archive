Extreme Shrimp May Hold Clues to Alien Life
===========================================

Date: 2014-11-21 21:11:00

[![Densely-Grouped

At one of the world\'s deepest undersea hydrothermal vents, tiny shrimp
are piled on top of each other, layer upon layer, crawling on rock
chimneys that spew hot water.
