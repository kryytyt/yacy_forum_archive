NASA\'s Kepler Marks 1,000th Exoplanet Discovery, Uncovers More Small Worlds in Habitable Zones
===============================================================================================

Date: 2015-01-06 21:01:00

[![Of the more than 1,000 verified planets found by NASA\'s

than 4,000 candidate planets \-- the 1,000th of which was recently
verified.
