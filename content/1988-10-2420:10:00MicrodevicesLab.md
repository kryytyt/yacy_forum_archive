Microdevices Laboratory Ceremony
================================

Date: 1988-10-24 20:10:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"

A dedication ceremony for the new three-story Microdevices Laboratory
building at Jet Propulsion Laboratory is scheduled for 1:30 p.m. October
27, Dr. Lew Allen, JPL director, announced.
