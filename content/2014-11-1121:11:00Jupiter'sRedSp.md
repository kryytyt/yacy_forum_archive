Jupiter\'s Red Spot is Likely a Sunburn, Not a Blush
====================================================

Date: 2014-11-11 21:11:00

[![Research suggests effects of sunlight produce the color of Jupiter\'s
Great Red
Spot.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/jupiter/20141111/jupiter20141111-16.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-391&rn=news.xml&rst=4372)\
\
The ruddy color of Jupiter\'s Great Red Spot is likely a product of
simple chemicals being broken apart by sunlight in the planet\'s upper
atmosphere, according to a new analysis of data from NASA\'s Cassini
mission.
