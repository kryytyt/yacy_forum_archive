Err is a plugin based chatbot designed to be easily deployable,
extensible and maintainable.

It allows you to start scripts interactively from your chatrooms for any
reason: random humour, starting a build, monitoring commits, triggering
alerts \...

It is really easy to add your own features.

It is open source under the GPL3 license.
