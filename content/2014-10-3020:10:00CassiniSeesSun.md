Cassini Sees Sunny Seas on Titan
================================

Date: 2014-10-30 20:10:00

[![Specular
Spectacular](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20141030/pia18432-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-378&rn=news.xml&rst=4359)\
\
As it soared past Saturn\'s large moon Titan recently, NASA\'s Cassini
spacecraft caught a glimpse of bright sunlight reflecting off
hydrocarbon seas.
