Scientists Improve Brown Dwarf Weather Forecasts
================================================

Date: 2017-08-17 21:08:00

[![This artist\'s concept shows a brown dwarf with bands of
clouds](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/spitzer/20170817/PIA21752-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-221&rn=news.xml&rst=6925)\
\
Researchers have a new model for explaining how clouds move and change
shape in brown dwarfs, using insights from NASA\'s Spitzer Space
Telescope.
