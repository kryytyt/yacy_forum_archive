NASA Explains Why June 30 Will Get Extra Second
===============================================

Date: 2015-06-30 21:06:00

[![A flower clock is seen in the Jardin Anglais, Geneva,
Switzerland.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20150630/genevaclock20150630-226.jpg){width="100"

\
The day will officially be a bit longer than usual on Tuesday, June 30,
2015, because an extra second, or \"leap\" second, will be added.
