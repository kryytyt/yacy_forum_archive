NASA Finds Oceans Slowed Global Temperature Rise
================================================

Date: 2015-07-09 21:07:00

[![An Argo float,
foreground.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/climate/20150709/earth20150709-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-234&rn=news.xml&rst=4655)\
\
A NASA study shows heat has been trapped in the Pacific and Indian
oceans. The finding explains the recent slowdown in global temperature
rise.
