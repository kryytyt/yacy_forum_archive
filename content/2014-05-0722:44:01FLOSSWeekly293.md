**Hosts:** [Randal Schwartz](http://twit.tv/floss) and [Guillermo
Amaral](https://twitter.com/gamaral).

Jitsi (formerly SIP Communicator) is an audio/video and chat
communicator that supports protocols such as SIP, XMPP/Jabber, AIM/ICQ,
Windows Live, Yahoo! and many other useful features.

Emil Ivov is the founder of the Jitsi project and current project lead
for the jitsi.org community.
