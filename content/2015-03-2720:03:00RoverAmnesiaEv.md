Rover Amnesia Event Follows Latest Memory Reformatting
======================================================

Date: 2015-03-27 20:03:00

[![Mars Rover Opportunity Examines Bright
\'Athens\'](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mer/2015-03-27/pia19160-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-103&rn=news.xml&rst=4528)\
\
The team operating the Mars Rover Opportunity learned that it
experienced a brief amnesia event related to its flash memory, the first
since a reformatting a week earlier.
