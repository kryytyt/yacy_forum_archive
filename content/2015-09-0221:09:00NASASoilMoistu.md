NASA Soil Moisture Radar Ends Operations, Mission Science Continues
===================================================================

Date: 2015-09-02 21:09:00

[![NASA\'s Soil Moisture Active Passive (SMAP) (Artist
Concept)](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/smap/20150224/smap20150224-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-290&rn=news.xml&rst=4710)\
\
NASA SMAP observatory managers have determined its radar can no longer
return data, but the mission continues to produce high-quality science
with its radiometer instrument.
