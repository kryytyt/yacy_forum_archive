Hosts: [Randal Schwartz](https://twitter.com/merlyn) and [Gareth
Greenaway](https://twitter.com/garethgreenaway)

Solomon Hykes, CTO and chief architect of Docker, joins Randal and
Gareth this week to talk about Docker\'s 2nd birthday and the vast
ecosystem it has developed during the time. Docker is an open platform
for developers and sysadmins to build, ship, and run distributed
applications.
