NASA Juno Mission Prepares for December 11 Jupiter Flyby
========================================================

Date: 2016-12-09 21:12:00

[![This artist\'s rendering shows NASA\'s Juno spacecraft making one of
its close passes over
Jupiter.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/juno/20160506/PIA19639-226.jpg){width="100"

\
On Sunday, December 11, at 9:04 a.m. PST (12:04 p.m. EST, 17:04 UTC)
NASA\'s Juno spacecraft will make its third science flyby of Jupiter.
