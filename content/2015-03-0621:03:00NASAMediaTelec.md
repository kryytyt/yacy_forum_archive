NASA Media Telecon Today: First Spacecraft Successfully Enters Orbit Around a Dwarf Planet
==========================================================================================

Date: 2015-03-06 21:03:00

[![This artist\'s concept shows NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft heading toward

NASA will host a media teleconference at 11 a.m. PST (2 p.m. EST) today
to discuss the historic arrival of the agency\'s Dawn spacecraft at the
dwarf planet Ceres.
