Next NASA Mars Mission Reaches Milestone
========================================

Date: 2014-11-17 21:11:00

[![Work on NASA\'s InSight Lander Starts New
Phase](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/insight/20141117/pia18884-226.jpg){width="100"

NASA\'s InSight mission has begun its assembly, test and launch
operations phase, on track for a March 2016 launch to Mars.
