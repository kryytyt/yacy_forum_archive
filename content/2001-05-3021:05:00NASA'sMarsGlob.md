NASA\'s Mars Global Surveyor Captures Dust Storms
=================================================

Date: 2001-05-30 21:05:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion
Laboratory](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5861)\
\
Daily global maps, created with images from NASA\'s Mars Global Surveyor
spacecraft, provide a moving picture of Martian weather during 1999-2000
similar to the familiar satellite weather maps we see of Earth.
