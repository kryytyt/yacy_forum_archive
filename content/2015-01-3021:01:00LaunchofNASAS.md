Launch of NASA Soil Moisture Mapping Mission Set for Saturday
=============================================================

Date: 2015-01-30 21:01:00

[![United Launch Alliance Delta II rocket with the Soil Moisture Active
Passive (SMAP) observatory
onboard](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/smap/20150129/201501290001-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-045&rn=news.xml&rst=4470)\
\
NASA\'s Soil Moisture Active Passive mission (SMAP) now is scheduled to
launch from Vandenberg Air Force Base, California at 6:20 a.m. PST (9:20
a.m. EST) Saturday, Jan. 31.
