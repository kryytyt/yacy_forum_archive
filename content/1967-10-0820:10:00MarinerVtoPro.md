Mariner V to Probe Venus Atmosphere, Heat
=========================================

Date: 1967-10-08 20:10:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"

Venus, the cloud-shrouded mystery planet, may yield some of her secrets
in mid-October to Mariner V experimenters at Caltech\'s Jet Propulsion
Laboratory and several allied installations.
