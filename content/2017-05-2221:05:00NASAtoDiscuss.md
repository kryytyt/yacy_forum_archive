NASA to Discuss First Science Results from Juno Jupiter Mission
===============================================================

Date: 2017-05-22 21:05:00

[![This illustration depicts NASA\'s Juno spacecraft approaching
Jupiter](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/juno/20160630b/PIA20702-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-146&rn=news.xml&rst=6852)\
\
Scientists from NASA\'s Juno mission to Jupiter will discuss their first
in-depth science results in a media telecon at 11 a.m. PDT (2 p.m. EDT)
Thursday, May 25.
