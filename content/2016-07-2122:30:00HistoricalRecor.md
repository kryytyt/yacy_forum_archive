Historical Records Miss a Fifth of Global Warming: NASA
=======================================================

Date: 2016-07-21 22:30:00

[![Difficulties in making weather measurements in the
Arctic.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/arctic/20160721/earth20160721-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-194&rn=news.xml&rst=6576)\
\
A new NASA-led study finds that almost one-fifth of the global warming
that has occurred in the past 150 years has been missed by historical
records due to quirks in how global temperatures were recorded.
