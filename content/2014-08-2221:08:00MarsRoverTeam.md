Mars Rover Team Chooses Not to Drill \'Bonanza King\'
=====================================================

Date: 2014-08-22 21:08:00

[![](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20140822/pia18603-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-286&rn=news.xml&rst=4268)\
\
The team operating NASA\'s Curiosity Mars rover has chosen not to
proceed with drilling at a target rock called \"Bonanza King\" after the
rock budged when tapped by the drill.
