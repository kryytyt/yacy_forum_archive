NASA: California Tuolumne Snowpack 40 Percent of Worst Year
===========================================================

Date: 2015-04-01 21:04:00

[![Deficit in the total volume of water contained within the Tuolumne
River Basin snowpack from this time in 2014 to
now.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20150401/earth20150401c-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-111&rn=news.xml&rst=4534)\
\
New NASA data find the snowpack in the Tuolumne River Basin currently
contains just 40 percent as much water as it did near this time at its
highest level of 2014.
