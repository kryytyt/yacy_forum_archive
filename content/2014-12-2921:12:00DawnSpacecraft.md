Dawn Spacecraft Begins Approach to Dwarf Planet Ceres
=====================================================

Date: 2014-12-29 21:12:00

[![On the Way to
Ceres](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20131203/pia17650-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-443&rn=news.xml&rst=4425)\
\
NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft has entered an approach phase in which it will
continue to close in on Ceres, a Texas-sized dwarf planet never before
visited by a spacecraft.
