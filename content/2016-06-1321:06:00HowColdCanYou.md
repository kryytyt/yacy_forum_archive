How Cold Can You Go? Cooler Tested for NASA Telescope
=====================================================

Date: 2016-06-13 21:06:00

[![Technicians inspect the cooler for the Mid-Infrared Instrument, or
MIRI](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/miri/20160613/pia20687-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-148&rn=news.xml&rst=6531)\
\
A first-of-its-kind cooler for NASA\'s James Webb Space Telescope,
scheduled to launch in 2018, has completed testing at NASA\'s Jet
Propulsion Laboratory in Pasadena, California.
