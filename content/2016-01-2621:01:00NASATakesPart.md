NASA Takes Part in Airborne Study of Southern Ocean
===================================================

Date: 2016-01-26 21:01:00

[![The ORCAS campaign is studying carbon dioxide in the sea around
Antarctica.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20160126/earth20160126-226.jpg){width="100"

\
NASA\'s PRISM instrument is part of a flying lab that is studying the
Southern Ocean\'s appetite for carbon dioxide.
