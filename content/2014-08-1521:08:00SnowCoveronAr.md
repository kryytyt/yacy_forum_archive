Snow Cover on Arctic Sea Ice Has Thinned 30 to 50 Percent
=========================================================

Date: 2014-08-15 21:08:00

[![Matthew Sturm of the University of Alaska Fairbanks, a co-author of
this
study](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/arctic/20140815/earth20140815-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-281&rn=news.xml&rst=4264)\
\
New research led by NASA and the University of Washington, Seattle,
confirms that springtime snow on Arctic sea ice has thinned
significantly in the last 50 years.
