Prolific Mars Orbiter Completes 50,000 Orbits
=============================================

Date: 2017-03-29 21:03:00

[![Images of
Mars](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20170329/PIA21487-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-094&rn=news.xml&rst=6800)\
\
The most data-productive Mars spacecraft passed orbit 50,000 this week,
continuing to compile the most sharp-eyed global coverage ever gathered
by a camera at the Red Planet.
