Cassini Spacecraft Makes Its Final Approach to Saturn
=====================================================

Date: 2017-09-13 21:09:00

[![NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft is shown heading for the gap between
Saturn](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20170829/PIA21438-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-240&rn=news.xml&rst=6945)\
\
NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft is on final approach to Saturn, following
confirmation by navigators that it is on course to dive into the
planet\'s atmosphere on Friday, Sept. 15.
