5,000 Days on Mars; Solar-Powered Rover Approaching 5,000th Martian Dawn
========================================================================

Date: 2018-02-15 21:02:00

[![NASA\'s Mars Exploration Rover Opportunity recorded the dawn of the

The Sun will rise on NASA\'s solar-powered Mars rover Opportunity for
the 5,000th time on Saturday, sending rays of energy to a robot that
continues to provide revelations.
