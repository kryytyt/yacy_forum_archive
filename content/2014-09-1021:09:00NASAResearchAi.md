NASA Research Aids Response to California Napa Quake
====================================================

Date: 2014-09-10 21:09:00

[![NASA\'s UAVSAR Studies Ground Deformation from Napa California
Quake](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/uavsar/20140910/pia18801-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-306&rn=news.xml&rst=4290)\
\
NASA data and expertise are proving invaluable in California\'s ongoing
response to the Aug. 24 magnitude 6.0 earthquake in Napa Valley,
northeast of San Francisco.
