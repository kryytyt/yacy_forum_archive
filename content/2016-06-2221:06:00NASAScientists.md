NASA Scientists Discover Unexpected Mineral on Mars
===================================================

Date: 2016-06-22 21:06:00

[![Looking Up at Mars Rover Curiosity in \'Buckskin\'
Selfie](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20160622/PIA19808-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-157&rn=news.xml&rst=6540)\
\
Scientists have discovered an unexpected mineral in a rock sample at
Gale Crater on Mars, a finding that may alter our understanding of how
the planet evolved.
