Local High Schools Win Robotics Competition
===========================================

Date: 2016-03-14 20:03:00

[![Sixty-six teams competed at the Los Angeles Regional FIRST Robotics
Competition on March 11 and
12.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/first/first2016/robotics20160314-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-072&rn=news.xml&rst=6110)\
\
Proving their robots have what it takes to storm a castle, an alliance
of three high schools won the Los Angeles regional FIRST Robotics
Competition on March 12.
