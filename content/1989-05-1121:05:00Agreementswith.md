Agreements with Nations\'s HBCU\'s Formalized
=============================================

Date: 1989-05-11 21:05:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5848)\
\
Citing \"an expression of our mutual intent to increase our common
interests,\" Dr. Lew Allen, director of the NASA/Caltech Jet Propulsion
Laboratory, Tuesday (May 9) formalized set of agreements with seven of
the nation\'s Historically Black Colleges and Universities (HBCU\'s).
