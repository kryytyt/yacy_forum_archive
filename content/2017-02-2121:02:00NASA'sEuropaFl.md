NASA\'s Europa Flyby Mission Moves into Design Phase
====================================================

Date: 2017-02-21 21:02:00

[![This artist\'s rendering shows NASA\'s Europa mission
spacecraft.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/europa/20170221/PIA20025-226.jpg){width="100"

\
A mission to examine the habitability of Jupiter\'s ocean-bearing moon
Europa is taking one step closer to the launchpad, with the recent
completion of a major NASA review.
