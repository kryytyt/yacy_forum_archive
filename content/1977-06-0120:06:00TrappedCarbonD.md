Trapped Carbon Dioxide Key to Mar\'s Drastic Climate
====================================================

Date: 1977-06-01 20:06:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6052)\
\
A large reservoir of carbon dioxide trapped in the soil of Mars may
account for apparent drastic changes in the red planet\'s climate over
billions of years, two scientists at Caltech\'s Jet Propulsion
Laboratory told their colleagues today.
