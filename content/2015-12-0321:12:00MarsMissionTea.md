Mars Mission Team Addressing Vacuum Leak on Key Science Instrument
==================================================================

Date: 2015-12-03 21:12:00

[![This artist\'s concept from August 2015 depicts NASA\'s InSight Mars
lander](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/insight/20151203/pia19811-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-361&rn=news.xml&rst=4782)\
\
A key science instrument for NASA\'s InSight spacecraft, being prepared
for a March 2016 launch, is experiencing a leak in the vacuum container
carrying its main sensors.
