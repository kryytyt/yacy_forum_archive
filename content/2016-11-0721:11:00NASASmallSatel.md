NASA Small Satellites Will Take a Fresh Look at Earth
=====================================================

Date: 2016-11-07 21:11:00

[![One of eight microsatellites in the CYGNSS constellation under
construction](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cubesat/20161107/cubesat20161107-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-290&rn=news.xml&rst=6671)\
\
NASA is launching six next-generation, Earth-observing small satellite
missions to demonstrate innovative approaches for studying our changing
planet.
