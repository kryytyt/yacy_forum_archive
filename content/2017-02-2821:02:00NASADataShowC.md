NASA Data Show California\'s San Joaquin Valley Still Sinking
=============================================================

Date: 2017-02-28 21:02:00

[![Total subsidence in California\'s San Joaquin
Valley](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20170228/earth20170228-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-054&rn=news.xml&rst=6761)\
\
Areas of California\'s San Joaquin Valley continue to sink in response
to the state\'s drought, finds a new update to a 2015 JPL report
commissioned by the state of California.
