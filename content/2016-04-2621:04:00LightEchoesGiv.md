Light Echoes Give Clues to Protoplanetary Disk
==============================================

Date: 2016-04-26 21:04:00

[![This illustration shows a star surrounded by a protoplanetary
disk](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/spitzer/20160426/PIA20645-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-113&rn=news.xml&rst=6439)\
\
Astronomers used echoes of light to determine the distance from a star
to the inner wall of its surrounding protoplanetary disk.
