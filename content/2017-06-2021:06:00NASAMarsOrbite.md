NASA Mars Orbiter Views Rover Climbing Mount Sharp
==================================================

Date: 2017-06-20 21:06:00

[![Curiosity Rover on Mount Sharp, Seen from Mars
Orbit](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20170620/PIA21710-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-169&rn=news.xml&rst=6875)\
\
Using the most powerful telescope ever sent to Mars, NASA\'s Mars
Reconnaissance Orbiter caught a view of the Curiosity rover this month
amid rocky mountainside terrain.
