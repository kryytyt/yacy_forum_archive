NASA Studying Shared Venus Science Objectives with Russian Space Research Institute
===================================================================================

Date: 2017-03-10 21:03:00

[![Venus orbiter that would operate for up to three
years](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/venus/20170310/Venus20170310-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-067&rn=news.xml&rst=6774)\
\
NASA-sponsored scientists will meet with the Russian Academy of
Sciences\' Space Research Institute (IKI) next week to continue work on
a Joint Science Definition Team study focused on identifying shared
science objectives for Venus exploration.
