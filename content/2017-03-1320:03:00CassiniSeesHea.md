Cassini Sees Heat Below the Icy Surface of Enceladus
====================================================

Date: 2017-03-13 20:03:00

[![This enhanced-color Cassini view of southern latitudes on
Enceladus](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20170313/PIA07800-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-068&rn=news.xml&rst=6775)\
\
A new study from NASA\'s Cassini mission shows that Saturn\'s moon
Enceladus is warmer than expected just a few feet below its icy surface.
