JPL/Caltech Arts And Crafts Fair
================================

Date: 1988-09-21 21:09:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5826)\

Painters, potters, sculptors, glass blowers and wood carvers will be
among exhibitors at an arts and crafts fair benefitting the Jet
Propulsion Laboratory/Caltech Child Educational Center.
