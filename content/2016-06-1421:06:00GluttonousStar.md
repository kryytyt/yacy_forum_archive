Gluttonous Star May Hold Clues to Planet Formation
==================================================

Date: 2016-06-14 21:06:00

[![](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/spitzer/20160614/pia20689-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-151&rn=news.xml&rst=6534)\
\
Astronomers have gained a new perspective on the behavior of outbursting
star FU Orionis, using data from an airborne observatory and a space
telescope.
