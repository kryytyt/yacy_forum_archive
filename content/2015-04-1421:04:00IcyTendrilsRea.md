Icy Tendrils Reaching into Saturn Ring Traced to Their Source
=============================================================

Date: 2015-04-14 21:04:00

[![Simulations of the
Tendrils](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20150414/pia17191-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-129&rn=news.xml&rst=4551)\
\
Long, sinuous, tendril-like structures seen near Enceladus originate
directly from its geysers, according to scientists studying images from
NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft.
