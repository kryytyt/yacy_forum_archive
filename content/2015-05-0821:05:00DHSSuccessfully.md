DHS Successfully Transitions Search and Rescue Tool That Pinpoints Buried Victims
=================================================================================

Date: 2015-05-08 21:05:00

[![Rubble from the earthquake in
Nepal](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/earthquake/20150508/kathmandu20150508MAIN-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-160&rn=news.xml&rst=4580)\
\
The Department of Homeland Security Science and Technology Directorate,
in partnership with NASA\'s Jet Propulsion Laboratory, announced the
transition of the final prototype of the Finding Individuals for
Disaster and Emergency Response (FINDER) technology to the commercial
market.
