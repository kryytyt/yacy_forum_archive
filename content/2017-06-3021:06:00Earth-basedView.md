Earth-based Views of Jupiter to Enhance Juno Flyby
==================================================

Date: 2017-06-30 21:06:00

[![Animation of
Jupiter](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/juno/20170630b/pia21713-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-185&rn=news.xml&rst=6889)\
\
Telescopes in Hawaii have obtained new images of Jupiter and its Great
Red Spot, which will assist the first-ever close-up study of the Great
Red Spot, planned for July 10.
