NASA Mars Spacecraft Prepare for Close Comet Flyby
==================================================

Date: 2014-07-25 21:07:00

[![This graphic depicts the orbit of comet C/2013 A1 Siding
Spring](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/comet/20140725/SidingSpring-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-244&rn=news.xml&rst=4230)\
\
NASA is taking steps to protect its Mars orbiters, while preserving
opportunities to gather valuable science, as Comet C/2013 A1 Siding
Spring nears Mars on Oct. 19.
