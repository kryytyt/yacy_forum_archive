Latest Selfie from NASA Mars Rover Shows Wide Context
=====================================================

Date: 2015-02-24 21:02:00

[![Curiosity Self-Portrait at \'Mojave\' Site on Mount
Sharp](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20150224/pia19142-226.jpg){width="100"

NASA\'s Curiosity Mars rover used the camera on its robotic arm to
record the pieces of a portrait that shows the rover within a panorama
of the area it is studying.
