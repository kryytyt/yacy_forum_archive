Rosetta\'s First Peek at the Comet\'s Dark Side
===============================================

Date: 2015-10-01 21:10:00

[![Taking a Comet\'s
Temperature](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/rosetta/20151001/rosetta20151001b-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-307&rn=news.xml&rst=4728)\
\
Using the Microwave for Rosetta Orbiter (MIRO) instrument aboard
Rosetta, scientists have studied the southern polar regions of its
target comet at the end of their long winter season.
