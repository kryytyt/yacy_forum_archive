NASA\'s Juno Spacecraft Getting Close to Jupiter
================================================

Date: 2016-07-01 21:07:00

[![This illustration depicts NASA\'s Juno spacecraft at Jupiter, with
its solar arrays and main antenna pointed toward the distant sun and
Earth.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/juno/20160701/pia20703-226.jpg){width="100"

\
NASA\'s Juno mission, launched nearly five years ago, will soon reach
its final destination: the most massive planet in our solar system,
Jupiter.
