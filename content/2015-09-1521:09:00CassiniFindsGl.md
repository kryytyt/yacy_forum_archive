Cassini Finds Global Ocean in Saturn\'s Moon Enceladus
======================================================

Date: 2015-09-15 21:09:00

[![Global Ocean on Enceladus (Artist\'s
Rendering)](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20150915/PIA19656-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-298&rn=news.xml&rst=4718)\
\
A global ocean lies beneath the icy crust of Saturn\'s geologically
active moon Enceladus, according to new research using data from NASA\'s
Cassini mission.
