Great news for schizophrenics!
==============================

Date: 2015-02-07 03:42:23

[![](http://www.jwz.org/images/they-live.jpg)Samsung: watch what you say
in front of our TVs, they\'re sending your words to third
parties](http://boingboing.net/2015/02/06/samsung-watch-what-you-say-in.html)

> Part of the Samsung Smarttv EULA: \"Please be aware that if your
> spoken words include personal or other sensitive information, that
> information will be among the data captured and transmitted to a third
> party through your use of Voice Recognition.\"
>
> This is part of their speech-recognition tech, which uses third
> parties (whose privacy policies Samsung doesn\'t make any
> representations about) to turn your words into text.
