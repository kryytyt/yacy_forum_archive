NASA Releases Tool Enabling Citizen Scientists to Examine Asteroid Vesta
========================================================================

Date: 2015-03-31 21:03:00

[![Viewof Vesta Trek\'s
interface](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20150331/vesta20150331-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-110&rn=news.xml&rst=4531)\
\
NASA has announced the release of Vesta Trek, a free, web-based
application that provides detailed visualizations of Vesta, one of the
largest asteroids in our solar system.
