Mars Gullies Likely Not Formed by Liquid Water
==============================================

Date: 2016-07-29 21:07:00

[![Adding Composition Data About Mars
Gullies](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20160729/PIA20763-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-198&rn=news.xml&rst=6580)\
\
New findings using data from NASA\'s Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter show
that gullies on modern Mars are likely not being formed by flowing
liquid water.
