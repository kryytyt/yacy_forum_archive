Mariner IV Sets New Communications Distance Record
==================================================

Date: 1965-04-29 20:04:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6071)\
\
The Mars-bound Mariner IV spacecraft early today established a new
United States and world communications distance record when it reached a
point in space more than 66 million miles from Earth, the Jet Propulsion
Laboratory reported.
