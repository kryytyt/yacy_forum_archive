NASA\'s Juno Spacecraft in Orbit Around Mighty Jupiter
======================================================

Date: 2016-07-04 21:07:00

[![Juno team celebrates at NASA\'s Jet Propulsion Laboratory in
Pasadena,
California](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/juno/20160704/juno_hug-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-176&rn=news.xml&rst=6558)\
\
After an almost five-year journey to the solar system\'s largest planet,
NASA\'s Juno spacecraft successfully entered Jupiter\'s orbit during a
35-minute engine burn.
