Similar-Looking Ridges on Mars Have Diverse Origins
===================================================

Date: 2017-01-25 21:01:00

[![This view from the HiRISE camera on NASA\'s Mars Reconnaissance
Orbiter.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20170125/pia21264-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-015&rn=news.xml&rst=6725)\
\
Thin, blade-like walls, some as tall as a 16-story building, dominate a
previously undocumented network of intersecting ridges on Mars, found in
images from NASA\'s Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter.
