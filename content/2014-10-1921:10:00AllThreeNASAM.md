All Three NASA Mars Orbiters Healthy After Comet Flyby
======================================================

Date: 2014-10-19 21:10:00

[![Artist\'s concept showing NASA\'s Mars orbiters lining up behind the
Red
Planet](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/20141019/comet-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-364&rn=news.xml&rst=4347)\
\
All three NASA orbiters around Mars confirmed their healthy status
Sunday after each took shelter behind Mars during a period of risk from
dust released by a passing comet.
