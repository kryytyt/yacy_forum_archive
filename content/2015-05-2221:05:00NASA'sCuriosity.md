NASA\'s Curiosity Rover Adjusts Route Up Martian Mountain
=========================================================

Date: 2015-05-22 21:05:00

[![Unfavorable Terrain for Crossing Near \'Logan
Pass\'](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20150522/pia19662-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-176&rn=news.xml&rst=4596)\
\
NASA\'s Curiosity Mars rover climbed a hill Thursday to approach an
alternative site for investigating a geological boundary, after a
comparable site proved hard to reach.
