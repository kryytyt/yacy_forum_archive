Date: 2017-07-06 08:29:24

[![](https://www.jwz.org/images/knoxville_jameson_01.jpg)Thank you to my

> I wanted to show him the keys or reach into my bag for the
> registration and bill of sale. I fought every impulse to do anything
> that would make him feel threatened. I don\'t have de-escalation
> training. I\'m the one being held at gunpoint. I\'m the one thinking
> my life could end if he panics. Yet, I\'m the one expected to remain
> calm.
>
> It seems that the legal system is really asking civilians to
> de-escalate adrenaline-fueled cops. We must remain calm while facing a
> loaded gun while the trained officers can panic and overreact.
>
> What about our lives? Who protects us from the people who are supposed
> to protect us?
