Complete Land Mobile Communications System Tested
=================================================

Date: 1989-09-18 21:09:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5870)\
\
A complete, end-to-end system for land mobile communications has been
field-tested for the first time by researchers from NASA\'s Jet
Propulsion Laboratory.
