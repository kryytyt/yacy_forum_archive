Jupiter\'s Extended Family? A Billion or More
=============================================

Date: 2016-08-26 21:08:00

[![Comparing Jupiter with Jupiter-like planets that orbit other stars
can teach us about those distant
worlds.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/juno/20160826/exoJupiter20160826-226.jpg){width="100"

The giant planet Jupiter could hold the secret to the formation of the
solar system, of Earth itself, and of a billion or more \"Jupiters\"
orbiting distant stars.
