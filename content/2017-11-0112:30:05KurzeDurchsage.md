Kurze Durchsage von Papst Franziskus:"When I pray, \...
=======================================================

Date: 2017-11-01 12:30:05

[Kurze Durchsage von Papst
Franziskus](https://www.theguardian.com/world/2017/oct/31/pope-francis-admits-when-i-pray-sometimes-i-fall-asleep):

> "When I pray, sometimes I fall asleep," he said in an episode of a
> Catholic TV2000 television programme published Tuesday on YouTube.
>
> "Saint Therese did it too," he said in reference to a 19th-century
> French nun, adding that Christians were called to feel like children
> lying in their fathers' arms -- a place conducive to napping, he
> implies.
