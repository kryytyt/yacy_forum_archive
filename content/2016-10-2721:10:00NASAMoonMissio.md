NASA Moon Mission Shares Insights into Giant Impacts
====================================================

Date: 2016-10-27 21:10:00

[![Orientale basin is about 580 miles (930 kilometers) wide and has
three distinct
rings.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/grail/20161027/grail20161027-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-281&rn=news.xml&rst=6662)\
\
New results from NASA\'s GRAIL mission yield insights into the huge
impacts that dominated the early history of Earth\'s moon and other
solid worlds in our solar system.
