DHS and NASA Technology Helps Save Four in Nepal Earthquake Disaster
====================================================================

Date: 2015-05-05 21:05:00

[![The village of Chautara, Nepal as seen from space. Image credit:
Google
Earth](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/earthquake/20150505/finder20150505-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-153&rn=news.xml&rst=4574)\
\
Four men trapped under up to 10 feet of bricks, mud and other debris
have been rescued in Nepal thanks to new search-and-rescue technology
developed in partnership by the Department of Homeland Security\'s
Science and Technology Directorate and JPL.
