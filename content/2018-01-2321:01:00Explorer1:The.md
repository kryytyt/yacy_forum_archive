Explorer 1: The Beginning of American Space Science
===================================================

Date: 2018-01-23 21:01:00

[![A vintage JPL graphic celebrating the Explorer 1
satellite](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/explorer/20180122/PIA04601-226.jpg){width="100"

\
Sixty years ago next week, America launched its first satellite, the
JPL-built Explorer 1, marking the beginning of the scientific
exploration of space.
