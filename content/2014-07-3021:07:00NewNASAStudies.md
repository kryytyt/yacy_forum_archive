New NASA Studies to Examine Climate/Vegetation Links
====================================================

Date: 2014-07-30 21:07:00

[![Two new spaceborne Earth-observing instruments will help scientists
better understand how global
forests](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/climate/20140730/earth20140730-226.jpg){width="100"

NASA has selected a proposal from JPL for a new International Space
Station instrument that will observe effects on global vegetation caused
by changes in climate or land use.
