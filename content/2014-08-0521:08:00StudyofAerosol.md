Study of Aerosols Stands to Improve Climate Models
==================================================

Date: 2014-08-05 21:08:00

[![Low-level clouds along the California coast are visible in this July
26, 2014
image](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20140805/earth20140805-226.jpg){width="100"

A new JPL/California Institute of Technology study examines how changes
in the level of tiny particles in our atmosphere affect a key type of
clouds that help cool our planet.
