NASA\'s Europa Mission Begins with Selection of Science Instruments
===================================================================

Date: 2015-05-26 21:05:00

[![This artist\'s rendering shows a concept for a future NASA mission to
Europa](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/europa/20150526/europa_clipper-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-178&rn=news.xml&rst=4598)\
\
NASA has selected nine science instruments for a mission to Jupiter\'s
moon Europa, to investigate whether the mysterious icy moon could harbor
conditions suitable for life.
