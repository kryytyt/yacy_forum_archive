Dawn In Excellent Shape One Month After Ceres Arrival
=====================================================

Date: 2015-04-06 21:04:00

[![Artist\'s concept of Dawn above Ceres around the time it was captured
into orbit by the dwarf planet in early
March.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20150406/pia18922-226.jpg){width="100"

\
Since its capture by the gravity of dwarf planet Ceres on March 6,
NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft has performed flawlessly, continuing to thrust
with its ion engine as planned.
