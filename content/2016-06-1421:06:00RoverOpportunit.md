Rover Opportunity Wrapping up Study of Martian Valley
=====================================================

Date: 2016-06-14 21:06:00

[![](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mer/20160614/pia20749-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-153&rn=news.xml&rst=6536)\
\
\"Marathon Valley,\" slicing through a large crater\'s rim on Mars, has
provided fruitful research targets for NASA\'s Opportunity rover since
July 2015, but the rover may soon move on.
