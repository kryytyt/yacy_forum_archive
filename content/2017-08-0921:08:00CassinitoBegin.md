Cassini to Begin Final Five Orbits Around Saturn
================================================

Date: 2017-08-09 21:08:00

[![Artist concept of Cassini at
Saturn](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20170809/cassini20170809-226.jpg){width="100"

\
NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft is about to embark on a set of ultra-close
passes through Saturn\'s upper atmosphere with its final five orbits
around the planet.
