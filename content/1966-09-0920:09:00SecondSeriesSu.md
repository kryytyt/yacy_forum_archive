Second Series Surveyor Launch
=============================

Date: 1966-09-09 20:09:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6080)\
\
The second of a series of Surveyor spacecraft, designed to soft land on
the moon, will be launched from Cape Kennedy during a launch period
beginning Tuesday, September 20, and ending Friday, September 23.
