Chlorine Linked to Annual Depletion of Atmospheric Ozone
========================================================

Date: 1987-09-10 21:09:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5780)\
\
Scientists have discovered new evidence linking chlorine to the annual
depletion of atmospheric ozone over the Antarctic, it was reported today
in the British science journal Nature.
