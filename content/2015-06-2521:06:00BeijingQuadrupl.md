Beijing Quadrupled in Size in a Decade, NASA Finds
==================================================

Date: 2015-06-25 21:06:00

[![Beijing city
center.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20150625/beijing20150625-226.gif){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-220&rn=news.xml&rst=4641)\
\
Using a NASA satellite-based technique to measure urban growth,
researchers found that Beijing, China, quadrupled in physical size
between 2000 and 2009.
