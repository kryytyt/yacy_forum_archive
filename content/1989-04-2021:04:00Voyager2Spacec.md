Voyager 2 Spacecraft Completes Last Change-of-Course Maneuvers
==============================================================

Date: 1989-04-20 21:04:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5845)\
\
NASA\'s Voyager 2 spacecraft, only four months away from its flyby of
the planet Neptune, today completed one of the last major
change-of-course maneuvers it will make before reaching the planet in
August.
