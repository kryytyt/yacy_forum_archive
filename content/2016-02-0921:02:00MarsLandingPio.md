Mars Landing Pioneer Elected to National Academy of Engineering
===============================================================

Date: 2016-02-09 21:02:00

[![Adam Steltzner of JPL holds up a model of a Mars entry
capsule.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/people/AdamSteltzner/Steltzner-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-038&rn=news.xml&rst=5031)\
\
Adam Steltzner, a JPL engineer who helped pioneer the breakthrough
technique for landing a one-ton rover on Mars, has been elected into the
National Academy of Engineering.
