Two-way Digital Voice Terminal Demonstrated
===========================================

Date: 1989-04-20 21:04:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5844)\
\
A two-way digital voice terminal for land, aeronautical or maritime
mobile communications has been demonstrated in the field for the first
time by Jet Propulsion Laboratory researchers.
