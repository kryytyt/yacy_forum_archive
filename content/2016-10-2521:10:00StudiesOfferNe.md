Studies Offer New Glimpse of Melting Under Antarctic Glaciers
=============================================================

Date: 2016-10-25 21:10:00

[![A view from Operation IceBridge\'s aircraft of Crosson Ice Shelf,
foreground. Mt. Murphy is in the
background.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/antarctica/20161025/antarctica20161025-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-279&rn=news.xml&rst=6609)\
\
Two new studies offer a direct view of rapid melting from the undersides
of glaciers in West Antarctica, with implications for future rates of
global sea level rise.
