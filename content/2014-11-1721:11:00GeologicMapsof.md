Geologic Maps of Vesta from NASA\'s Dawn Mission Published
==========================================================

Date: 2014-11-17 21:11:00

[![Geological Map of
Vesta](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20141117/pia18788-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-399&rn=news.xml&rst=4381)\
\
Images from NASA\'s Dawn Mission have been used to create a series of
high-resolution geological maps of the large asteroid Vesta, revealing
the variety of surface features in unprecedented detail.
