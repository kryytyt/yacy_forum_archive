Star Explosion is Lopsided, Finds NASA\'s NuSTAR
================================================

Date: 2015-05-07 21:05:00

[![Witnessing a Star\'s Explosive
Death](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/nustar/20150507/1987A-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-156&rn=news.xml&rst=4577)\
\
NASA\'s NuSTAR, has found evidence that a massive star exploded in a
lopsided fashion, sending ejected material flying in one direction and
the core of the star in the other.
