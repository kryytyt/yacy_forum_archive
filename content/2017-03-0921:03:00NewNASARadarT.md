New NASA Radar Technique Finds Lost Lunar Spacecraft
====================================================

Date: 2017-03-09 21:03:00

[![Deep Space
Network](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/moon/20170309/DSN_main-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-062&rn=news.xml&rst=6769)\
\
A new technological application of interplanetary radar pioneered by JPL
scientists has successfully located spacecraft orbiting the moon \-- one
active, and one dormant.
