Numero Uno in Space: Nasa Unveils Web Site In Spanish
=====================================================

Date: 2001-01-05 21:01:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion
Laboratory](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6393)\
\
Spanish-speaking space fans can hitch a ride via the Internet on an
orbiting space observatory, thanks to the first-ever Spanish-language
web site tied to a NASA mission.
