Close Views Show Saturn\'s Rings in Unprecedented Detail
========================================================

Date: 2017-01-30 21:01:00

[![Saturn\'s A
ring](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20170130/PIA21060-226.jpg){width="100"

\
Newly released images showcase the incredible closeness with which
NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft, now in its \"Ring-Grazing\" orbits phase, is
observing Saturn\'s dazzling rings of icy debris.
