A \'Comet\' Lands in Pasadena
=============================

Date: 2014-10-02 21:10:00

[![The interactive comet
sculpture](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/asteroid/20141002/comet20141002-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-337&rn=news.xml&rst=4320)\
\
JPL is participating in AxS 2014, an arts and sciences celebration in
Pasadena, California, which includes an interactive comet sculpture on
display through Oct. 5.
