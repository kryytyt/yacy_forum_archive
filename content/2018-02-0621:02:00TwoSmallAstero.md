Two Small Asteroids Safely Pass Earth This Week
===============================================

Date: 2018-02-06 21:02:00

[![Asteroid 2018 CB will pass closely by Earth on Friday, Feb.
9](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/asteroid/20180206/2018CBangle-226.jpg){width="100"

Two small recently discovered asteroids are safely passing by Earth
within one lunar distance this week.
