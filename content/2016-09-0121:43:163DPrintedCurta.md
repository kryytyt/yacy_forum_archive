Not exactly \"actual size\" \-- looks like 4x or 5x \-- but wow, I am
amazed this thing works at all! Look at how grainy and fibrous the print
is. This feels like someone trying to replicate a mechanical wrist watch
using flint tools. Metallurgy? Who needs it. This is as bonkers as
[\"Transistor-level Simulation 6502 in
Javascript\"](https://www.jwz.org/blog/2010/09/visual-transistor-level-simulation-of-the-6502-in-javascript/)
or [\"Octophant, I Should Not
Be\".](https://www.jwz.org/blog/2007/11/i-for-one-welcome-our-new-octophant-overlords/)

Do you feel cheated every time you see something that is \"3d printed\"
but is full of steel screws, springs and bearings? I do. Like, \"It\'s a
paper airplane! Except for the internal combustion engine.\" The lambda
that is not self-hosting is not the one true lambda.
