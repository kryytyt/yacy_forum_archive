Jupiter\'s North Pole Unlike Anything Encountered in Solar System
=================================================================

Date: 2016-09-02 21:09:00

[![Jupiter](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/juno/20160902/PIA21030-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-231&rn=news.xml&rst=6613)\
\
NASA\'s Juno spacecraft has sent back the first-ever images of
Jupiter\'s north pole, taken during the spacecraft\'s first flyby of the
planet with its instruments switched on.
