NASA\'s Juno Mission Exits Safe Mode, Performs Trim Maneuver
============================================================

Date: 2016-10-25 21:10:00

[![This artist\'s rendering shows NASA\'s Juno spacecraft making one of
its close passes over
Jupiter.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/juno/20160506/PIA19639-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=&rn=news.xml&rst=6661)\
\
NASA\'s Juno spacecraft at Jupiter has left safe mode and has
successfully completed a minor burn of its thruster engines in
preparation for its next close flyby of Jupiter.
