GRACE Mission Making Plans for Final Science Data Collection
============================================================

Date: 2017-09-14 21:09:00

[![Illustration of the Gravity Recovery and Climate Experiment (GRACE)
twin satellites in
orbit.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/grace/20170914/PIA04236-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-242&rn=news.xml&rst=6947)\
\
With one of its twin satellites almost out of fuel after 15 years
measuring Earth\'s gravity field, the U.S./German GRACE mission is
making plans for a final science collection.
