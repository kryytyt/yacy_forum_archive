Merging Galaxies Have Enshrouded Black Holes
============================================

Date: 2017-05-09 21:05:00

[![This illustration compares growing supermassive black holes in two
different kinds of
galaxies.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/nustar/20170509/nustar20170509-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-136&rn=news.xml&rst=6841)\
\
Galaxies that are far along in the merging process are completely
covered in a cocoon of gas and dust, a study using NuSTAR finds.
