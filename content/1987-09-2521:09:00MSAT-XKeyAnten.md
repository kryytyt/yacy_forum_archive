MSAT-X Key Antenna Design Tested Successfully
=============================================

Date: 1987-09-25 21:09:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"

play key role in the Laboratory\'s Mobile Satellite Experiment (MSAT-X)
program for NASA.
