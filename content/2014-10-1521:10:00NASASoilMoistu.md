NASA Soil Moisture Mapper Arrives at Launch Site
================================================

Date: 2014-10-15 21:10:00

[![The transportation canister containing NASA\'s Soil Moisture Active
Passive
(SMAP)](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/smap/20141015/smap20141015-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-355&rn=news.xml&rst=4338)\
\
A NASA spacecraft designed to track Earth\'s water in one of its most
important, but least recognized, forms \-- soil moisture \-- has arrived
at its California launch site.
