Curiosity Rover Enters Precautionary Safe Mode
==============================================

Date: 2016-07-06 21:07:00

[![Self-portrait of NASA\'s Curiosity Mars rover shows the vehicle at
the \'Okoruso\' drilling site on lower Mount Sharp\'s \'Naukluft
Plateau.\'](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20160613/pia20602-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-177&rn=news.xml&rst=6559)\
\
The team operating NASA\'s Curiosity Mars rover is taking steps to
return the rover to full activity following a precautionary stand-down
over the Fourth of July weekend.
