Date: 2015-07-03 07:06:10

half](http://www.dnalounge.com/calendar/2015/07-12.html), we\'re

[tickets](http://www.dnalounge.com/calendar/2015/07-12.html) yet? Let me
introduce you to our confirmed contestants so far:

[![](http://www.dnalounge.com/backstage/log/2015/07/robotdanceparty-thumb.jpg)](http://www.robotdanceparty.org/)
We have extended the registration deadline, so if you think you can pull
something together in time, you still have a shot!

We also have as our special guest DJ: **[Robot Dance
Party](http://www.robotdanceparty.org/)**, San Francisco\'s own
ambulatory sound system, recently [perp-walked out of Dolores
Park](http://www.sfist.com/2015/06/24/robot_dance_party_exit_interview.php)
for being just too awesome!

Here is our panel of celebrity judges for this year:

-   [Doug Dalton](https://www.facebook.com/dougdalton) is a craft
    cocktail expert, and co-owner of popular local watering holes
    [Bourbon and Branch](http://www.bourbonandbranch.com/),
    [Rickhouse](http://www.bourbonandbranch.com/), [Local
    Edition](http://www.localeditionsf.com/) and
    [Tradition](http://www.tradbar.com/).
-   [Ryan Spurlock](http://www.techshop.ws/tssf.html) is the general
    manager of [TechShop SF](http://www.techshop.ws/tssf.html), and an
    enthusiast of both robots and cocktails.
-   [Terry Sandin](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0761931/) is a robot
    builder and special effects expert known for his work on *Hannibal*,
    *The Passion of the Christ*, *Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas* and
    many others.

Hosted, of course, by the inimitable [MC
Kingfish](http://www.hubbarevue.com/) of [Hubba Hubba
Revue](http://www.hubbarevue.com/).
