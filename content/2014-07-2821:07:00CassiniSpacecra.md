Cassini Spacecraft Reveals 101 Geysers and More on Icy Saturn Moon
==================================================================

Date: 2014-07-28 21:07:00

[![Elevated View of Enceladus\' South
Pole](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20140728/pia17183-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-246&rn=news.xml&rst=4231)\
\
Scientists using mission data from NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft have
identified 101 distinct geysers erupting on Saturn\'s icy moon
Enceladus.
