New Study Brings Antarctic Ice Loss Into Sharper Focus
======================================================

Date: 2018-02-20 21:02:00

[![The flow of Antarctic
ice](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20180220/antarctic20180220-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2018-034&rn=news.xml&rst=7065)\
\
A NASA study based on an innovative technique for crunching torrents of
satellite data provides the clearest picture yet of changes in Antarctic
ice flow into the ocean.
