**Hosts:** [Randal Schwartz](http://twit.tv/floss) and [Gareth
Greenaway](http://twitter.com/garethgreenaway).

Buddycloud is a publish-subscribe architecture with realtime updates.\
Buddycloud publishers put, update and delete content in Buddycloud
channels. Changes are pushed out to all channel subscribers.

Simon believes that email is the one \'app\' that has changed
everything. He is driving buddycloud to be the next open communication
standard.
