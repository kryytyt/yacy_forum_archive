Network for the Detection of Stratospheric Change Prototype Successfully Tested
===============================================================================

Date: 1989-02-03 21:02:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5840)\
\
The prototype measuring station of NASA\'s proposed Network for the
Detection of Stratospheric Change has completed an important test
validating its laser remote sensing technique for measuring atmospheric
ozone. The data acquired by this station will help to determine trends
in the ozone concentration overhead.
