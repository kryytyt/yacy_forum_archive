NASA\'s Opportunity Mars Rover Passes Marathon Distance
=======================================================

Date: 2015-03-24 20:03:00

[![Opportunity\'s Approach to \'Marathon
Valley\'](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mer/2015-03-24/pia19155-16.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-097&rn=news.xml&rst=4521)\
\
NASA\'s Mars Exploration Rover Opportunity completed its first Mars
marathon Tuesday \-- 26.219 miles (42.195 kilometers) - with a finish
time of roughly 11 years and two months.
