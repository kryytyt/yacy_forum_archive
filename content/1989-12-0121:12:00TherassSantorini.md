Thera/Santorini Eruption
========================

Date: 1989-12-01 21:12:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5882)\
\
Three scientists at NASA\'s Jet Propulsion Laboratory and university
professor have dated the most powerful volcano eruption in recorded
history, the Thera/Santorini eruption, to the late 17th Century BC.
