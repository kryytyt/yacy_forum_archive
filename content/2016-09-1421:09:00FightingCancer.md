Fighting Cancer with Space Research
===================================

Date: 2016-09-14 21:09:00

[![From astronomy to cancer
research](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/technology/20160914/cancer20160914-226.jpg){width="100"

development of data science that originated in space exploration and now
supports cancer discoveries.
