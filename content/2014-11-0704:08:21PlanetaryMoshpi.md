**Exhibit A:\
How Jupiter protects the inner planets from the asteroid belt:**
:::

![](http://www.jwz.org/images/fte4ly9.gif)

Hilda asteroids are in a 3:2 resonance of mean motion with Jupiter. This
enables them to have aphelia up to the distance of Jupiter and not
beeing scattered. They form a typical triangle.
:::
