NASA Maps Thawed Areas Under Greenland Ice Sheet
================================================

Date: 2016-08-03 21:08:00

[![The Greenland Ice sheet discharging into
valleys.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/antarctica/20160803/earth20160803-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-202&rn=news.xml&rst=6584)\
\
NASA has helped produce the first map of thawing at the bottom of the
Greenland Ice Sheet \-- key information in better predicting how it will
react to a warming climate.
