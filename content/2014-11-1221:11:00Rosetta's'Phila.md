Rosetta\'s \'Philae\' Makes Historic First Landing on a Comet
=============================================================

Date: 2014-11-12 21:11:00

[![View of Comet from Lander During
Descent](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/rosetta/20141112/pia18874-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-394&rn=news.xml&rst=4375)\
\
After a daring seven-hour descent from its mother ship, the European
Space Agency\'s Philae lander achieves a space exploration milestone:
the first-ever landing on a comet.
