International Spacecraft Carrying NASA\'s Aquarius Instrument Ends Operations
=============================================================================

Date: 2015-06-17 21:06:00

[![Artist\'s concept of the Aquarius/SAC-D
spacecraft](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/aquarius/pia13971-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-207&rn=news.xml&rst=4628)\
\
Aquarius/SAC-D, an international Earth-observing mission that carries
NASA\'s Aquarius instrument, ended June 8 when an essential component of
the spacecraft power and attitude control system stopped operating.
