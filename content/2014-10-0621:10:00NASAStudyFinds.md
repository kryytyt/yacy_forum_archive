NASA Study Finds Earth\'s Ocean Abyss Has Not Warmed
====================================================

Date: 2014-10-06 21:10:00

[![anemones](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20141006/anemone-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-338&rn=news.xml&rst=4321)\
\
Earth\'s deep ocean has not warmed measurably since 2005, finds a new
NASA study, leaving unsolved the mystery of why global warming appears
to have slowed in recent years.
