Opportunity Mars Rover Goes Six-Wheeling up a Ridge
===================================================

Date: 2016-02-25 21:02:00

[![Steep \'Knudsen Ridge\' on Mars (Enhanced
Color)](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mer/2016-02-25/PIA20319-226.jpg){width="100"

NASA\'s senior Mars rover, Opportunity, is working adeptly in some of
the most challenging terrain of the vehicle\'s 12 years on Mars, on a
slope of about 30 degrees.
