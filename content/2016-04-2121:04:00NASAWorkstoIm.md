NASA Works to Improve Solar Electric Propulsion for Deep Space Exploration
==========================================================================

Date: 2016-04-21 21:04:00

[![Advanced solar electric
propulsion](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/technology/20160420/solar20160420-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-108&rn=news.xml&rst=6426)\
\
NASA has selected Aerojet Rocketdyne, Inc. to design/develop an advanced
electric propulsion system for deep space exploration missions like
NASA\'s Asteroid Redirect Mission.
