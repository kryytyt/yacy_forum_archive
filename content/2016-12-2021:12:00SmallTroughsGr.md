Small Troughs Growing on Mars May Become \'Spiders\'
====================================================

Date: 2016-12-20 21:12:00

[![Sequence of three HiRISE
images](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20161220/pia21257-226.gif){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-325&rn=news.xml&rst=6707)\
\
Erosion-carved troughs that grow and branch during multiple Martian
years may be infant versions of larger features known as Martian
\"spiders.\"
