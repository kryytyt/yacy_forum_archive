Artificial Intelligence, NASA Data Used to Discover Eighth Planet Circling Distant Star
=======================================================================================

Date: 2017-12-14 21:12:00

[![Artist\'s concept of Kepler-90
system](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/kepler/20171214/PIA22192-226.jpg){width="100"

\
Our solar system now is tied for most number of planets around a single
star, with the discovery of an eighth planet circling a Sun-like star
2,545 light years from Earth.
