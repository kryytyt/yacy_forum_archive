NASA\'s Summer Research on Sea Level Rise in Greenland
======================================================

Date: 2015-08-28 21:08:00

[![Laurence Smith (University of California, Los Angeles) deploys an

boat.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/ocean/20150828/greenland20150828-226.jpg){width="100"

Researchers camped on Greenland\'s ice sheet this summer studied streams
that form atop the ice and carry meltwater to the ocean, where it adds
to sea level rise.
