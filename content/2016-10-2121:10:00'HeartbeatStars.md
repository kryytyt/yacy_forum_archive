\'Heartbeat Stars\' Unlocked in New Study
=========================================

Date: 2016-10-21 21:10:00

[![This artist\'s concept depicts \'heartbeat stars,\' which have been
detected by NASA\'s Kepler Space Telescope and
others.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/kepler/20161021/PIA21075-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-277&rn=news.xml&rst=6659)\
\
Heartbeat stars got their name because if you were to map out their
brightness over time, the result looks like an electrocardiogram, a
graph of the electrical activity of the heart.
