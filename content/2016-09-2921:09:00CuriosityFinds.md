Curiosity Finds Evidence of Mars Crust Contributing to Atmosphere
=================================================================

Date: 2016-09-29 21:09:00

[![Isotopic Clues to Mars\' Crust-Atmosphere
Interactions](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20160929/pia20847-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-249&rn=news.xml&rst=6631)\
\
NASA\'s Curiosity rover has found evidence that chemistry in the surface
material on Mars contributed to the makeup of its atmosphere.
