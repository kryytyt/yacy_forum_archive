NASA Mission Named \'Europa Clipper\'
=====================================

Date: 2017-03-09 21:03:00

[![The puzzling, fascinating surface of Jupiter\'s icy moon Europa looms
large in this reprocessed color
view](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/jupiter/europa/20141121/pia19048-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-065&rn=news.xml&rst=6772)\
\
NASA\'s upcoming mission to investigate the habitability of Jupiter\'s
icy moon Europa now has a formal name: Europa Clipper.
