Venus Encounter
===============

Date: 1962-12-14 21:12:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6061)\
\
Man\'s first chance to obtain information from another planet will come
on December 14 when the Mariner II spacecraft passes approximately
21,000 miles from Venus.
