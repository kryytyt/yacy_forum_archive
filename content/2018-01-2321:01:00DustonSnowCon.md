Dust on Snow Controls Springtime River Rise in West
===================================================

Date: 2018-01-23 21:01:00

[![A coating of dust on snow speeds the pace of snowmelt in the
spring](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20180123/earth20180123-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2018-015&rn=news.xml&rst=7045)\
\
The amount of dust on the snowpack, not the warmth of the spring air,
controls the pace of spring snowmelt flowing into the Colorado River.
