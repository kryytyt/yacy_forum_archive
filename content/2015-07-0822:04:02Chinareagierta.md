China reagiert auf den Börsencrash:At least 1,331 \...
======================================================

Date: 2015-07-08 22:04:02

[China reagiert auf den
Börsencrash](http://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2015-07-08/china-trade-halts-hit-2-2-trillion-as-state-intervention-fails):

> At least 1,331 companies have halted trading on mainland exchanges,
> freezing \$2.6 trillion of shares, or about 40 percent of the
> country's market capitalization. Another 747 fell by the 10 percent
> daily limit on Wednesday, making it all but impossible to find buyers
> at the prevailing price.
