Overpumping Reduces California\'s Groundwater Storage
=====================================================

Date: 2017-04-12 21:04:00

[![Newly-planted almond trees on a San Joaquin Valley
farm](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20170412/earth20170412-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-104&rn=news.xml&rst=6810)\
\
A new study finds decades of overpumping groundwater in California\'s
Central Valley have irreversibly altered the aquifer and permanently
reduced its ability to store water.
