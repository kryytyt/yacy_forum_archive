NASA Celebrates Earth Day with \#NoPlaceLikeHome Event
======================================================

Date: 2015-04-06 21:04:00

[![There are (so far) 1,800 known planets beyond our solar system, but
among all of them, there\'s no place like
Earth.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/earthday/20150406/noplacelikehome-16.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-115&rn=news.xml&rst=4538)\
\
This Earth Day, April 22, NASA is asking people around the world to
share pictures and videos on social media that show there is no place
like home \-- planet Earth.
