Veteran Ocean Satellite to Assume Added Role
============================================

Date: 2017-06-30 21:06:00

[![Illustration of the U.S./European Ocean Surface Topography Mission
(OSTM)/Jason-2
satellite](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/jason/20170630/jason20170630-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-184&rn=news.xml&rst=6890)\
\
A veteran oceanography satellite that has expanded our knowledge of
global sea level, ocean currents and climate will take on an added role:
improving maps of the sea floor.
