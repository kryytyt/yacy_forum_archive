[The Amazons\' New
Clothes:](http://www.goldenlasso.net/the-amazons-new-clothes/)

> This abrupt change of direction is a shock and these outfits look like
> generic barbarian women from a game of Dungeons and Dragons. They
> completely lack the unique flavor of the Greco-Roman-inspired armor
> ensembles that Lindy Hemming put so much thought and historical
> research into creating for Wonder Woman. The Wonder Woman designs
> received acclaim from fans and costume fanatics alike. They were
> clearly inspired by the Amazon\'s origins in the Mediterranean and
> were feminine but very functional. Why mess with perfection?
>
> Oh, right. The all-male team of directors and executive directors
> wanted women to fight in bikinis.
