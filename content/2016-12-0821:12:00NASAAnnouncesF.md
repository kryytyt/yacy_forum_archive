NASA Announces First Geostationary Vegetation, Atmospheric Carbon Mission
=========================================================================

Date: 2016-12-08 21:12:00

[![Earth
map](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20161208/earth20161208-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-312&rn=news.xml&rst=6694)\
\
NASA has selected a first-of-its-kind Earth science mission that will
extend our nation\'s lead in measuring key greenhouse gases and
vegetation health from space.
