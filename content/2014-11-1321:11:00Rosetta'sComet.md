Rosetta\'s Comet Lander Landed Three Times
==========================================

Date: 2014-11-13 21:11:00

[![Image of the first touchdown site for the Rosetta spacecraft\'s
Philae lander on comet
67P/Churyumov-Gerasimenko](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/rosetta/20141113/pia18875-226.jpg){width="100"

\
The Rosetta mission\'s Philae comet lander bounced off the surface twice
before coming to rest on the surface. Many of its science instruments
have already sent back data.
