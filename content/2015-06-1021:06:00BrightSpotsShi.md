Bright Spots Shine in Newest Dawn Ceres Images
==============================================

Date: 2015-06-10 21:06:00

[![Bright Spots in Ceres\' Second Mapping
Orbit](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20150610/pia19568-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-198&rn=news.xml&rst=4619)\
\
New images of dwarf planet Ceres, taken by NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft, show
the cratered surface of this mysterious world in sharper detail than
ever before.
