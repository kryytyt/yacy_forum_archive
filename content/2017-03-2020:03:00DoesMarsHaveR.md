Does Mars Have Rings? Not Right Now, But Maybe One Day
======================================================

Date: 2017-03-20 20:03:00

[![Two Moons Passing in the Martian
Night](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/20170320/PIA17089-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-075&rn=news.xml&rst=6781)\
\
The two moons of Mars may be progeny of past rings and parents of future
rings around the Red Planet, NASA-funded research at Purdue University
suggests.
