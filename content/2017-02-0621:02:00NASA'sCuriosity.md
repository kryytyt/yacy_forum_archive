NASA\'s Curiosity Rover Sharpens Paradox of Ancient Mars
========================================================

Date: 2017-02-06 21:02:00

[![View of Yellowknife Bay Formation, With Drilling
Sites](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20170206/PIA17595-226.jpg){width="100"

Evidence shows ancient Mars sometimes had water on its surface. Yet
climate modelers struggle to produce scenarios where its surface is warm
enough to keep water unfrozen.
