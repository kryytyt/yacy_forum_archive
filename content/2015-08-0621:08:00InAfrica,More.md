In Africa, More Smoke Leads to Less Rain, NASA Shows
====================================================

Date: 2015-08-06 21:08:00

[![The MODIS instrument captured this image of numerous fires burning in

Agricultural fires in North Africa reduce the region\'s rainfall during
the dry season, according to a NASA study.
