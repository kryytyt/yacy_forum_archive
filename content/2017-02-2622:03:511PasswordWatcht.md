====================

Date: 2017-02-26 22:03:51

[![](https://www.jwz.org/images/opm5-watchtower-popup.png)](https://support.1password.com/cs/1password-watchtower/)I
didn\'t know this existed! Just the other day I was wishing that this
existed, and it turns out it already does.

[1Password
Watchtower](https://support.1password.com/cs/1password-watchtower/)

> Watchtower identifies website vulnerabilities and alerts you when one
> is found. Website vulnerability information is refreshed daily to
> verify items in your vault. 1Password downloads the information and
> checks it locally against your Logins. Watchtower will list Logins
> associated with sites which have (or had) known vulnerabilities.

Sadly, my list of passwords that should be changed is of the \"there
goes my afternoon\" magnitude.
