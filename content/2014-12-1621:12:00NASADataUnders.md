NASA Data Underscore Severity of California Drought
===================================================

Date: 2014-12-16 21:12:00

[![Trends in total water storage in California, Nevada and bordering
states from NASA\'s Gravity
Recovery](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/california/20141216/earth20141216-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-431&rn=news.xml&rst=4412)\
\
It will take about 1.5 times the maximum volume of the largest U.S.
reservoir to recover from California\'s continuing drought, according to
a new analysis of NASA satellite data.
