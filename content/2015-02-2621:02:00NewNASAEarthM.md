New NASA Earth Missions Expand View of Home Planet
==================================================

Date: 2015-02-26 21:02:00

[![Over the past 12 months NASA has added five missions to its orbiting
Earth-observing
fleet](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20150226b/earth20150226c-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-072&rn=news.xml&rst=4495)\
\
Four new NASA Earth-observing missions are collecting data from space -
with a fifth newly in orbit - after the busiest year of NASA Earth
science launches in more than a decade.
