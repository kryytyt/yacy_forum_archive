Dueling Climate Cycles May Increase Sea Level Swings
====================================================

Date: 2016-03-14 20:03:00

[![Higher Pacific sea levels increase coastal flooding
risks.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/elnino/20160314/elnino20160314-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-071&rn=news.xml&rst=6082)\
\
A new study explores the relationship between climate cycles and sea
level variations, and could lead to better predictions of sea level
changes.
