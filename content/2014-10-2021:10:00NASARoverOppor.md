NASA Rover Opportunity Views Comet Near Mars
============================================

Date: 2014-10-20 21:10:00

[![Mars Rover Opportunity\'s View of Passing
Comet](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/20141020/pia18591-226.jpg){width="100"

\
NASA\'s Mars Exploration Rover Opportunity caught an image of a
celestial visitor \-- comet C/2013 A1 Siding Spring \-- as the comet
approached near to Mars on Oct. 19, 2014.
