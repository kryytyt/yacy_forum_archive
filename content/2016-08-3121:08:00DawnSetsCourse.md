Dawn Sets Course for Higher Orbit
=================================

Date: 2016-08-31 21:08:00

[![This artist concept shows NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft above dwarf planet
Ceres, as seen in images from the
mission.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20150717/pia19598-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-227&rn=news.xml&rst=6608)\
\
After studying Ceres for more than eight months from its low-altitude
science orbit, NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft will move higher up for different
views of the dwarf planet.
