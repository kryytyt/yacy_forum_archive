Observatories Combine to Crack Open the Crab Nebula
===================================================

Date: 2017-05-10 21:05:00

[![Crab
Nebula](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/spitzer/20170510/PIA21474-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-137&rn=news.xml&rst=6842)\
\
Astronomers have produced a highly detailed image of the Crab Nebula by
combining data from telescopes spanning nearly the entire breadth of the
electromagnetic spectrum.
