Rover Takes on Steepest Slope Ever Tried on Mars
================================================

Date: 2016-03-31 21:03:00

[![Opportunity\'s Shadow and Wheel Tracks on
Slope](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mer/2016-03-31/PIA20328-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-092&rn=news.xml&rst=6193)\
\
NASA\'s long-lived Mars rover Opportunity is driving to an alternative
hillside target after a climb on the steepest slope ever tackled by any
Mars rover.
