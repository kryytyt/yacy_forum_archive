Helicopter Could be \'Scout\' for Mars Rovers
=============================================

Date: 2015-01-22 21:01:00

[![Helicopter Could be \'Scout\' for Mars
Rovers](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/20150122/mars20150122-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-031&rn=news.xml&rst=4457)\
\
A proposed helicopter could triple the distances that Mars rovers can
drive in a Martian day and help pinpoint interesting targets for study.
