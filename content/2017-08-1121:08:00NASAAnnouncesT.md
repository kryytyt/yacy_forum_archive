NASA Announces Television Coverage for Aug. 21 Solar Eclipse
============================================================

Date: 2017-08-11 21:08:00

[![This illustration depicts a rare alignment of the Sun and Moon
casting a shadow on
Earth.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/eclipse/20170811/eclipse20170811-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-216&rn=news.xml&rst=6921)\
\
NASA TV will carry the Aug. 21 solar eclipse live from unique vantage
points on the ground, from aircraft and from spacecraft, including the
International Space Station.
