Loss of Carbon in Martian Atmosphere Explained
==============================================

Date: 2015-11-24 21:11:00

[![Carbon Exchange and Loss Processes on
Mars](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20151124/pia20163-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-356&rn=news.xml&rst=4778)\
\
A process involving ultraviolet light from the sun helps explain the
ratio of heavier to lighter carbon in Mars\' atmosphere and resolve a
dilemma about \"missing\" carbon.
