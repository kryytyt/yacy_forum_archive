New Insights into Titan\'s Complex Chemistry
============================================

Date: 2017-07-26 21:07:00

[![Natural color image shows Titan\'s upper
atmosphere](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20170726/PIA06236-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-199&rn=news.xml&rst=6904)\
\
Cassini has made a surprising detection of a molecule that is
instrumental in producing complex organics within the hazy atmosphere of
Saturn\'s moon Titan.
