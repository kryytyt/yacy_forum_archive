Dawn Heads Toward Final Orbit
=============================

Date: 2015-10-26 20:10:00

[![Occator and Surrounding
Terrain](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20151026/PIA19996-226.jpg){width="100"

\
NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft fired up its ion engine on Friday, Oct. 23, to
begin its journey toward its fourth and final science orbit at dwarf
planet Ceres.
