Chorus of Black Holes Sings in X-Rays
=====================================

Date: 2016-07-28 21:07:00

[![The blue dots in this field of galaxies, show galaxies that contain
supermassive black holes emitting high-energy
X-rays.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/nustar/20160728/PIA20865-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-197&rn=news.xml&rst=6579)\
\
Supermassive black holes in the universe are like a raucous choir
singing in the language of X-rays. When black holes pull in surrounding
matter, they let out powerful X-ray bursts.
