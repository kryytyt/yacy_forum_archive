From Mars Rover: Panorama Above \'Perseverance Valley\'
=======================================================

Date: 2017-07-20 21:07:00

[![Panorama Above \'Perseverance Valley\' on
Mars](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mer/20170720/PIA21723-226.jpg){width="100"

NASA\'s Mars Exploration Rover Opportunity recorded a panoramic view
before entering the upper end of a fluid-carved valley that descends the
inner slope of a crater\'s rim.
