Houston-Based Lifeco Services Corp Awarded the Travel Management Contract
=========================================================================

Date: 1989-10-30 21:10:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5874)\
\
Houston-based Lifeco Services Corporation has been awarded the travel
management contract for the Jet Propulsion Laboratory, Pasadena, Calif.
