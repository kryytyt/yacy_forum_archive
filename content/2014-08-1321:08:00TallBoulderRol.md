Tall Boulder Rolls Down Martian Hill, Lands Upright
===================================================

Date: 2014-08-13 21:08:00

[![Boulder on
Mars](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20140813/pia18594-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-275&rn=news.xml&rst=4258)\
\
A new photo from NASA\'s Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter shows an oblong
boulder about 20 feet (6 meters) tall standing upright on Mars after
tumbling downhill, leaving a trail.
