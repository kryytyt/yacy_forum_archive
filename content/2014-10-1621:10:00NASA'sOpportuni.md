NASA\'s Opportunity Rover Gets Panorama Image at \'Wdowiak Ridge\'
==================================================================

Date: 2014-10-16 21:10:00

[![Opportunity\'s Northward View of \'Wdowiak
Ridge\'](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mer/2014-10-16/pia18614-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-358&rn=news.xml&rst=4341)\
\
NASA\'s decade-old Mars Exploration Rover Opportunity is working near
the base of a steep-sloped hill called \"Wdowiak Ridge,\" shown in a
dramatic new panorama from the rover.
