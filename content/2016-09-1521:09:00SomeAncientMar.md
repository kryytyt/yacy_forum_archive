Some Ancient Mars Lakes Came Long After Others
==============================================

Date: 2016-09-15 21:09:00

[![Landscape of Former Lakes and Streams on Northern
Mars](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20160915/PIA20838-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-241&rn=news.xml&rst=6619)\
\
Lakes and snowmelt-fed streams on Mars formed much later than previously
thought possible, according to new findings using data primarily from a
NASA orbiter.
