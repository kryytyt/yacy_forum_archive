NASA\'s ISS-RapidScat to Launch Aboard SpaceX Rocket
====================================================

Date: 2014-09-18 21:09:00

[![A SpaceX Falcon 9 rocket on the launch pad at Launch Complex 40 at

The launch of NASA\'s International Space Station Rapid Scatterometer,
or ISS-RapidScat, is scheduled for 11:14 p.m. PDT Sept. 19 (2:14 a.m.
EDT Sept. 20).
