NASA Mars Rover Mission Picked for Smithsonian Honor
====================================================

Date: 2013-03-13 09:35:13

[![Curiosity Rover\'s Self Portrait at \'John Klein\' Drilling
Site](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20130304/pia16763-th.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.cfm?release=2013-093&rn=news.xml&rst=3724)\
\
The team in charge of successfully landing NASA\'s Mars rover Curiosity
will receive the Smithsonian National Air and Space Museum\'s 2013
Trophy for Current Achievement.
