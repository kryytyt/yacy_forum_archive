NASA Rocket Experiment: Universe is Brighter Than We Thought
============================================================

Date: 2014-11-06 21:11:00

[![CIBER
launch](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/universe/20141106/pia18853-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-382&rn=news.xml&rst=4363)\
\
A NASA sounding rocket experiment has detected a surprising surplus of
infrared light in the dark space between galaxies, a diffuse cosmic glow
as bright as all known galaxies combined.
