New Movie Shows Cassini\'s First Dive over Saturn
=================================================

Date: 2017-05-03 21:05:00

[![Cassini took this movie sequence of images during its first dive
between Saturn and its rings on April 26,
2017.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20170503/cassini20170503-226.jpg){width="100"

\
A new movie sequence of images from NASA\'s Cassini shows the view as
the spacecraft swooped over Saturn during its first Grand Finale dive on
April 26.
