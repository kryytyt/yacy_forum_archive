NuSTAR Probes Black Hole Jet Mystery
====================================

Date: 2017-10-30 20:10:00

[![This artist\'s concept shows a black hole with an accretion
disk.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/nustar/20171030/nustar20171030-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-281&rn=news.xml&rst=6986)\
\
Scientists have been able to measure the distance that particles in
black hole jets travel before they \"turn on\" and become bright sources
of light.
