Swirling Cloud at Titan\'s Pole is Cold and Toxic
=================================================

Date: 2014-10-01 21:10:00

[![Spectral map of Titan with Polar
Vortex](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20141001/pia18431-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-331&rn=news.xml&rst=4314)\
\
Scientists analyzing data from NASA\'s Cassini mission have found a
giant, toxic cloud hovering over the south pole of Titan, after the
atmosphere there cooled dramatically.
