Mariner VII Launch
==================

Date: 1969-03-17 21:03:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"

The second Mariner \'69 spacecraft, Mariners VII, will be launched on
fly-by missions to Mars on Monday, March 24, at about 2 p.m. The first
spacecraft Mariner VI, was launched February 24.
