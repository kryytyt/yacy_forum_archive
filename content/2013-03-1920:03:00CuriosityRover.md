Curiosity Rover Exits \'Safe Mode\'
===================================

Date: 2013-03-19 20:03:00

[![This self-portrait of NASA\'s Mars rover Curiosity combines 66
exposures](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20130304/pia16763-th.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.cfm?release=2013-104&rn=news.xml&rst=3735)\
\
NASA\'s Mars rover Curiosity has returned to active status and is on
track to resume science investigations, following two days in a
precautionary standby status, \"safe mode.\"
