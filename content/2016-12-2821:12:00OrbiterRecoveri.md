Orbiter Recovering from Precautionary Pause in Activity
=======================================================

Date: 2016-12-28 21:12:00

[![Artist\'s concept of NASA\'s Mars Odyssey
spacecraft](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/odyssey/20161228/pia04816-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-329&rn=news.xml&rst=6711)\
\
NASA\'s Mars Odyssey orbiter, which has been in service at Mars since
October 2001, put itself into safe mode \-- a protective standby status
\-- on Dec. 26, while remaining in communication with Earth.
