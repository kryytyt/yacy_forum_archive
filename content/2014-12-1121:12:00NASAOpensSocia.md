NASA Opens Social Media Accreditation for SMAP Launch
=====================================================

Date: 2014-12-11 21:12:00

[![This view of Earth comes from NASA\'s Moderate Resolution Imaging
Spectroradiometer aboard the Terra
satellite.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/globe-modis-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-427&rn=news.xml&rst=4407)\
\
NASA is inviting social media users to apply for credentials to cover
the launch of the agency\'s SMAP spacecraft, the fifth NASA Earth
science mission to launch since January 2014.
