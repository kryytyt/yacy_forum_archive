NASA Maps California Drought Effects on Sierra Trees
====================================================

Date: 2016-06-27 21:06:00

[![Tree
diagram](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20160627b/PIA20717-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-163&rn=news.xml&rst=6546)\
\
A new map created with measurements from an airborne instrument
developed by NASA\'s Jet Propulsion Laboratory, Pasadena, California,
reveals the devastating effect of California\'s ongoing drought on
Sierra Nevada conifer forests.
