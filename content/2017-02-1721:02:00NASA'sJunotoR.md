NASA\'s Juno to Remain in Current Orbit at Jupiter
==================================================

Date: 2017-02-17 21:02:00

[![Jupiter\'s south
pole](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/juno/20170217/PIA21382-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-043&rn=news.xml&rst=6752)\
\
NASA\'s Juno mission to Jupiter, which has been in orbit around the gas
giant since July 4, 2016, will remain in its current 53-day orbit for
the remainder of the mission.
