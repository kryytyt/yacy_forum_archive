Dawn Sends Sharper Scenes from Ceres
====================================

Date: 2015-08-25 21:08:00

[![Ceres](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20150825/pia19631-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-277&rn=news.xml&rst=4697)\
\
The closest-yet views of Ceres, delivered by NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft,
show the small world\'s features in unprecedented detail, including
Ceres\' tall, conical mountain; crater formation features and narrow,
braided fractures.
