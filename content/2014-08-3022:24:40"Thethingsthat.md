\"The things that will last on the internet are not owned.\"
============================================================

Date: 2014-08-30 22:24:40

[Inessential:](http://inessential.com/2014/08/27/waffle_on_social_media)

> My blog isn\'t part of a system where its usefulness is just a hook to
> get me to use it.
>
> My blog\'s older than Twitter and Facebook, and it will outlive them.
> It has seen Flickr explode and then fade. It\'s seen Google Wave and
> Google Reader come and go, and it\'ll still be here as Google Plus
> fades. When Medium and Tumblr are gone, my blog will be here.
>
> The things that will last on the internet are not owned. Plain old
> websites, blogs, RSS, irc, email.
