NASA Seeks Information from Potential Funders for Spitzer
=========================================================

Date: 2017-10-13 21:10:00

[![Artist\'s concept shows NASA\'s Spitzer Space
Telescope](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/spitzer/20171013/spitzer20171013-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-269&rn=news.xml&rst=6974)\
\
NASA is seeking information from U.S. parties interested in operating
the Spitzer Space Telescope with non-NASA funding after March 2019, when
NASA financial support ends.
