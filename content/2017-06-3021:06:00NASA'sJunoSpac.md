NASA\'s Juno Spacecraft to Fly Over Jupiter\'s Great Red Spot July 10
=====================================================================

Date: 2017-06-30 21:06:00

[![True color mosaic of
Jupiter](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/juno/20170630/PIA04866-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-186&rn=news.xml&rst=6891)\
\
NASA\'s Juno spacecraft will fly directly over Jupiter\'s Great Red
Spot, the gas giant\'s iconic, 10,000-mile-wide storm, on July 10,
providing the closest-ever view of it.
