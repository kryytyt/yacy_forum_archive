New Strategy Needed to Process Data From Eos
============================================

Date: 1989-03-06 21:03:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5842)\
\
Earth scientists, inundated by volumes of data from space satellites,
will need whole new strategy to process the additional one-trillion bits
per day expected from NASA\'s Earth Observing System (Eos) in the 1990s,
two NASA scientists said.
