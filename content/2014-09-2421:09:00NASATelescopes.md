NASA Telescopes Find Clear Skies and Water Vapor on Exoplanet
=============================================================

Date: 2014-09-24 21:09:00

[![Seeing Starlight Through a Planet\'s
Rim](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/spitzer/20140924/pia18837-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-322&rn=news.xml&rst=4306)\
\
Astronomers using data from three of NASA\'s space telescopes \--
Hubble, Spitzer and Kepler \-- have discovered clear skies and steamy
water vapor on a gaseous exoplanet.
