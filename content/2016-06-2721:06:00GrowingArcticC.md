Growing Arctic Carbon Emissions Could Go Unobserved
===================================================

Date: 2016-06-27 21:06:00

[![Alaska](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20160627/ArcticWinter-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-162&rn=news.xml&rst=6545)\
\
Winter changes in Arctic carbon dioxide may accelerate global warming.
Scientists could detect these changes by aircraft with ongoing
monitoring programs.
