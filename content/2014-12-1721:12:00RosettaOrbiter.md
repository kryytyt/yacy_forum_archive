Rosetta Orbiter to Swoop Down On Comet in February
==================================================

Date: 2014-12-17 21:12:00

[![Philae Lander\'s View of \'Perihelion Cliff\' on Comet
Surface](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/rosetta/20141217/pia19095-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-434&rn=news.xml&rst=4415)\
\
The European Space Agency\'s orbiting Rosetta spacecraft is expected to
come within four miles (six kilometers) of the surface of comet
67P/Churyumov-Gerasimenko in February of next year.
