Eliot Middle School Straight-A Students Awarded
===============================================

Date: 1989-12-04 21:12:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5887)\
\
Straight-A students and science fair award-winners from Pasadena\'s
Eliot Middle School will be honored at an awards banquet sponsored by
the Jet Propulsion Laboratory on Thursday, Dec. 7, 1989 at 6:30 p.m. at
JPL\'s von Karman Auditorium.
