Greenland Campaign Takes Flight to Measure Ice Sheet
====================================================

Date: 2015-08-28 21:08:00

[![A grayish turquoise melt pond on the Greenland ice sheet, as seen
from the air. Credit:
NASA](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/ocean/20150828b/earth20150828-226.jpg){width="100"

An airborne campaign over Greenland this summer helps scientists prepare
for a satellite mission that will monitor global ice sheets.
