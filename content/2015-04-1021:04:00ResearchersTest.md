Researchers Test Smartphones for Earthquake Warning
===================================================

Date: 2015-04-10 21:04:00

[![Cell phones can detect ground motion and warn others before strong
shaking
arrives.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/earthquake/20150410/earthquake20150410-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-124&rn=news.xml&rst=4547)\
\
Smartphones and other personal electronic devices could, in some regions
of the world, function as early warning systems for large earthquakes,
according to a new study.
