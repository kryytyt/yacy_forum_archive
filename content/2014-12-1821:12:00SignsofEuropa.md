Signs of Europa Plumes Remain Elusive in Search of Cassini Data
===============================================================

Date: 2014-12-18 21:12:00

[![Jupiter\'s icy moon Europa displays many signs of
activity](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20141218/pia19048-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-436&rn=news.xml&rst=4417)\
\
A new study suggests that the thin, hot gas around Jupiter\'s moon
Europa does not show evidence of plume activity occurring in 2001, when
NASA\'s Cassini spacecraft flew past.
