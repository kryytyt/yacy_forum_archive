Man arrested for stealing over \$160K worth of cheese
=====================================================

Date: 2016-10-23 05:34:47

> HACKENSACK, N.J. \-- A New Jersey man has been charged with stealing
> more than \$160,000 worth of Jamaican cheese.
>
> Prosecutors said Thursday that 18-year-old Darluis Ortiz stole pallets
> of Tastee Cheese products from a food warehouse in Moonachie on Sept.
> 23.
>
> Tastee cheese is often eaten with spiced or sweetened buns, especially
> at Easter. It\'s produced by Dairy Industries Jamaica Ltd.
>
> Ortiz was arrested on Wednesday and was charged with theft, burglary,
> conspiracy and possessing burglary tools. He was being held in the
> Bergen County jail on bail that was \$10,000 less than the value of
> the canned, processed cheese.
