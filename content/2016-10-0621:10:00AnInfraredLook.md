An Infrared Look at Hurricane Matthew from NASA\'s AIRS
=======================================================

Date: 2016-10-06 21:10:00

[![NASA\'s Atmospheric Infrared Sounder (AIRS) instrument on NASA\'s
Aqua satellite captured this infrared view of Hurricane
Matthew.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/hurricane/20161006/earth20161006-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-258&rn=news.xml&rst=6640)\
\
A JPL-developed advanced atmospheric sounding system aboard NASA\'s Aqua
satellite is providing important insights into the inner workings of
dangerous Hurricane Matthew.
