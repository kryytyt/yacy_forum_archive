NASA Mars Rover Moves Onward After \'Marias Pass\' Studies
==========================================================

Date: 2015-08-18 21:08:00

[![Looking Up at Mars Rover Curiosity in \'Buckskin\'
Selfie](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20150817/pia19808-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-271&rn=news.xml&rst=4690)\
\
After taking a low-angle selfie at its latest drilling site, NASA\'s
Curiosity Mars rover has departed the area called \"Marias Pass,\" where
it had been working since May.
