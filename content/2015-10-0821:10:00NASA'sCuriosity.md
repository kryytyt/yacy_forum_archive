NASA\'s Curiosity Rover Team Confirms Ancient Lakes on Mars
===========================================================

Date: 2015-10-08 21:10:00

[![Strata at Base of Mount
Sharp](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20151008/pia19839-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-313&rn=news.xml&rst=4734)\
\
A new study from NASA\'s Mars Science Laboratory/Curiosity team confirms
that Mars was, billions of years ago, capable of storing water in lakes
over an extended period.
