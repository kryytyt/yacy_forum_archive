**Hosts:** [Randal Schwartz](http://twit.tv/floss) and [Simon
Phipps](http://webmink.com/).

OpenStax CNX (previously Connexions) is a shared content repository of
educational resources - primarily textbook style content.

Ross Reedstrom is Chief Sysadmin as well as a developer for OpenStax
CNX. Kathi Fletcher is the Product Manager for OpenStax Tutor at Rice
University.

**Guests:** [Ross Reedstrom](http://cnx.org/) and [Kathi
Fletcher](http://cnx.org/)
