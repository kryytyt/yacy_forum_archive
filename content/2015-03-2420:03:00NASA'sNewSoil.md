NASA\'s New Soil Moisture Mapper Goes for a Spin
================================================

Date: 2015-03-24 20:03:00

[![NASA\'s Soil Moisture Active Passive (SMAP)
mission](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/smap/20150224/smap20150224-226.jpg){width="100"

NASA\'s new soil moisture mapping mission has moved a step closer to
starting science operations following the partial spin-up of its
\"golden lasso\" reflector antenna.
