Study Finds Drought Recoveries Taking Longer
============================================

Date: 2017-08-14 21:08:00

[![Global patterns of drought recovery time, in
months.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/climate/20170814/Drought-recovery20170814-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-217&rn=news.xml&rst=6922)\
\
Land ecosystems are taking longer to recover from droughts, and
incomplete recovery may become the norm in places, with tree deaths and
increased greenhouse gas emissions.
