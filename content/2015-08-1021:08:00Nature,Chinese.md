Nature, Chinese Pollution Offset U.S. West Ozone Gains
======================================================

Date: 2015-08-10 21:08:00

[![Cuts in ground-level, ozone-forming pollutants are cleansing the West
Coast\'s cloud-and-smog
mix](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/climate/20150810/earth20150810-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-263&rn=news.xml&rst=4685)\
\
An expected reduction in atmospheric ozone levels over the western U.S.
was offset by natural atmospheric processes and pollutants crossing the
Pacific Ocean from China.
