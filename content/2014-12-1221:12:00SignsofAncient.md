Signs of Ancient Mars Lakes and Quakes Seen in New Map
======================================================

Date: 2014-12-12 21:12:00

[![Geological Mapping of Hills in Martian
Canyon](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20141212/pia19084-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-428&rn=news.xml&rst=4408)\
\
High-resolution geological mapping based on images from NASA\'s Mars
Reconnaissance Orbiter suggests lakes and \"marsquakes\" were part of
the vast Valles Marineris canyon system.
