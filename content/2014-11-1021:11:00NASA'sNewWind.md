NASA\'s New Wind Watcher Ready for Weather Forecasters
======================================================

Date: 2014-11-10 21:11:00

[![ISS-RapidScat data on a North Atlantic extratropical
cyclone](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/rapidscat/20141110/earth20141110-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-390&rn=news.xml&rst=4371)\
\
In an early holiday gift to the world\'s weather and marine forecasting
agencies, ocean-winds data from NASA\'s newest Earth-observing mission
are being released two months ahead of schedule.
