NASA Contributes to Global Navigation Standard Update
=====================================================

Date: 2016-02-24 21:02:00

[![Sites around the world (yellow dots) contributed data and serve as

Just as trail landmarks aid hikers, the International Terrestrial
Reference Frame is a key navigation standard in science and daily life.
NASA has key roles in updating it.
