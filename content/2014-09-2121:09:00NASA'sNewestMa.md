NASA\'s Newest Mars Mission Spacecraft Enters Orbit around Red Planet
=====================================================================

Date: 2014-09-21 21:09:00

[![MAVEN (Artist\'s
Concept)](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/maven/20140921/mars20140921-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-318&rn=news.xml&rst=4302)\
\
NASA\'s MAVEN spacecraft successfully entered Mars\' orbit at 7:24 p.m.
PDT (10:24 p.m. EDT) Sunday, Sept. 21, where it now will prepare to
study the Red Planet\'s upper atmosphere.
