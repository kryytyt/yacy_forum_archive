Space Radar Helps Track Underground Water Pollution Risk
========================================================

Date: 2015-03-19 20:03:00

[![In this region, urbanization has increased the potential for
groundwater
contamination.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20150319/poplex20150319-226.jpg){width="100"

Satellite observations provide a new and more accurate way to assess
where underground water in northern Italy is most at risk from
pollutants.
