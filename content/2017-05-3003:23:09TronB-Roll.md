> The team referenced a lot of Ernst Haeckel, the German Biologist who
> rendered by hand surreal organisms that resembled graphic prismatic
> coral structures \[\...\] The IsoSurface housing Quorra\'s DNA had to
> be broken down and unwrapped in order to be accessed. To accomplish
> this, Flynn used a Voronoi Noise algorithim that suspended the heart
> by spherically wraping around it, allowing him to wade through an
> web-like interface to break open the DNA.
