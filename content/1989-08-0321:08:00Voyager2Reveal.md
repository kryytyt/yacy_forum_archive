Voyager 2 Reveals Three Additional Neptune Moons
================================================

Date: 1989-08-03 21:08:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5858)\
\
Images from the Voyager 2 spacecraft have revealed three additional new
moons in orbit around Neptune, Voyager imaging team scientists announced
today.
