NASA Telescope Reveals Largest Batch of Earth-Size, Habitable-Zone Planets Around Single Star
=============================================================================================

Date: 2017-02-22 21:02:00

[![](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/exoplanet/20170222/PIA21423-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-047&rn=news.xml&rst=6756)\
\
NASA\'s Spitzer Space Telescope has revealed the first known system of
seven Earth-size planets around a single star.
