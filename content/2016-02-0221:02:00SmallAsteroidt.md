Small Asteroid to Pass Close to Earth March 8
=============================================

Date: 2016-02-02 21:02:00

[![This graphic depicts the orbit of asteroid 2013
TX68.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/asteroid/20160202/asteroid20160225-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-033&rn=news.xml&rst=4888)\
\
A small asteroid that two years ago flew past Earth at a comfortable
distance of about 1.3 million miles (2 million kilometers) will safely
fly by our planet again in a few weeks, though this time it may be much
closer.
