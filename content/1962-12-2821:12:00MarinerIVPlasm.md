Mariner IV Plasma Experiment Failed
===================================

Date: 1962-12-28 21:12:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6064)\
\
Telemetry from the Mariner IV spacecraft, now en route across
interplanetary space to the planet Mars, indicates that one of its eight
experiments has ceased to function.
