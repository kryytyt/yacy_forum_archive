Rosetta Lander Captured Before/After Bounce
===========================================

Date: 2014-11-18 21:11:00

[![Images taken by the Rosetta spacecraft\'s OSIRIS imaging
system](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/rosetta/20141118/rosetta20141118-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-400&rn=news.xml&rst=4382)\
\
The descent of its comet lander Philae was captured by the Rosetta
spacecraft\'s main camera as the lander approached - and then rebounded
off - the comet\'s surface.
