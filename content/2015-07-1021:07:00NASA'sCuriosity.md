NASA\'s Curiosity Mars Rover Tracks Sunspots
============================================

Date: 2015-07-10 21:07:00

[![Tracking Sunspots from Mars, Summer
2015](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20150709/pia19801-226.gif){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-236&rn=news.xml&rst=4657)\
\
Curiosity is monitoring sunspots on the side of the sun facing away from
Earth, during weeks when sun-monitoring spacecraft can\'t provide that
information.
