Galileo Millennium Mission Status
=================================

Date: 2001-04-06 21:04:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion
Laboratory](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6287)\
\
NASA\'s Galileo spacecraft has successfully completed a flyby of
Jupiter\'s moon Io, skimming about 200 kilometers (124 miles) above the
surface of the highly volcanic moon at 0459 Universal Time today (9:59
p.m. Sunday, Pacific Daylight Time).
