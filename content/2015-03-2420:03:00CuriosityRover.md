Curiosity Rover Finds Biologically Useful Nitrogen on Mars
==========================================================

Date: 2015-03-24 20:03:00

[![This self-portrait of NASA\'s Mars rover Curiosity combines dozens of
exposures](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20150323/pia16937-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-096&rn=news.xml&rst=4516)\
\
A team using the Sample Analysis at Mars (SAM) instrument suite aboard
NASA\'s Curiosity rover has made the first detection of nitrogen on the
surface of Mars from release during heating of Martian sediments.
