Cassini Finds Saturn Moon Has \'Sea Level\' Like Earth
======================================================

Date: 2018-01-17 21:01:00

[![Vast Ligeia Mare in False
Color](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20130522/pia17031-226.jpg){width="100"

Saturn\'s moon Titan may be nearly a billion miles away from Earth, but
a recent paper from NASA\'s Cassini mission reveals a new way this world
and our own are eerily similar.
