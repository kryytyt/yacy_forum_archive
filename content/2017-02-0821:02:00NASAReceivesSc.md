NASA Receives Science Report on Europa Lander Concept
=====================================================

Date: 2017-02-08 21:02:00

[![Artist\'s rendering illustrates a conceptual design for a potential
future
mission](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/europa/20170208/PIA21048-226.jpg){width="100"

A report on the potential science value of a lander on Europa has been
delivered to NASA, and the agency is engaging the science community to
discuss its findings.
