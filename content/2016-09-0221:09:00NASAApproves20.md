NASA Approves 2018 Launch of Mars InSight Mission
=================================================

Date: 2016-09-02 21:09:00

[![NASA has set a new launch opportunity, beginning May 5, 2018, for the
InSight mission to
Mars.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/insight/20151203/pia19811-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-230&rn=news.xml&rst=6612)\
\
NASA is moving forward with a spring 2018 launch of its InSight mission
to study the deep interior of Mars, following final approval this week
by the agency.
