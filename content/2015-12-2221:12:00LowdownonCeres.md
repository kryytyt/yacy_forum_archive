Lowdown on Ceres: Images From Dawn\'s Closest Orbit
===================================================

Date: 2015-12-22 21:12:00

[![Dawn\'s low-altitude mapping orbit around a crater chain called
Gerber
Catena](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20151221/PIA20186-226.jpg){width="100"

\
NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft, cruising in its lowest and final orbit at dwarf
planet Ceres, has delivered the first images from its best-ever
viewpoint.
