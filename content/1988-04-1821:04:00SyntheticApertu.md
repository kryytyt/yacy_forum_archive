Synthetic Aperture Radar Instrument Shipped
===========================================

Date: 1988-04-18 21:04:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5813)\
\
A synthetic aperture radar instrument, the highly sophisticated imaging
radar that will be sent to Venus aboard the Magellan spacecraft, left
Los Angeles Monday (April 18) for Denver aboard specially designed
truck.
