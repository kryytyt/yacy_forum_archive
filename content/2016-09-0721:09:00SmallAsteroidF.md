Small Asteroid Flew Safely Past Earth Today
===========================================

Date: 2016-09-07 21:09:00

[![Animated gif of asteroid 2016 RB1\'s close approach to
Earth](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/asteroid/20160907/asteroid20160907-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-233&rn=news.xml&rst=6615)\
\
A small asteroid designated 2016 RB1 safely flew past Earth today, Sept.
7, at a distance of about 25,000 miles (just less than 1/10th the
distance of Earth to the moon).
