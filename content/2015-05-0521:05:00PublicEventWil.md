Public Event Will Celebrate NASA Mission to Dwarf Planet Ceres
==============================================================

Date: 2015-05-05 21:05:00

[![Dawn mission will celebrate the exploration of Ceres with a festival
called I C
Ceres](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20150505/ceres20150505-226.jpg){width="100"

\
NASA\'s Dawn mission will host the flagship event for its \"I C Ceres\"
space festival at the California Institute of Technology in Pasadena on
Saturday, May 9, from 10 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.
