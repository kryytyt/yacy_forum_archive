NASA\'s Dawn Prepares for Trek Toward Dwarf Planet
==================================================

Date: 2012-08-30 21:08:00

[![Artist\'s concept of Dawn and
Vesta](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20120830/dawn20120830-th.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.cfm?release=2012-271&rn=news.xml&rst=3499)\
\
NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft is on track to become the first probe to orbit
and study two distant destinations, to help scientists answer questions
about the formation of our solar system.
