Rosetta Arrives at Target Comet
===============================

Date: 2014-08-06 21:08:00

[![Rosetta\'s Comet from 177
Miles](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/rosetta/20140805/rosetta20140806-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-265&rn=news.xml&rst=4248)\
\
After a decade-long journey chasing its target, the European Space
Agency\'s Rosetta, carrying three NASA instruments, became the first
spacecraft to orbit a comet.
