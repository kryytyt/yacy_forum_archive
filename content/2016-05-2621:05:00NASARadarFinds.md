NASA Radar Finds Ice Age Record in Mars\' Polar Cap
===================================================

Date: 2016-05-26 21:05:00

[![Climatic cycles of ice and dust build the Martian polar
caps.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20160526/mro20160526-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-135&rn=news.xml&rst=6519)\
\
Scientists using radar data from NASA\'s Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter
(MRO) have found a record of the most recent Martian ice age recorded in
the planet\'s north polar ice cap.
