Prolific Earth Gravity Satellites End Science Mission
=====================================================

Date: 2017-10-27 21:10:00

[![Illustration of the twin Gravity Recovery and Climate Experiment
(GRACE) satellites in
orbit.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/grace/20170914/PIA04236-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-280&rn=news.xml&rst=6984)\
\
After more than 15 productive years in orbit, the U.S./German GRACE
(Gravity Recovery and Climate Experiment) satellite mission has ended
science operations.
