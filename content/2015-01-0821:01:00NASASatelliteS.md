NASA Satellite Set to Get the Dirt on Soil Moisture
===================================================

Date: 2015-01-08 21:01:00

[![Artist\'s rendering of the SMAP instrument. Image credit:
NASA](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/smap/20141230/SMAP_20141230-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-009&rn=news.xml&rst=4436)\
\
A new NASA satellite that will peer into the topmost layer of Earth\'s
soils to measure the hidden waters that influence our climate is in
final preparations for a Jan. 29 launch.
