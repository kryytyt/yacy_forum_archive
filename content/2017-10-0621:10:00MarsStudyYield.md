Mars Study Yields Clues to Possible Cradle of Life
==================================================

Date: 2017-10-06 21:10:00

[![Possible Floor of an Ancient Martian
Sea](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20171006/PIA22058-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-261&rn=news.xml&rst=6966)\
\
The discovery of evidence for ancient sea-floor hydrothermal deposits on
Mars identifies an area on the planet that may offer clues about the
origin of life on Earth.
