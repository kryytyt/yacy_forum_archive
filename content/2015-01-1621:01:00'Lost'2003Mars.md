\'Lost\' 2003 Mars Lander Found by Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter
==============================================================

Date: 2015-01-16 21:01:00

[![Components of Beagle 2 Flight System on
Mars](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20150116/pia19106-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-020&rn=news.xml&rst=4446)\
\
The Beagle 2 Mars Lander, built by the United Kingdom, has been thought
lost on Mars since 2003, but has now been found in images from NASA\'s
Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter.
