NASA to Discuss Next Mars Rover on Facebook Live
================================================

Date: 2016-07-14 21:07:00

[![NASA\'s Mars 2020 Project will re-use the basic engineering of
NASA\'s Curiosity to send a different rover to
Mars](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/2020/20160714/mars2020-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-186&rn=news.xml&rst=6570)\
\
NASA will host a Facebook Live event Friday, July 15 at 10 a.m. PDT,
about NASA\'s next Mars rover, Mars 2020, which is moving forward with
final design and construction.
