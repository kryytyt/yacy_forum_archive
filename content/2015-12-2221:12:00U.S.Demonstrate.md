U.S. Demonstrates Production of Fuel for Missions to the Solar System and Beyond
================================================================================

Date: 2015-12-22 21:12:00

[![This self-portrait of NASA\'s Mars rover Curiosity combines dozens of
exposures](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20150323/pia16937-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-386&rn=news.xml&rst=4806)\
\
The Department of Energy, with NASA funding, has completed the first
U.S. production in nearly 30 years of a specialized fuel to power future
deep space missions.
