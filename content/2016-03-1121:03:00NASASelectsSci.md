NASA Selects Scientists for Mars Rover Research Projects
========================================================

Date: 2016-03-11 21:03:00

[![Knobbly Textured Sandstone on Mount Sharp,
Mars](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20160311/PIA20322-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-068&rn=news.xml&rst=5748)\
\
NASA has selected 28 researchers as participating scientists for the
Curiosity Mars rover mission, including six newcomers to the rover\'s
science team.
