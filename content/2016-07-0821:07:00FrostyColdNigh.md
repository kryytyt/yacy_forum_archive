Frosty Cold Nights Year-Round on Mars May Stir Dust
===================================================

Date: 2016-07-08 21:07:00

[![This map shows the frequency of carbon dioxide frost\'s presence at
sunrise on
Mars.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20160708/pia20758-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-181&rn=news.xml&rst=6564)\
\
Some dusty parts of Mars get as cold at night year-round as the
planet\'s poles do in winter, even regions near the equator in summer,
according to new NASA findings based on Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter
observations.
