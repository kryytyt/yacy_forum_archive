Martian Crater Provides Reminder of Apollo Moonwalk
===================================================

Date: 2017-06-16 21:06:00

[![Mars Rover Opportunity\'s View of \'Orion
Crater\'](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mer/2017-06-16/pia21708-16.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-167&rn=news.xml&rst=6873)\
\
NASA\'s Mars rover Opportunity passed near a crater this spring during
the 45th anniversary of Apollo 16\'s trip to Earth\'s moon, prompting a
connection between two missions.
