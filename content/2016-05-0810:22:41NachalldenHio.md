Nach all den Hiobsbotschaften darüber, wie Jungs in \...
========================================================

Date: 2016-05-08 10:22:41

Nach all den Hiobsbotschaften darüber, wie Jungs in der Schule
systematisch unter den Bus geworfen werden, weil die Lehrerinnen lieber
die Mädchen fördern, gibt es [jetzt auch mal eine positive
Meldung](http://www.wsj.com/articles/can-boys-beat-girls-in-reading-1462202491):

> The research, in the Journal of Experimental Social Psychology, showed
> that boys outscored girls on reading tests if they were told the tests
> were a game. But boys scored significantly lower than girls when told
> the tests were assessments of their reading skills.
