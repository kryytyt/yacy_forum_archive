NASA\'s Kepler Mission Announces Largest Collection of Planets Ever Discovered
==============================================================================

Date: 2016-05-10 21:05:00

[![The image is a concept piece depicting select Kepler planetary
discoveries made to
date.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/kepler/20160510b/kepler20160510-226.jpg){width="100"

\
NASA\'s Kepler mission has verified 1,284 new planets \-- the single
largest finding of planets to date.
