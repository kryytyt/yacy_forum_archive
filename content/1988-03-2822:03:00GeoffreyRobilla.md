Geoffrey Robillard Retires
==========================

Date: 1988-03-28 22:03:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5810)\
\
Geoffrey Robillard, who started at Jet Propulsion Laboratory when it was
U.S. Army rocket center, will retire April 15 after 36 years.
