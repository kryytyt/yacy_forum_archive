NASA Finds Evidence of Diverse Environments in Curiosity Samples
================================================================

Date: 2017-06-09 21:06:00

[![Key Locations Studied at \'Pahrump Hills\' on
Mars](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20170609/PIA21709-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-165&rn=news.xml&rst=6870)\
\
NASA has found diverse minerals in rocks examined by the Curiosity rover
on lower Mount Sharp, Mars. These suggest that wet environmental
conditions there changed over time.
