Could \'Windbots\' Someday Explore the Skies of Jupiter?
========================================================

Date: 2015-07-22 21:07:00

[![An artist\'s rendering shows a windbot bobbing through the skies of
Jupiter](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/technology/20150722/windbot20150722-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-241&rn=news.xml&rst=4662)\
\
Wind-harvesting robotic probes might someday explore the atmospheres of
other planets, as well as our home planet, thanks to an innovative NASA
JPL study.
