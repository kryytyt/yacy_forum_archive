Jacques Cousteau Visits JPL
===========================

Date: 1987-11-16 21:11:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5791)\
\
Noted oceanographer Jacques Cousteau visited NASA\'s Jet Propulsion
Laboratory Nov. 16 to exchange ideas with scientists involved in
satellite studies of the world\'s oceans.
