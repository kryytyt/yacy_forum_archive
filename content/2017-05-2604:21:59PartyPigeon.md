Party Pigeon
============

Date: 2017-05-26 04:21:59

[![](https://www.jwz.org/images/96206178_pigeon_1.jpg)](http://www.bbc.com/news/world-middle-east-40042260)[![](https://www.jwz.org/images/96206174_pigeon_2.jpg)Pigeon
caught with backpack of
drugs](http://www.bbc.com/news/world-middle-east-40042260)

> Customs officials in Kuwait have apprehended a pigeon carrying drugs
> in a miniature backpack, Kuwaiti newspaper al-Rai reports. A total of
> 178 pills were found in the fabric pocket attached to its back.
>
> The bird was caught near the customs building in Abdali, close to the
> border with Iraq.
>
> An al-Rai journalist said the drugs were a form of ketamine, an
> anaesthetic also used as an illegal party drug.
>
> Abdullah Fahmi told the BBC that customs officials already knew
> pigeons were being used to smuggle drugs, but this was the first time
> they had caught a bird in the act.
