Rover Arm Delivers Rock Powder Sample
=====================================

Date: 2015-03-12 20:03:00

[![Curiosity Heading Away from \'Pahrump
Hills\'](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20150312/pia19148-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-087&rn=news.xml&rst=4509)\
\
NASA\'s Curiosity Mars rover used its robotic arm Wednesday, March 11,
to sieve and deliver a rock-powder sample to an onboard instrument.
