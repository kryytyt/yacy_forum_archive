Cassini Closes in on Enceladus, One Last Time
=============================================

Date: 2015-12-16 21:12:00

[![Enceladus \'E-22\' Flyby: Final Visit to
Enceladus](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/cassini/20151216/IMG005282-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-373&rn=news.xml&rst=4794)\
\
A thrilling chapter in solar system exploration will soon conclude, as
NASA\'s Saturn-orbiting Cassini spacecraft makes its final close flyby
of the ocean-bearing moon Enceladus.
