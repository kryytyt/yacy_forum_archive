NASA/UCI Find Evidence of Sea Level \'Fingerprints\'
====================================================

Date: 2017-09-07 21:09:00

[![Sea level rise fingerprints calculated from observations of mass
changes in Greenland,
Antarctica](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/grace/20170907/grace20170907-226.gif){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-237&rn=news.xml&rst=6941)\
\
For the first time, scientists have detected sea level \"fingerprints\"
\-- patterns of variation in global sea level due to changes in water
and ice on land \-- in GRACE data.
