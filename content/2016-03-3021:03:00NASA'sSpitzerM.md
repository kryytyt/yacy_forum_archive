NASA\'s Spitzer Maps Climate Patterns on a Super-Earth
======================================================

Date: 2016-03-30 21:03:00

[![This illustration shows one possible scenario for the hot, rocky
exoplanet called 55 Cancri
e.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/universe/20160330/pia20068-226.jpg){width="100"

\
Observations from NASA\'s Spitzer Space Telescope have led to the first
temperature map of a super-Earth planet \-- a rocky planet nearly two
times as big as ours.
