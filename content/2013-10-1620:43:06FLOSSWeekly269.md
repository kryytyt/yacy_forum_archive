corporation existing to provide education in and promotion of the design
and use of open-source software and hardware in embedded computing.
BeagleBoard.org provides a forum for the owners and developers of
open-source software and hardware to exchange ideas, knowledge and
experience. On occasion, BeagleBoard.org provides for the promotion of
communication with other individuals interested in open-source software
