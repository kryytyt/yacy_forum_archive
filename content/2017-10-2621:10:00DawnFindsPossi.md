Dawn Finds Possible Ancient Ocean Remnants at Ceres
===================================================

Date: 2017-10-26 21:10:00

[![This animation shows dwarf planet Ceres as seen by NASA\'s
Dawn.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20171026/PIA22083-226.gif){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-277&rn=news.xml&rst=6982)\
\
Ceres\' crust as we see it today, with its mixture of ice, salts and
hydrated materials, represents most of the dwarf planet\'s ancient
ocean, scientists say.
