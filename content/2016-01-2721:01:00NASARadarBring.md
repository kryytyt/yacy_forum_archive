NASA Radar Brings a New View of World Heritage Site
===================================================

Date: 2016-01-27 21:01:00

[![The hummingbird glyph and its surroundings in the Nasca world
heritage
site](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/uavsar/20160127/Nasca-lines20160127-226.jpg){width="100"

In just two 10-minute overflights, an airborne NASA synthetic aperture
radar proved it could pinpoint areas of disturbance in Peru\'s Nasca
lines World Heritage Site.
