NASA Demonstrates Airborne Water Quality Sensor
===============================================

Date: 2016-02-25 21:02:00

[![Maps of a) turbidity (water clarity), b) dissolved organic carbon and
c)
chlorophyll-a](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20160225/usgs20160225-16.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-051&rn=news.xml&rst=5581)\
\
Scientists have demonstrated how a NASA-developed airborne instrument
can help monitor water quality in San Francisco Bay and potentially
other water bodies.
