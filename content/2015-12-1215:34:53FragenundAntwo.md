Sorry to answer in English\... When you perform search queries in P2P
mode, by default search results coming from remote nodes are added to
your local index. And this gives you these messages.\
It is possible to modify this behavior in /ConfigPortal.html
(Administration \> Portal Configuration) with checkbox \"Index remote
results\", even if it is not recommended (indexing remote results enable
your peer to reuse it for you or for incoming queries).
