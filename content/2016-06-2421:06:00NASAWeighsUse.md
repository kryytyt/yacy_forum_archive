NASA Weighs Use of Rover to Image Potential Mars Water Sites
============================================================

Date: 2016-06-24 21:06:00

[![Rover\'s
self-portrait](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20160613/pia20602-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-159&rn=news.xml&rst=6542)\
\
NASA is weighing using the Curiosity Mars rover to capture images of
dark streaks present on some higher slopes of Mount Sharp that may be
potential water sites.
