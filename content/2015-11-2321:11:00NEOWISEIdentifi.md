NEOWISE Identifies Greenhouse Gases in Comets
=============================================

Date: 2015-11-23 21:11:00

[![An expanded view of comet C/2006 W3 (Christensen) is shown
here.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/wise/20151123/PIA20118-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-355&rn=news.xml&rst=4776)\
\
Data from NASA\'s NEOWISE mission are giving new insights into comet
dust, nucleus sizes, and production rates for difficult-to-observe gases
like carbon dioxide and carbon monoxide.
