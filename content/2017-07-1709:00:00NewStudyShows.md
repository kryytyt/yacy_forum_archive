New Study Shows the Amazon Makes Its Own Rainy Season
=====================================================

Date: 2017-07-17 09:00:00

[![The Amazon
rainforest.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/forest/20170717/amazon20170717-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-192&rn=news.xml&rst=6897)\
\
New research finds the southern Amazon rainforest triggers its own rainy
season. The finding highlights the close connection between the
rainforest ecosystem and climate.
