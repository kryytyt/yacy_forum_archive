New Map Shows Frequency of Small Asteroid Impacts, Provides Clues on Larger Asteroid Population
===============================================================================================

Date: 2014-11-14 21:11:00

[![This diagram maps the data gathered from 1994-2013 on small asteroids
impacting Earth\'s
atmosphere](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/asteroid/20141114/bolide20141114-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-397&rn=news.xml&rst=4380)\
\
A new map from NASA\'s Near Earth Object Program reveals that small
asteroids frequently enter and disintegrate in Earth\'s atmosphere with
random distribution around the globe.
