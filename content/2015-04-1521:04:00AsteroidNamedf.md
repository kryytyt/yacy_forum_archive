Asteroid Named for Nobel Prize Winner Joins Historic Lineup
===========================================================

Date: 2015-04-15 21:04:00

[![An asteroid discovered by NASA\'s NEOWISE
spacecraft](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/asteroid/20150415/asteroid20150415-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-132&rn=news.xml&rst=4554)\
\
An asteroid discovered by NASA\'s NEOWISE spacecraft has a new
designation: 316201 Malala, to honor Malala Yousafzai of Pakistan, who
received the 2014 Nobel Peace Prize.
