Film Premiere - \'To The Rescue\'
=================================

Date: 2017-05-17 21:05:00

[![An astronaut fixes the Hubble telescope during a space
walk.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/jpl/20170517/rescue-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-141&rn=news.xml&rst=6846)\
\
\"To the Rescue,\" chronicling JPL\'s efforts to save the prestigious,
expensive, myopic Hubble Space Telescope, premieres Tuesday evening, May
23 at Caltech. Admission is free on a first-come, first-served basis.
