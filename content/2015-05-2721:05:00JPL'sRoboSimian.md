JPL\'s RoboSimian to compete in DARPA Robotics Challenge Finals
===============================================================

Date: 2015-05-27 21:05:00

[![Limbed robot RoboSimian, which was developed at
JPL](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/robotics/20150527/pia17302-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-184&rn=news.xml&rst=4603)\
\
RoboSimian, the ape-like robot developed at NASA\'s Jet Propulsion
Laboratory, Pasadena, California, will compete in the DARPA Robotics
Challenge Finals at the Fairplex in Pomona, California, June 5 and 6.
