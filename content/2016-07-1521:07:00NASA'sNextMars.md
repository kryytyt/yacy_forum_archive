NASA\'s Next Mars Rover Progresses Toward 2020 Launch
=====================================================

Date: 2016-07-15 21:07:00

[![Computer-Design Drawing for NASA\'s 2020 Mars
Rover](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/2020/20160715/PIA20759-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-187&rn=news.xml&rst=6569)\
\
After an extensive review process, NASA is ready to proceed with final
design and construction of its next Mars rover, currently targeted to
launch in summer of 2020 and arrive on Mars in February 2021.
