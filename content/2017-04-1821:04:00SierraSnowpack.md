Sierra Snowpack Bigger Than Last Four Years Combined: NASA
==========================================================

Date: 2017-04-18 21:04:00

[![Snow water equivalent\-- the water content of snow \-- in the
Tuolumne River Basin in 2015 and
2017.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/california/20170418/earth20170418-226.gif){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-109&rn=news.xml&rst=6815)\
\
NASA data show that snowpack in the Tuolumne River Basin in
California\'s Sierra Nevada is larger than the last four years of
snowpack combined.
