> It was important to us that during the day the mural was interesting
> enough to enjoy, the colors are soft and the complexity meaningful. At
> night, though, the image looses its pastel daytime appearance and
> reveals 3 distinct crisp drawings. Heat sensing cameras mounted high
> above the artwork track viewers movement. The red, green and blue
> lights playfully follow viewers as the move.
