NASA Completes Study of Future \'Ice Giant\' Mission Concepts
=============================================================

Date: 2017-06-20 21:06:00

[![Uranus and
Neptune](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/voyager/20170620/uranus_neptune-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-171&rn=news.xml&rst=6877)\
\
NASA has released a study of potential future missions to Uranus and
Neptune \-- part of a series of NASA studies in support of the next
Planetary Science Decadal Survey.
