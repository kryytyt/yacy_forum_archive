Hosts: [Randal Schwartz](http://www.stonehenge.com/merlyn/) and [Dan
Lynch](http://danlynch.org).

Pootle is an online translation platform that allows your professional
or community translators to easily complete localization tasks. For you
it becomes easier to manage and monitor the distributed work.

Multiple file formats -\
Key localization file formats are supported, including Gettext PO,
XLIFF, Java .properties, PHP arrays and many more supported by the
Translate Toolkit.

Focus on quality -\
Ensures the best quality localizations by automatically detecting common
errors made by translators.

Powerful online editor -\
Integrates user suggestions, terminology, Translation Memory, Machine
Translation, and more.

Guest: [Dwayne Bailey](http://pootle.translatehouse.org).
