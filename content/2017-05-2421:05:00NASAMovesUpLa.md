NASA Moves Up Launch of Psyche Mission to a Metal Asteroid
==========================================================

Date: 2017-05-24 21:05:00

[![Artist\'s concept of the Psyche spacecraft, which will conduct a
direct exploration of an asteroid thought to be a stripped planetary
core.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/asteroid/psyche/20170523/PIA21499-20170523-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-149&rn=news.xml&rst=6854)\
\
Psyche, NASA\'s mission to a unique metal asteroid, has moved up one
year, with launch in the summer of 2022, and a planned arrival at the
main belt asteroid in 2026.
