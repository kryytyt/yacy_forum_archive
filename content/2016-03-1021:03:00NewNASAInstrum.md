New NASA Instruments to Study Air Pollution, Cyclones
=====================================================

Date: 2016-03-10 21:03:00

[![Air pollution smoke rising from plant
tower.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/climate/20160310/air-pollution-smoke-rising-from-plant-tower-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-067&rn=news.xml&rst=5866)\
\
NASA has selected two new Earth science investigations, including one
from JPL, that will put instruments in orbit to track harmful air
pollutants and study tropical cyclones.
