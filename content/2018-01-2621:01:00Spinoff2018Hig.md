Spinoff 2018 Highlights Space Technology Improving Life on Earth
================================================================

Date: 2018-01-26 21:01:00

[![Scientists at NASA\'s Jet Propulsion Laboratory used their expertise
at detecting faint signals in satellite
data](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/spinoff/20180126/spinoff2018_finder-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2018-017&rn=news.xml&rst=7048)\
\
JPL technology to help find disaster survivors under rubble is included
in the 2018 edition of NASA\'s annual Spinoff publication, which
features 49 technologies the agency helped create.
