JPL Sponsor Awards Banquet for Eliot Middle School Academic Achievers
=====================================================================

Date: 1987-10-27 21:10:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5789)\
\
Academic achievers at Pasadena\'s Eliot Middle School will be honored at
an awards banquet sponsored by the Jet Propulsion Laboratory on
Thursday, Oct. 29, 1987 at 6:30 p.m. at the Brookside Clubhouse
Restaurant adjacent to Pasadena\'s Rose Bowl.
