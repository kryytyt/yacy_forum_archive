Please go to the List of active Peers of your Peer
([localhost:8090[/Network.html?page=1]{style="font-weight: bold"}](http://localhost:8090/Network.html?page=1){.postlink})
and try some different Peer-Lists of other Peers (follow the Link on the
right-most icon in the column \"Info\", you should reach
/Network.html?page=1 on an external IP-Address) and search there your
Peer.\
\
With the Hash or Name of your Peer (the two left-most columns on the
Peer-List), you can check on
[yacy.info/tool\_query](http://yacy.info/tool_query){.postlink} what the
YaCy-Network think about your Peer.\
