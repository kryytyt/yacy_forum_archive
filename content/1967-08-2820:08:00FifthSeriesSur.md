Fifth Series Surveyor Launch
============================

Date: 1967-08-28 20:08:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=6092)\
\
The fifth of a series of Surveyor spacecraft, designed to soft land on
the moon, will be launched from Cape Kennedy during a launch period
beginning Friday, September 8, and ending Wednesday, September 13.
