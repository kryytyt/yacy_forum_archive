U.S., India to Collaborate on Earth, Mars Missions
==================================================

Date: 2014-09-30 21:09:00

[![NASA Administrator Charles Bolden (left) and Chairman K.
Radhakrishnan of the Indian Space Research
Organisation](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mars/20140930/mars20140930-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-328&rn=news.xml&rst=4313)\
\
NASA and the Indian Space Research Organisation signed agreements to
launch a joint space mission to observe Earth and establish a pathway
for joint Mars exploration missions.
