Farewell Rosetta: ESA Mission to End on Comet Surface
=====================================================

Date: 2016-09-29 21:09:00

[![This view shows Comet 67P/Churyumov-Gerasimenko as seen by the OSIRIS
wide-angle camera on ESA\'s Rosetta
spacecraft.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/rosetta/20160929/rosetta20160929-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-250&rn=news.xml&rst=6633)\
\
The European Space Agency Rosetta mission will end dramatically Friday,
Sept. 30, by touching down on a region of a comet known for active pits
that spew dust into space.
