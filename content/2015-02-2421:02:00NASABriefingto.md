NASA Briefing to Discuss First Spacecraft Arrival at a Dwarf Planet
===================================================================

Date: 2015-02-24 21:02:00

[![This artist\'s concept shows NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft heading toward
the dwarf planet
Ceres.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20141229/PIA17650-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-065&rn=news.xml&rst=4488)\
\
JPL will host a briefing Monday, March 2, about the March 6 arrival of
NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft at the dwarf planet Ceres. The event will be
carried live on NASA TV and online.
