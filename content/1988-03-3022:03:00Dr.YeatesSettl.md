Dr. Yeates Settles Controversial Astronomical Theories
======================================================

Date: 1988-03-30 22:03:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5811)\
\
Dr. Clayne Yeates, scientist at NASA\'s Jet Propulsion Laboratory, has
apparently confirmed one of the decade\'s most controversial
astronomical theories \-- that millions of small water-bearing comets
strike the Earth\'s atmosphere every year.
