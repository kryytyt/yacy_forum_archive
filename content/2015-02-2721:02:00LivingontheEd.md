Living on the Edge: Stars Found Far from Galaxy Center
======================================================

Date: 2015-02-27 21:02:00

[![Our Milky Way Gets a
Makeover](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/wise/20150227/pia10748-226.jpg){width="100"

Data from NASA\'s WISE mission have led to the discovery of two clusters
of newborn stars thousands of light-years below the plane of our galaxy.
