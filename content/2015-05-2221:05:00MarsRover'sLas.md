Mars Rover\'s Laser-Zapping Instrument Gets Sharper Vision
==========================================================

Date: 2015-05-22 21:05:00

focusing capability of the Chemistry and Camera (ChemCam) instrument on
NASA\'s Curiosity Mars rover.
