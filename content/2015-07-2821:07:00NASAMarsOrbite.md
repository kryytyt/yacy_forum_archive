NASA Mars Orbiter Preparing for Mars Lander\'s 2016 Arrival
===========================================================

Date: 2015-07-28 21:07:00

[![Artist concept of Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter. Image credit:
NASA/JPL](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20081126/mro-concept-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-249&rn=news.xml&rst=4670)\
\
NASA\'s Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter will fire thrusters on Wednesday for
a maneuver to get into position for the 2016 arrival of NASA\'s next
Mars lander.
