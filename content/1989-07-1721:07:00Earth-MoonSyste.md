Earth-Moon System
=================

Date: 1989-07-17 21:07:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"

An experiment left on the lunar surface 20 years ago by the Apollo 11
astronauts continues to study the Earth-Moon system and return data to
NASA\'s Jet Propulsion Laboratory and other scientific centers around
the world.
