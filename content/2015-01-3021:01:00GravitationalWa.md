Gravitational Waves from Early Universe Remain Elusive
======================================================

Date: 2015-01-30 21:01:00

[![Color scale image from
Planck](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/planck/20150130/pia18911-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=4469)\
\
A joint analysis of data from Planck and the ground-based experiment
BICEP2 has found no conclusive evidence of gravitational waves from the
birth of our universe.
