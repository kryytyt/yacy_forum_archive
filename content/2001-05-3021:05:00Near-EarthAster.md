Near-Earth Asteroid Is Two Chunks In One
========================================

Date: 2001-05-30 21:05:00

Laboratory](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"

taken by astronomers last week using NASA\'s Goldstone radar telescope.
