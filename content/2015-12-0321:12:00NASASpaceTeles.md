NASA Space Telescopes See Magnified Image of Faintest Galaxy from Early Universe
================================================================================

Date: 2015-12-03 21:12:00

[![Faint Compact Galaxy in the Early
Universe](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/spitzer/20151203/pia20054-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-359&rn=news.xml&rst=4780)\
\
Astronomers harnessing the combined power of NASA\'s Hubble and Spitzer
space telescopes have found the faintest object ever seen in the early
universe.
