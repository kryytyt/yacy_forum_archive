Mars Rover Leader Peter Theisinger to Receive National Trophy
=============================================================

Date: 2017-03-28 21:03:00

[![Peter Theisinger of NASA\'s Jet Propulsion Laboratory managed
projects that put three rovers on
Mars.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/people/Theisinger/Theisinger20170328-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-091&rn=news.xml&rst=6797)\
\
Peter Theisinger, who led projects that developed multiple NASA rovers
that successfully landed on Mars, will receive a National Air and Space
Museum Lifetime Achievement Trophy.
