Mars Ice Deposit Holds as Much Water as Lake Superior
=====================================================

Date: 2016-11-22 21:11:00

[![Scalloped Terrain Led to Finding of Buried Ice on
Mars](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20161122/PIA21136-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-299&rn=news.xml&rst=6680)\
\
Frozen beneath cracked, pitted plains on Mars lies about as much water
as is in Lake Superior, researchers using NASA\'s Mars Reconnaissance
Orbiter have determined.
