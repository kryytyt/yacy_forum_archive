Your Home Planet, as Seen From Mars
===================================

Date: 2017-01-06 21:01:00

[![Earth and Its Moon, as Seen From
Mars](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/mro/20170106/pia21260-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-004&rn=news.xml&rst=6716)\
\
From the most powerful telescope orbiting Mars comes a new view of Earth
and its moon, showing continent-size detail on the planet and the
relative size of the moon.
