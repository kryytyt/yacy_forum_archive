Kirk Dawson Appointed Assistant Laboratory Director
===================================================

Date: 1987-07-20 21:07:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5775)\
\
Kirk Dawson has been appointed to the position of assistant laboratory
director for the Office of Technical Divisions of the Jet Propulsion
Laboratory, JPL Director Dr. Lew Allen announced.
