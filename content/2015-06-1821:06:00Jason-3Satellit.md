Jason-3 Satellite Arrives at California Launch Site
===================================================

Date: 2015-06-18 21:06:00

[![The Jason-3 spacecraft is unloaded from a 747 transport aircraft at
Vandenberg Air Force Base, California, on June 18,
2015](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/jason/20150618/jason3-20150618-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-211&rn=news.xml&rst=4631)\
\
The newest addition to a venerable line of ocean-monitoring satellites
has arrived at its launch site in California to prepare for its launch
in August.
