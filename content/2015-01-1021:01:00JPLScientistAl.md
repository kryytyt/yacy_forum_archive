JPL Scientist Alberto Behar Remembered
======================================

Date: 2015-01-10 21:01:00

[![Alberto
Behar](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/people/behar20150110-226.jpg){width="100"

\
JPL employees were deeply saddened by the death of Dr. Alberto Behar,
who died in the crash of a small plane on Friday, Jan. 10, near Van Nuys
Airport in the Los Angeles area.
