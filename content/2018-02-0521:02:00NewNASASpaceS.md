New NASA Space Sensors to Address Key Earth Questions
=====================================================

Date: 2018-02-05 21:02:00

[![A pair of CubeSats will probe a little-studied portion of the radiant
energy emitted by the Arctic
environment](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20180205/arctic_evi4-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2018-023&rn=news.xml&rst=7054)\
\
NASA has selected two new research proposals to develop small,
space-based instruments that will tackle fundamental questions about our
home planet and its environment.
