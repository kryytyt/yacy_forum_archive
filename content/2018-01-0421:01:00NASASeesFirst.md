NASA Sees First Direct Proof of Ozone Hole Recovery
===================================================

Date: 2018-01-04 21:01:00

[![Antarctic ozone
hole](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20180104/earth20180104-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2018-003&rn=news.xml&rst=7033)\
\
For the first time, direct satellite observations of the ozone hole have
shown that ozone-destroying chlorine levels are declining, resulting in
less ozone depletion.
