Curiosity Mars Rover Checks Odd-looking Iron Meteorite
======================================================

Date: 2016-11-02 20:11:00

[![Colorized view of a meteorite on Mars from the ChemCam instrument on
NASA\'s Curiosity Mars
rover](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20161102/pia21133-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-287&rn=news.xml&rst=6667)\
\
Laser-zapping of a globular, golf-ball-size object on Mars by NASA\'s
Curiosity rover confirms that it is an iron-nickel meteorite fallen from
the Red Planet\'s sky.
