In Atmospheric River Storms, Wind Is a Risk, Too
================================================

Date: 2017-02-21 21:02:00

[![Wind damage in Washington
state](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/earth/20170221/earth20170221-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-045&rn=news.xml&rst=6754)\
\
While rain and snow in atmospheric river storms can be drought busters,
they bring a well-known flood risk. A new NASA study shows they also
bring high wind damage worldwide.
