Triton Moon Regenerating Surface
================================

Date: 1989-12-01 21:12:00

[![NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of
Technology](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/&rn=news.xml&rst=5879)\
\
Triton, the large frozen moon of Neptune, has surface that apparently
undergoes regeneration as do Jupiter\'s moons Europa and Io, Voyager 2
scientists said.
