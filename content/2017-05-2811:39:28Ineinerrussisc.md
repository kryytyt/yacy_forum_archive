In einer russischen Studie mit isolierten Kosmonauten \...
==========================================================

Date: 2017-05-28 11:39:28

In einer russischen Studie mit isolierten Kosmonauten [kamen
erstaunliche Sachen zur salzreichen Ernährung zum
Vorschein](https://www.nytimes.com/2017/05/08/health/salt-health-effects.html):

> New studies of Russian cosmonauts, held in isolation to simulate space
> travel, show that eating more salt made them less thirsty but somehow
> hungrier. Subsequent experiments found that mice burned more calories
> when they got more salt, eating 25 percent more just to maintain their
> weight.
