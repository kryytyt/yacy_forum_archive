Dawn Mission Celebrates 10 Years in Space
=========================================

Date: 2017-09-27 21:09:00

[![Dawn launched 10 years ago on Sept. 27,
2007.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20170927/dawn20170927-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2017-249&rn=news.xml&rst=6955)\
\
Ten years ago, NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft set sail for the two most massive
bodies in the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter: giant asteroid
Vesta and dwarf planet Ceres.
