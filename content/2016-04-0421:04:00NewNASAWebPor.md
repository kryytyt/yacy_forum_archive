New NASA Web Portal Shines Beacon on Rising Seas
================================================

Date: 2016-04-04 21:04:00

[![Fort Lauderdale, Florida, is at risk from rising sea

JPL-developed Web portal gives researchers, decision makers and the
public alike a new resource to stay up to date.
