Dawn Operating Normally After Safe Mode Triggered
=================================================

Date: 2014-09-16 21:09:00

[![Artist concept of NASA\'s Dawn spacecraft orbiting Ceres during an
upcoming
flyby.](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/dawn/20140916/xenonceres-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-312&rn=news.xml&rst=4297)\
\
The Dawn spacecraft has resumed normal ion thrusting after the thrusting
unexpectedly stopped and the spacecraft entered safe mode on September
11.
