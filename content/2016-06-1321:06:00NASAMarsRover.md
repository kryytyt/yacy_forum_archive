NASA Mars Rover Descends Plateau, Turns Toward Mountain
=======================================================

Date: 2016-06-13 21:06:00

[![Rover\'s
self-portrait](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/msl/20160613/pia20602-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2016-149&rn=news.xml&rst=6532)\
\
NASA\'s Curiosity Mars rover has analyzed its 12th drilled sample of
Mars. This sample came from mudstone bedrock, which the rover resumed
climbing in late May after six months studying other features.
