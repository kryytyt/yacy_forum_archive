Comet Feature Named After Late NASA Scientist Claudia Alexander
===============================================================

Date: 2015-09-29 21:09:00

[![Comet Scientists Honor
Colleagues](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/images/rosetta/20150929/pia19838-226.jpg){width="100"
height="75"}](http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2015-304&rn=news.xml&rst=4724)\
\
Scientists from the European Space Agency\'s Rosetta mission honor their
deceased colleague, Claudia Alexander of JPL, by naming a feature after
her on the mission\'s target comet.
