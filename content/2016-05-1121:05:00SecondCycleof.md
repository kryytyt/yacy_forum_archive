Second Cycle of Martian Seasons Completing for Curiosity Rover
==============================================================

Date: 2016-05-11 21:05:00

[![NASA\'s Curiosity Mars rover has made measurements of Martian weather

NASA\'s Curiosity Mars rover has completed its second Martian year since
landing in 2012, recording environmental patterns through two full
cycles of Martian seasons.
