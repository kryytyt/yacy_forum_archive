I just found out that Blood Drive was cancelled. This is tragic. That show was fantastic. Pretty much every episode is an homage to a different 70s or 80s splatter movie, with a Death Race framing story around it. They do Big Trouble, they do A Boy and His Dog, one of the MacGuffins is 2-4-5 Trioxin, from Return of the Living Dead. They even went to a town called Cronenburgh -- I died. And the season doesn\’t end on a cliffhanger, so that\’s nice. They had more to tell, but they wrapped up the first story, more or less.

I had a couple of friends say, \“Um, I watched the first episode and that was pretty stupid.\” No. That just means you haven\’t acclimated yet. Have another drink and let it wash over you.
