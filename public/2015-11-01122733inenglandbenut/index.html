In England benutzt die Polizei schonmal ihre Terror-Erlaubnis, ...

Date: 2015-11-01 12:27:33

In England benutzt die Polizei schonmal ihre Terror-Erlaubnis, um einem Reporter den Laptop zu beschlagnahmen und zu untersuchen.

Officers obtained an order from a judge that was served on the BBC and Secunder Kermani, who joined the flagship BBC2 news show early last year and has produced a series of reports on British-born jihadis.
