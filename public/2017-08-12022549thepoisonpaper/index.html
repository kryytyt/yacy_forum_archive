Date: 2017-08-12 02:25:49

The Poison Papers, ein Haufen Dokumente über die Chemieindustrie und ihre Regulierungsbehörden in den USA, zurück bis in die 1920er Jahre.

Taken as a whole, the papers show that both industry and regulators understood the extraordinary toxicity of many chemical products and worked together to conceal this information from the public and the press.
