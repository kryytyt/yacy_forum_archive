Durchsuchung bei einem Hoster. Nur heisst der Hoster Cyberbunker

Date: 2013-03-27 15:11:05

Durchsuchung bei einem Hoster. Nur heisst der Hoster Cyberbunker und hat das Datacenter in einem Atombunker:

Before the break of dawn on a morning in April, a full SWAT team was sent to execute a search warrant on CyberBunker\’s property.

...

Two SWAT officers are seen to hit the blast door of the bunker with a battering ram. It must not have occurred to the officers that the blast doors were designed to withstand a 20 megaton nuclear explosion from close range. When the SWAT team realized that the door was not being opened for them, they throw flashbangs and take other actions to draw attention.

Die Besatzung des Bunkers hat von der ganzen Aktion live nichts mitgekriegt und da die Polizei den Bunker nicht geoeffnet bekommen hat, sind sie wieder abgezogen. Spaeter, nachdem die Videoaufzeichnungen dann ausgewertet waren, hat Cyberbunker bei der Lokalen Polizei angerufen und gefragt, warum ein SWAT Team da war:

In an attempt to learn exactly what happened, CyberBunker\’s general manager Jordan Robson, decided to call the police. Unfortunately, the police department claimed that they were not aware of the raid. They insisted that no SWAT team had been sent to the property.

Einfach zu gut!
