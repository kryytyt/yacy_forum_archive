Date: 2017-11-19 11:14:25

Hier hat jemand mal geguckt, ob das Auswirkungen auf die Sterblichkeitsrate hat, wenn das Blut von einer schwangeren Frau kam.

In this cohort study that included 31 118 patients who received red blood cell transfusions, receipt of a transfusion from an ever-pregnant female donor was associated with a statistically significant increase in all-cause mortality among male recipients of red blood cell transfusions (hazard ratio, 1.13) but not among female recipients (hazard ratio, 0.99).
