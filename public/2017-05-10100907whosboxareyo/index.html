Fyodor Pavlov-Andreevich was dropped off nude and wedged inside a clear fiberglass box outside the Metropolitan Museum of Art.

His two handlers left the lewd display in a restricted area for the star-studded celebration, prompting the Met\’s security team to quickly cover it with a white sheet and drag it out.

Cops descended on the bizarre stunt and demanded the lanky 6-foot-2 performer step out of the box. Pavlov allegedly refused and firefighters were forced to cut open the see-through prop, said Sgt. Brendan Ryan.

A witness said the box was \“relatively small\” for a man of Pavlov\’s size.
